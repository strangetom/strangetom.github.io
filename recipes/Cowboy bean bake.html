Cowboy bean bake

  • Pork

  • 55 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin mixed pulses
  • 225 g sausages
  • 225 g bacon
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp mustard

Instructions

  1. Chop onion, slice bacon and sausages.
  2. Tip beans into colander, rinse and drain.
  3. Cook onion, bacon and sausages together in oil until onion is soft.
  4. Add tomatoes, sugar, mustard and season.
  5. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins.
  6. Add beans and cook until beans are hot.
