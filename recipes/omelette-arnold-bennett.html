Omelette Arnold Bennett

Serves 2     |     Prep 2 mins     |     Cook 14 mins

Contriving to be creamy and fluffy at the same time, this smoked haddock souffle omelette is delicious. No wonder the author Arnold Bennett loved it so much.

Source: The Half Hour Cook

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 175 g smoked haddock fillet
  • 50 g butter, diced
  • 175 ml double cream
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 40 g mature cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper
  • watercress, to garnish
