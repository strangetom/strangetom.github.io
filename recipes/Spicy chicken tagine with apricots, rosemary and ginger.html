Spicy chicken tagine with apricots, rosemary and ginger

This tagine is both fruity and spicy, and the rosemary and ginger give it a delightful aroma.

1 hr 15 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil, with a knob of butter
  • onion, finely chopped
  • 3 sprigs rosemary, 1 finely chopped, the other 2 cut in half
  • 1 large thumb sized piece fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 red chillies, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1-2 cinnamon sticks
  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 175 g ready to eat dried apricots
  • 2 tbsp clear honey
  • 1x 400 g can plum tomatoes with their juice
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • small bunch of fresh green or purple basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil and butter in a tagine or heavy-based casserole. Stir in the

    onion

    rosemary3 sprigs
    sprigs and the
    cinnamon sticks1-2
    .
  2. Add the
    chicken thighs8
    and brown them on both sides. Toss in the
    apricots175 g
    with the honey, then stir in the
    plum tomatoes with their juice1x 400 g can
    . (Add a little water if necessary, to ensure there is enough liquid to cover the base of the tagine and submerge the
    apricots175 g
    .)
  3. Bring the liquid to the boil, then reduce the heat. Cover with a lid and cook gently for 35 - 40 minutes.
  4. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Shred the large basil leaves and leave the small ones intact. Sprinkle them over the chicken and serve the dish immediately.
