Vegetarian casserole

A Mediterranean one-pot stew with peppers, courgettes, lentils, sweet smoked paprika and thyme

50 mins
4 servings

Vegan

  • kcal 216
  • fat 5.1 g
  • saturates 0.7 g
  • carbs 31 g
  • sugar 16.1 g
  • fibre 9.8 g
  • protein 12.3 g
  • salt 1.6 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 clove garlic, sliced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 3 medium carrot, sliced (about 200 g)
  • 2 medium sticks celery, finely sliced (about 120 g)
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow pepper, chopped
  • 2x 400 g tins tomatoes or peeled cherry tomatoes
  • 250 ml vegetable stock
  • 2 courgettes, sliced thickly (about 300 g)
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 250 g cooked lentil or 125 g dried lentils

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large, heavy-based pan. Add the onions and cook gently for 5-10 mins until softened.
  2. Add the
    garlic3 clove
    , spices, dried thyme, carrots,
    celery2 medium sticks
    and peppers and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the
    tomatoes2x 400 g tins
    ,
    stock250 ml
    ,
    courgettes2
    and
    fresh thyme2 sprigs
    and cook for 20 - 25 minutes.
  4. Take out the thyme sprigs. Stir in the
    lentils250 g
    and bring back to a simmer. Serve with wild and white basmati rice, mash or quinoa.
