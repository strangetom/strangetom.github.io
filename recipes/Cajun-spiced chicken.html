Chicken

20 mins

4 servings

Cajun-spiced chicken


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, about 140 g each
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • tzatziki, mixed salad and new potatoes, to serve

Instructions

  1. Mix together the flour, Cajun seasoning and salt.
  2. Rub both sides of the chicken breasts with a tbsp of the oil. Dust each side with the seasoned flour. Heat the remaining oil in a fry pan.
  3. Fry the coated chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side until cooked golden. Serve with the tzatziki, mixed salad and new potatoes.
