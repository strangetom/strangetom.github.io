Springtime lamb stew

1 hr 10 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 312
  • fat 12 g
  • saturates 4 g
  • carbs 24 g
  • sugar 8 g
  • fibre 6 g
  • protein 21 g
  • salt 0.58 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 12 shallots, peeled
  • 350 g trimmed diced lamb, from the chump or loin fillet
  • 350 g new potatoes, scrubbed and cut into chunks
  • 12 baby carrots, trimmed and peeled
  • 150 ml white wine
  • 250 ml vegetable stock
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 200 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g frozen peas
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley leaves
  • crusty bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tsp
    in a large pan and add the
    shallots12
    and
    lamb350 g
    . Fry over a medium heat until they are starting the brown, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Add the
    potatoes350 g
    ,
    carrots12
    ,
    white wine150 ml
    ,
    stock250 ml
    ,
    bay leaves3
    and
    tomatoes200 g can
    to the pan. Season and bring to the boil. Cover the pan and leave the stew to simmer gently over a medium heat for 25-30 minutes until the vegetables and
    lamb350 g
    are tender.
  3. Stir in the
    peas100 g
    and cook for another 2-3 minutes until tender. Scatter in the
    parsley1 tbsp
    , adjust the seasoning and serve with crusty bread.
