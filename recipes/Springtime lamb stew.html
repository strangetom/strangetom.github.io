Lamb

70 mins

4 servings

Springtime lamb stew


Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 12 shallots, peeled
  • 350 g trimmed diced lamb, from the chump or loin fillet
  • 350 g new potatoes, scrubbed and cut into chunks
  • 12 baby carrots, trimmed and peeled
  • 150 ml white wine
  • 250 ml vegetable stock
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 200 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100 g frozen peas
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley leaves
  • crusty bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and add the shallots and lamb. Fry over a medium heat until they are starting the brown, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Add the potatoes, carrots, white wine, stock, bay leaves and tomatoes to the pan. Season and bring to the boil. Cover the pan and leave the stew to simmer gently over a medium heat for 25-30 minutes until the vegetables and lamb are tender.
  3. Stir in the peas and cook for another 2-3 minutes until tender. Scatter in the parsley, adjust the seasoning and serve with crusty bread.
