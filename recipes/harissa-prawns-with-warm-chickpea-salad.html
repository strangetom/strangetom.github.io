Harissa prawns with warm chickpea salad

12 mins Serves 2

This makes a very speedy supper, packed with good things. You could add some halved cherry tomatoes to the salad, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1x 400 g can chickpeas, drained
  • 50 g shredded curly kale
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • juice 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • 1x 150 g pack raw shelled prawns
  • 2 tsp harissa

Instructions

  1. Heat a large non-stick frying pan and add the oil. Tip in the onion and fry over a high heat for 1-2 minutes. Add the chickpeas and kale and warm through. Mix the tahini with the lemon juice and parsley, then stir into the pan. Tip onto a serving platter.
  2. Return the pan to the heat. Mix the prawns with the harissa and fry for 1-2 minutes until the prawns are cooked through. Tip the prawns over the salad and serve.
