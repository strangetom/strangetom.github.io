Beef chow mein

  • Beef

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Dairy Free

Our easy chow mein is the perfect 'fake-away'.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry (or Shaoxing cooking wine, or apple juice)
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 300 g minced beef (12% fat)
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 2.5cm piece root ginger, peeled and shredded
  • 1x 600 g pack sweet & crunchy stir fry veg or other stir fry pack
  • 1x 410 g pack fresh egg noodles

Instructions

  1. Mix the soy sauce, sherry, oyster sauce, caster sugar, sesame oil and cornflour together in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large, deep, nonstick frying pan or wok over a high heat. Add the minced beef, garlic and ginger and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes, breaking up the meat into small pieces as it cooks, until it starts to crisp up. Add the veg and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, then add the noodles and stir-fry for another minute.
  3. Add the sauce mixture and toss everything together over a high heat for 1 minute or until piping hot. Spoon the chow mein into warmed bowls and serve.
