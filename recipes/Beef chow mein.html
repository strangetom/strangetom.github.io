Beef chow mein

Our easy chow mein is the perfect 'fake-away'.

20 mins
4 servings

Dairy Free

  • kcal 434
  • fat 19 g
  • saturates 4 g
  • carbs 39 g
  • sugar 7 g
  • fibre 6 g
  • protein 22 g
  • salt 2.5 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry (or Shaoxing cooking wine or apple juice)
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 300 g minced beef (12% fat)
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely sliced
  • 2½ cm piece root ginger, peeled and shredded
  • 1x 600 g pack sweet & crunchy stir fry veg or other stir fry pack
  • 1x 410 g pack fresh egg noodles

Instructions

  1. Mix the
    soy sauce2 tbsp
    ,
    sherry2 tbsp
    ,
    oyster sauce3 tbsp
    ,
    caster sugar1 tsp
    ,
    sesame oil1 tsp
    and
    cornflour2 tsp
    together in a small bowl and set aside.
  2. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a large, deep, nonstick frying pan or wok over a high heat. Add the
    minced beef300 g
    ,
    garlic2 cloves
    and
    ginger2½ cm piece
    and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes, breaking up the meat into small pieces as it cooks, until it starts to crisp up. Add the
    veg1x 600 g pack
    and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, then add the
    noodles1x 410 g pack
    and stir-fry for another minute.
  3. Add the sauce mixture and toss everything together over a high heat for 1 minute or until piping hot. Spoon the chow mein into warmed bowls and serve.
