Italian cod and bacon stew

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

Italian fish stew with a hint of spice. Find some good crusty bread to serve with this dish. Gluten-free: make sure the fish stock cubes are gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 4 slices streaky bacon, chopped
  • 6 spring onions, chopped
  • 6 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 400 g cannellini beans, drained and washed
  • 1 mug of water + 1 fish stock cube
  • 4 cod steaks, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • Crusty bread to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok. Add the pepper and bacon and fry until brown. Season well with salt and pepper.
  2. Add the spring onions, tomatoes and garlic and fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add the paprika, saffron, beans, water and fish stock and bring to the boil. Add the cod and simmer gently for 3-4 minutes,or until the cod is cooked.
  4. Stir in the coriander and serve with crusty bread.
