Italian cod and bacon stew

20 mins Serves 4 Gluten Free

Italian fish stew with a hint of spice. Find some good crusty bread to serve with this dish. Gluten-free: make sure the fish stock cubes are GlutenFree.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • 4 slices streaky bacon, chopped
  • 6 spring onions, chopped
  • 6 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch saffron
  • 400 g cannellini beans, drained and washed
  • 1 mug of water + 1 fish stock cube
  • 4 cod steaks, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • Crusty bread , to serve

    oil1 tbsp
    in a large pan or wok. Add the
    pepper1
    and
    bacon4
    and fry until brown. Season well with salt and
    pepper1
    .
  2. Add the
    spring onions6
    ,
    tomatoes6
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    and fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add the
    paprika2 tsp
    ,
    saffron1 pinch
    ,
    beans400 g
    ,
    water1 mug
    and fish stock and bring to the boil. Add the
    cod4
    and simmer gently for 3-4 minutes,or until the
    cod4
    is cooked.
  4. Stir in the
    coriander2 tbsp
    and serve with crusty bread.
