Prawn & spinach risotto

Serves 3     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Delightfully light, but tasty dish. Really quick and simple to make. The spinach is hardly cooked at all, so keeps all its glorious goodness.

Source: Nosh: Quick & Easy

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 mug risotto rice
  • 2 mugs water
  • 2 fish stock cubes
  • 450 g king prawns
  • 200 g spinach, roughly chopped
  • ½ mug finely grated parmesan
