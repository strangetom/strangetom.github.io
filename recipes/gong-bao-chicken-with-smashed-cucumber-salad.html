Gong bao chicken with smashed cucumber salad

Serves 4     |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 25 mins

Gong bao chicken is a dish of succulent chicken stir-fried with peanuts and spring onions. The kick of chilli, tingle of Sichuan pepper and gentle sweet-sour sauce form a typical Sichuanese combination of flavours and this delicious dish needs little more accompaniment than some plain rice and a simple smashed cucumber salad.

Source: The Spicery

Ingredients

  • 500 g diced chicken (thighs are best)
  • 1 bunch spring onions, cut into 2 cm lengths (including the green bits)
  • 1 cucumber
  • 3 tbsps soy sauce (any type)
  • 3 tbsps vinegar (rice, white wine or cider vinegar is best)
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsps sugar
  • 75 g peanuts (roasted and salted is fine)
  • Rice, to serve
Sichuan pepper blend
  • ½ tsp ground toasted Sichuan pepper
Chicken marinade
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ tsp ground toasted Sichuan pepper
Cucumber spices
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • pinch of chilli flakes
