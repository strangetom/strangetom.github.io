Bean, pepper and chorizo stew

Serves 4    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

A Spanish inspired stew that will warm you to your core. Perfect for when the winter evenings start to draw in.

Source: Mob Kitchen

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1 large ring chorizo
  • 2 red peppers
  • 2 yellow peppers
  • 2 orange peppers
  • 2 brown onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 red chilli
  • fresh coriander
  • paprika
  • 1x 400 g tin chickpeas - don't drain!
  • 2x 400 g tin butter beans - don't drain!
  • olive oil
