Cajun chicken salad with bacon and potatoes

35 mins Serves 4 Cajun

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • oil
  • 5 rashers streaky bacon
  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 1x 100 g bag green salad
  • 1 avacado, peeled and cut into chunks
  • salt and pepper
Dressing
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • juice of lime
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/gas 7.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut into 1" dice. Place on a baking tray and pour over oil. Season with salt and distribute the oil evenly using your hands. Place in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until the potatoes are brown.
  3. While the potatoes are cooking, combine the ingredients for the dressing in a bowl and set aside.
  4. Heat a little oil in a frying pan and fry the bacon until it is crispy. Once cooled, cut into pieces and set aside.
  5. Pour the Cajun seasoning into a shallow dish. Place the chicken breasts in the dish and coat evenly with the seasoning.
  6. Pan roast the chicken breasts, then leave to cool a little before cutting into thin strips.
  7. Mix the salad in a large bowl with the bacon, avacado and the spring onions.
  8. When the potatoes are cooked, drain any excess oil and mix with the salad.
  9. Arrange the salad on individual plates. Arrange the chicken on top of the salad. Drizzle the dressing over the top of each plate.
