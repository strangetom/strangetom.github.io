Chicken with apples and cider

This simple recipe uses only 6 ingredients. It also works well with pork steaks.

Servings: 4

Total: 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 eating apples, peeled cored and cut into 8 wedges
  • 300 ml dry cider
  • 150 ml chicken stock
  • rice or mashed potato, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1-2 tbsp
    in a large frying pan with a lide and fry the
    chicken breasts4
    for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside. Lower the temperature slightly and add the
    onion1
    . Fry, stirring, for 2-3 minutes until tinged brown. Add the
    apple2
    and cook over a high heat for 5 minutes until golden.
  2. Still over a high heat, pour in the
    cider300 ml
    and bubble for 2 minutes to reduce slight. Add the
    stock150 ml
    , stirring to scrape the bits from the bottom of the pan. Lower the heat
  3. Return the chicken to the pan, cover and simmer for 5 minutes until it is almost cooked. Remove the lid and simmer for 3-4 minutes to thicken the sauce a little. Serve with rice or mashed potato.
