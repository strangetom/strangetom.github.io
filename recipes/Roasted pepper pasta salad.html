Roasted pepper pasta salad

40 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 whole roasted red bell peppers, homemade or jarred see note above on how to make at home
  • 1 cup toasted walnuts, finely chopped and divided
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, divided
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • kosher salt
  • ½ cup plus 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 lb trottole pasta or medium shells, fusilli, rotini, cavatappi
  • 1½ cups fresh breadcrumbs
  • 1 lb ripe red heirloom tomatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 oz finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup basil leaves, torn if large
