Amatriciana chicken traybake

Inspired by the classic Italian pasta sauce, chicken thighs and new potatoes are flavoured with bacon and tomato in this easy one-pan meal.

1 hr 15 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

  • kcal 472
  • fat 20 g
  • saturates 5 g
  • carbs 27 g
  • sugar 6 g
  • fibre 4 g
  • protein 46 g
  • salt 1.5 g

Ingredients

  • 1 long red chilli
  • 3 tbsp tomato puree
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 8 skinless chicken thighs
  • 500 g new potatoes
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 140 g cubetti di pancetta (or smoked bacon lardons)
  • 400 g tomatoes, half cherry or baby plum, any larger ones halved
  • green salad and bread, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 200°C/180°C fan/Gas 6. Find a large roasting tin that will hold the
    chicken8
    thighs and
    potatoes500 g
    in a single layer.
  2. Halve the
    chilli1
    , scrape out and discard the seeds if you don't like it too hot, and remove the stalk. Put in a small food processor or mini chopper with the
    tomato puree3 tbsp
    ,
    olive oil3 tbsp
    and
    garlic3 cloves
    . Whizz to a paste, then spread over the
    chicken8
    .
  3. Add the
    chicken8
    and
    potatoes500 g
    to the tin with a good grinding of black pepper and some salt, then mix everything together well with your hands. Add the
    thyme4 sprigs
    and roast for 30 mins.
  4. Stir in the
    pancetta140 g
    and roast for 15 mins more, then add the
    tomatoes400 g
    and roast for another 15 mins until the
    tomatoes400 g
    have softened and the
    chicken8
    is cooked.
  5. Serve straight from the pan and eat with a green salad and some bread, if you like, for mopping up the juices.
