One-pot chicken & mushroom risotto

Risotto is the ultimate one-pot winter supper – the perfect vehicle for letting simple, comforting ingredients shine. Scatter on cheese and parsley to serve.

Servings: 4

Total: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 60 g butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 sprigs thyme, leaves picked
  • 250 g pack chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 300 g risotto rice
  • 1½ l hot chicken stock
  • 200 g cooked chicken, chopped into chunks
  • 50 g grated parmesan, plus extra to serve (optional)
  • small pack parsley, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    butter60 g
    in a large pan over a gentle heat and add the
    onion1 large
    . Cook for 10 mins until softened, then stir in the
    thyme2 sprigs
    leaves and
    mushrooms250 g
    . Cook for 5 mins, sprinkle in the
    rice300 g
    and stir to coat in the mixture.
  2. Ladle in a quarter of the
    stock1½ l
    and continue cooking, stirring occasionally and topping up with more
    stock1½ l
    as it absorbs (you may not need all the
    stock1½ l
    ).
  3. When most of the
    stock1½ l
    has been absorbed and the
    rice300 g
    is nearly cooked, add the
    chicken200 g
    and stir to warm through. Season well and stir in the
    parmesan50 g
    and
    parsleysmall pack
    . Serve scattered with extra
    parmesan50 g
    , if you like.
