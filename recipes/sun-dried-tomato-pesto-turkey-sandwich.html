Sun-dried tomato pesto turkey sandwich

Serves 6    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

A delicious dinner-style sandwich...great for playoff games and the upcoming Super Bowl!

Source: www.halfbakedharvest.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
Dressing
  • ½ cups tahini (or use mayo)
  • ¼ cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, grated
  • salt and black pepper
  • ½ cups parmesan, grated
Sandwich
  • 1 loaf Ciabatta bread, halved lengthwise
  • ½ cups basil pesto
  • ½ cups oil packed sun-dried tomatoes, sliced
  • ½ cups pepperoncini, sliced
  • 6-12 thin slices deli-style turkey
  • 6 slices gouda or havarti cheese
  • 6 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh broccoli sprouts / microgreens
