Pull-apart meatball sliders

Serves 6    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 35 mins

Make these moreish meatball sliders with tomato sauce and melted cheese for a party or buffet. They're easy to make and the whole family will enjoy them

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 12 beef meatballs
  • 12 small bread rolls (check the bakery section of the supermarket for a batch of rolls that are baked together, so they're still attached to 1 another)
  • 100 g tomato pasta sauce
  • 100 g grated mozzarella
  • 50 g butter, melted
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 20 g grated parmesan
