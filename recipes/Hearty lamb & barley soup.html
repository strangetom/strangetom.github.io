Hearty lamb & barley soup

This Lancashire hotpot in soup form is nourishing and filling, but low in fat.

Servings: 4

Total: 35 mins

Dairy Free

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 200 g lamb neck fillet, trimmed of fat and cut into small pieces
  • ½ large onion, finely chopped
  • 50 g pearl barley
  • 600 g mixed root vegetables
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 litre lamb or beef stock
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 100 g green beans, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tsp
    in a large saucepan. Season the
    lamb200 g
    , then fry for a few mins until browned. Add the
    onion½ large
    and
    barley50 g
    , then gently fry for 1 min. Add the
    veg600 g
    , cook for 2 more mins, then add the
    Worcestershire sauce2 tsp
    ,
    stock1 litre
    and
    thyme1 sprig
    . Cover, then simmer for 20 mins.
  2. When everything is cooked, spoon about a quarter of the soup into a separate pan. Purée with a stick blender (or put into a normal blender and whizz), then stir it back into the rest of the soup. Add the
    green beans100 g
    , simmer for 3 mins, then ladle the soup into bowls and serve with granary bread.
