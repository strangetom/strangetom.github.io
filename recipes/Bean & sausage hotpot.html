Bean & sausage hotpot

  • Pork

  • 45 mins

  • 4 servings

Warm up after work with this ultimate comfort food dish, just add a buttery dollop of mash.

Ingredients

  • 8 large sausages (course-cut ones like Toulouse or Cumberland are ideal)
  • 3 x 400 g cans butter beans
  • 1 tbsp black treacle or muscovado sugar
  • 1 tsp English mustard
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onions, finely chopped
  • 1 carrots, finely chopped
  • 1 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and add the sauce vegetables and bay leaf. Stir in the sugar with some salt. Gently cook for 10-15 mins until the veg is tender. Stir in the tomatoes and 150ml water. Simmer very gently for 30 mins until the sauce has reduced by two-thirds and is very thick.
  2. In a large casserole, fry the sausages until brown all over â€“ about 10 mins.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, stirring well, then stir in the beans, treacle or sugar and mustard. Bring to the simmer, cover and cook for 30 mins. Great served with crusty bread or rice.
