Honey-mustard sausage, bacon and sweet potato bake

  • Pork

  • 45 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp clear honey
  • 4 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 8 British pork sausages
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon, halved lengthwise
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  • 2 red onions, sliced
  • 750 g sweet potato, sliced into wedges
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • fresh parsley, to serve (optional)
  • salad, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 6. In a bowl, mix the honey and mustard with 1 tbsp hot water.
  2. Wrap each sausage with a strip of bacon. Put them in a large roasting dish with the peppers, onions and sweet potato wedges, then pour over the honey mixture. Season and drizzle with the oil.
  3. Roast for 30-35 minutes until the sausages are sticky and cooked through. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with a salad.
