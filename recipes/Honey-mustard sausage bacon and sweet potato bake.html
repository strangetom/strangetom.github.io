Honey-mustard sausage, bacon and sweet potato bake

Servings: 4

Total: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp clear honey
  • 4 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 8 British pork sausages
  • 4 rashers smoked streaky bacon, halved lengthwise
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced
  • 2 red onions, sliced
  • 750 g sweet potato, sliced into wedges
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • fresh parsley, to serve (optional)
  • salad, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 6. In a bowl, mix the
    honey2 tbsp
    and
    mustard4 tbsp
    with 1 tbsp hot water.
  2. Wrap each
    sausage8
    with a strip of
    bacon4 rashers
    . Put them in a large roasting dish with the peppers,
    onions2
    and
    sweet potato750 g
    wedges, then pour over the
    honey2 tbsp
    mixture. Season and drizzle with the
    oil2 tsp
    .
  3. Roast for 30-35 minutes until the sausages are sticky and cooked through. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with a salad.
