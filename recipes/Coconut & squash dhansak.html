Coconut & squash dhansak

20 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

This quick and easy vegetarian curry is perfect for a healthy weeknight dinner - with butternut squash, coconut milk, lentils and spinach.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 500 g butternut squash (about 1 small squash), peeled and chopped into bite-sized chunks
  • 100 g frozen chopped onions
  • 4 heaped tbsp mild curry paste
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400 g can light coconut milk
  • mini naan bread, to serve
  • 400 g can lentils, drained
  • 200 g bag baby spinach
  • 150 ml coconut yogurt

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large pan. Put the
    squash500 g
    in a bowl with a splash of water. Cover with cling film and microwave on High for 10 mins or until tender. Meanwhile, add the
    onions100 g
    to the hot
    oil1 tbsp
    and cook for a few mins until soft. Add the
    curry paste4 heaped tbsp
    ,
    tomatoes400 g can
    and
    coconut milk400 g can
    , and simmer for 10 mins until thickened to a rich sauce.
  2. Warm the naan breads in a low oven or in the toaster. Drain any liquid from the
    squash500 g
    , then add to the sauce with the
    lentils400 g can
    ,
    spinach200 g
    and some seasoning. Simmer for a further 2-3 mins to wilt the
    spinach200 g
    , then stir in the
    coconut yogurt150 ml
    . Serve with the warm naan and a dollop of extra yogurt.
