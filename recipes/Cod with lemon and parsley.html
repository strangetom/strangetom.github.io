Cod with lemon and parsley

20 mins Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cod fillets, about 175 g each
  • 2 tbsp plain flour, seasoned
  • 25 g butter
  • juice 1 lemon
  • 1 heaped tbsp chopped parsley leaves
  • new potatoes and greens, to serve

Instructions

  1. Coat the
    cod fillets2
    with the
    flour2 tbsp
    , dusting off any excess.
  2. Heat half the
    butter25 g
    in a frying pan, when it is bubbling add the fish skin side down and cook over a fairly high heat for 4-5 minutes. Using a fish slice, turn the fillets carefully and brown the other side. When the fish is just cooked, add the remaining
    butter25 g
    to the pan. When it is bubbling, stir in the lemon juice and season.
  3. Bubble the sauce up until it is slightly thickened, then stir in the
    parsley leaves1 heaped tbsp
    . Serve with new potatoes and greens.
