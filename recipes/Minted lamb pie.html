Minted lamb pie

Servings: 4

Total: 1 hr 5 mins

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 4 medium carrots, diced
  • 500 g minced lamb
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 mugs water
  • 1 lamb stock cube
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped mint
Shortcrust pastry
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 175 g butter, measure using packet
  • ⅓ mug cold water
  • 1 egg
  • 360 g plain flour
  • green veg
  • 6 medium potatoes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven 180°C/Gas 6.
  2. Heat the
    oil1 tablespoon
    in a frying pan or large saucepan. Add the
    onion1
    and
    carrots4 medium
    and fry until the
    onion1
    begins to soften.
  3. Add the
    mince500 g
    and fry until no longer pink. Add the
    flour2 tbsp
    and mix well. Add the
    water2 mugs
    and
    stock1
    , season and bring to the boil. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, put the
    plain flour360 g
    ,
    xanthan gum1 teaspoon
    ,
    salt½ teaspoon
    and
    butter175 g
    in a processor. Whizz until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Add the
    egg1
    and
    cold water⅓ mug
    and pulse the processor until a soft dough is formed. Turn out onto a floured surface and roll out until it is just under 1 cm thick.
  5. Add the
    mint2 tbsp
    to the meat and pour into a casserole dish. Wet the top edges of the dish with
    water2 mugs
    . Carefully lift the pastry onto the rolling pin and then spread over the meat. Trim the edges and crimp them. Brush the top with beaten
    egg1
    . Make a hole in the centre to let the steam out.
  6. Place in the oven for 35-40 minutes. Serve with broccoli and minted pea mash.
