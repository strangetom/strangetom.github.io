Linguine with ham and mascarpone

Mascarpone cheese masquerades as cream in this simple yet delicious recipe. Its thick, unctuous consistency makes it perfect for sauce which cling beautifully to the pasta.

Servings: 6

Total: 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 25 g butter
  • 150 g mascarpone
  • 90 g cooked ham, cut into thin strips
  • 30 ml milk
  • 3 tbsp freshly grated Parmesan cheese, plus extra to serve
  • 500 g fresh linguine
  • salt and ground black pepper

    pasta500 g

    pasta500 g

    Parmesan3 tbsp
    and toss well.
  4. Taste for seasoning and serve the
    pasta500 g
    immediately, with more ground black pepper and extra grated
    Parmesan3 tbsp
