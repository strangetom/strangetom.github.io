Whole eggs in coconut masala

This is a nice, simple vegetarian egg curry with a delicate coconut masala. The whole eggs are fried first then finished off in the curry.

60 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free
Dairy Free

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 6 free-range eggs, hard-boiled, peeled and left whole
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 400 ml oz coconut milk
  • 2 medium red onions, very thinly sliced
  • 4 cm fresh root ginger, finely shredded
  • 3 fresh green chillies, thinly sliced, with seeds
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • handful of coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • boiled basmati rice

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a heavy-based saucepan or karahi over a medium heat, add the whole eggs and fry for 1-2 minutes, or until lightly coloured, then add the turmeric and chilli powder and cook for another 30 seconds. Stir in the coconut milk and bring to a simmer.
  2. Add the onions, ginger, chillies and salt, and simmer for five minutes, or until the coconut milk has reduced in volume by half and the onions are just softened, adding a splash of water if it becomes too thick. Stir in the
    sugar1 tsp
    and coriander and sprinkle with garam masala. Halve the eggs, and serve with rice.
