Baked sweet potato with easy black bean chilli

1 hr 35 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian Mexican

Step-by-step method for making baked sweet potato with easy black bean chilli yourself.

Ingredients

  • 4x baking sweet potatoes, around 275 g-300 g each
  • fine sea salt
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1x 30 g pack taco seasoning mix
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2x 380 g carton black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 15 g Belgian dark cooking chocolate, chopped
To serve
  • 4 generous tbsp soured cream
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • ½ red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • lime zest, to serve

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 7. Scrub the sweet potatoes, prick with a fork and sprinkle with a little fine sea salt, which will stick to the damp skins. Bake directly on the oven shelf, but put a foil-lined baking tray on the shelf below to catch any sugary drips. Bake for 1 hour or until soft. Split open (or halve), and top with one of the fillings below.
  2. In a large saucepan, soften the onion in the oil for 8-10 minutes then add the taco seasoning mix and the tomato purée. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute then add the black beans, the chocolate and 200 ml water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Divide the black bean chilli between the halved or split sweet potatoes. Add a dollop of soured cream, sliced avocado, chopped coriander, red chilli and lime zest, to serve.
