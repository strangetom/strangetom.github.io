Pad thai omelette

35 mins
2 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 small courgette, cut into matchsticks
  • 5 g chunk of fresh root ginger, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 100 g shelled raw tiger prawns, roughly chopped
  • 100 g bean sprouts
  • oil, for spraying
  • 4 eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp tamarind paste
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 0.25 tsp shrimp paste (optional)
  • 2 spring onions, shredded
  • handful coriander, chopped
  • 1 tsp finely chopped peanuts

Instructions

  1. To make the sauce, mix the
    tamarind paste1 tsp
    ,
    fish sauce1 tbsp
    ,
    caster sugar1 tsp
    and
    shrimp paste0.25 tsp
    , if using, together. Taste a little and adjust the quantities of any of the ingredients to taste.
  2. To make the pad thai, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok until smoking, then add the
    shallot1
    ,
    carrot1
    ,
    courgette1 small
    ,
    ginger5 g
    and
    chilli1
    . Stir-fry for about 3 minutes until the vegetables are starting to cook through, then add the
    garlic2 cloves
    . Stir-fry for another minute, then push everything to one side of the wok and add the
    prawns100 g
    . Cook for another minute and then pour over the sauce. Let it bubble for a few minutes and stir in the
    bean sprouts100 g
    . Set it aside and keep warm.
  3. Lightly spray a large non-stick frying pan with
    oil1 tbsp
    and place it over a medium heat until hot. Break 2
    eggs4
    into a bowl and whisk, then season with salt and pepper. Pour the
    eggs4
    into the frying pan, swirling it around so the whole base is covered. Cook for a couple of minutes until the
    eggs4
    are just cooked through, then turn down the heat.
  4. Put half of the pad thai mixture in a line slightly to the side of the centre of the omelette. Sprinkle over half the
    spring onions2
    ,
    corianderhandful
    and
    peanuts1 tsp
    . Fold one side over the filling, then folder the other side. Now roll the omelette over and out of the frying pan straight on to a plate. Repeat with the other 2
    eggs4
    and the rest of the filling to make the second omelette and serve at once.
