Meat stuffed potato pancake

Serves 6    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

This meat stuffed potato pancake is full of excellent flavors. Chicken, peas, corn, mushrooms, and red bell pepper – a delicious combination. The perfect idea for a weekend lunch.

Source: www.cookhomey.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
Chicken filling
  • 500 g chicken breast or thighs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 300 g mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 150 g corn
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 150 g peas (optional)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • salt and black pepper to taste
Potato pancakes
  • 6 large potatoes, approx. 700 g
  • 1 egg + 1 yolk
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3-5 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 tsp marjoram
  • salt and pepper to taste
