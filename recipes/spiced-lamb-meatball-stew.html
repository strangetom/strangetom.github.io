Spiced lamb meatball stew

Serves 4     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

Enjoy spiced lamb meatballs with chickpeas and pomegranate seeds in a harissa sauce for an easy midweek meal. Freeze any leftovers for busy weekdays

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /

    /    
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 12 lamb meatballs
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 large cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp rose harrisa
  • 1x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 200 ml low-salt stock (lamb or beef)
  • 2x 400 g can chickpeas
  • small bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • ½ small bunch mint, chopped
  • 50 g pomegranate seeds
