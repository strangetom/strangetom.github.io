Teriyaki salmon with asian noodle salad

Serves 4-6     |     Prep 30 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

Easy Teriyaki Salmon served on a bed of Asian Noodles made with brown rice noodles, fresh veggies and a homemade Asian Sesame Dressing! A light, simple refreshing recipe for the summer!

Source: www.joyfulhealthyeats.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
Teriyaki Salmon
  • 2 tbsp rape seed oil
  • 4x 4 oz filets salmon
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp tamari sauce or soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp garlic-chilli paste
Asian Noodle Salad
  • 8 oz whole what rice noodles
  • 1 yellow pepper, julienned
  • 1 red pepper, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas, sliced
  • 1 cup cucumber, julienned
  • ½ cup green onions, sliced
Sesame Dressing
  • 4½ tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2½ tsp honey
  • 1 tsp fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ tsp fresh ginger, minced
