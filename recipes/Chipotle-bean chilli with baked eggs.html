Chipotle-bean chilli with baked eggs

Servings: 4

Total: 35 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp chipotle paste
  • 2x 400 g cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 400 g can mixed beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes with garlic and herbs
  • 1 heaped tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • small handful coriander leaves
  • soured cream and warm flour tortillas, to serve
