Nduja pesto pasta

Serves 4 Vegetarian Share

One of the easiest, quickest dishes we’ve ever done. Make sure to keep stirring the cheese in until it has completely melted.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp of Sacla' 'Nduja pesto
  • Bunch of basil
  • Cheddar
  • Parmesan
  • 500 g fusilli
  • Oregano
  • Red wine vinegar
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 clove garlic
  • pack of sweet peppers
