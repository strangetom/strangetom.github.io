Florentine chicken bake

Florentine dishes tend to have lots of spinach in them and are often topped with mozzarella, so this bake fits the brief nicely. Gluten-free: use GlutenFree pasta and stock cube and GlutenFree plain flour in the sauce.

25 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 3 mugs pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 mug water
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 2 tbsp sun-dried tomato puree
  • 100 g cream cheese
  • 250 g spinach, roughly chopped
  • 250 g mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 mug grated Parmesan

    pasta3 mugs

    oil1 tbsp

    chicken breasts3
    and fry until no longer pink.
  3. Add the
    flour2 tbsp
    and mix. Add the
    water1 mug
    ,
    stock1
    cube and
    tomato puree2 tbsp
    and bring to the boil, stirring frequently. Add the
    cream cheese100 g
    ad stir on a low heat until it melts.
  4. Add the
    spinach250 g
    and cook until it wilts. Add the cooked
    pasta3 mugs
    and mix together.
  5. Pour into a casserole dish. Arrange the
    mozzarella250 g
    over the top and then sprinkle over the
    Parmesan1/2 mug
    . Place under the grill for about 5 minutes, or until the cheese bubbles and browns.
