Thai pork curry

20 mins Serves 4 Gluten Free

Gluten-free: make sure the curry sauce and fish sauce are gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 3 pork steaks, cut into strips
  • 6 spring onions
  • 1 red pepper, cut into 2 cm chunks
  • 125 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 400 ml thick and creamy coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 can bamboo shoots, drained
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • 2x 300 g packs fresh rice noodles

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok or large frying pan. Add the pork and fry on a high heat for 2-3 minutes, or until it beings to brown. Remove from the pan. Set to one side until needed.
  2. Add the onions, peppers and
    mushrooms125 g
    to the pan and fry for 2-3 minutes, or until the peppers begin to brown.
  3. Add the
    coconut milk400 ml
    ,
    curry paste2 tbsp
    and
    fish sauce1 tbsp
    to the pan and bring to the boil.
  4. Add the
    bamboo shoots1 can
    ,
    sugar1 tsp
    and
    coriander2 tbsp
    to the pan, along with the pork and simmer for 1 minute.
  5. Put the rice noodles in a bowl and poor boiling water over them. Leave to stand for 1 minute, then drain. Serve with the curry.
