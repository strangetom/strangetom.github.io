Sweet and sour chicken noodles

25 mins
2 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 spring onions
  • ½ red pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 0.5x 400 g pack of fresh or ready-to-wok egg noodles
  • oil to fry
  • Sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 3 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 6-7 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok, add the
    onions4
    ,
    pepper½
    and
    garlic1 clove
    , fry for 1 minute.
  2. Add the
    chicken1
    and cook until no longer pink.
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients together and add. Cook until it bubbles.
  4. Add the
    noodles0.5x 400 g pack
    . Cook for 1 minute, stirring well to mix everything together.
  5. Eat immediately!
