Leek and salmon pie

Gluten-free: use gluten-free plain flour in the sauce.

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 5 medium potatoes, cut into 2 cm dice
  • 75 g butter
  • 2 medium leeks, sliced
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 1½ mugs milk
  • 300 ml cream, double or single
  • 4 salmon steaks, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 1 mug frozen peas, defrosted
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped dill
  • ½ mug Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Grease a large casserole dish.
  2. Put the
    potatoes5 medium
    on to boil and simmer for 10 minutes. Drain and return to the pan. Add 25 g of the
    butter75 g
    and shake the pan around to distribute.
  3. Add another 25 g of the
    butter75 g
    to a large frying pan and fry the leaks until they begin to soften and brown. Put the
    leeks2 medium
    in the casserole dish along with the defrosted
    peas1 mug
    .
  4. Add the last 25 g
    butter75 g
    to the frying pan along with the
    flour2 tbsp
    , and cook on a medium heat until the
    flour2 tbsp
    is absorbed. Add the
    milk1½ mugs
    and
    cream300 ml
    to the pan and gently bring to the boil, stirring all the time. Add the
    fish4
    ,
    peas1 mug
    and
    dill2 tbsp
    to the sauce and cook, on a medium heat, for 2 minutes. The
    fish4
    does not need to be cooked through as it will go in the oven.
  5. Pour the sauce and the
    fish4
    into the casserole dish and mix with the
    leeks2 medium
    . Pile the
    potatoes5 medium
    on top and sprinkle the
    Parmesan½ mug
    over.
  6. Place in the oven for 20 minutes. The top should be lightly browned.
