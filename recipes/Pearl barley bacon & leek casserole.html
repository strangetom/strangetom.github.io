Pearl barley, bacon & leek casserole

This storecupboard grain makes a great alternative to rice or couscous. Team with bacon and veggies in this risotto-like stew.

Servings: 4

Total: 55 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 leeks, thickly sliced
  • 2 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 300 g pearl barley
  • 4 carrots, cubed
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard, plus extra to serve
  • 1 litre chicken stock
  • 300 g Savoy cabbage, shredded
  • 200 g lean bacon joint, cooked, chopped into small pieces

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pan over a medium heat. Add the
    olive oil1 tbsp
    and cook the
    leeks2
    for a few mins, then add the
    garlic2 clove
    and cook for just 1 min more.
  2. Add the
    pearl barley300 g
    ,
    carrots4
    and
    mustard1 tbsp
    , then pour over the
    chicken stock1 litre
    . Season with plenty of ground black pepper and simmer for 20 mins, stirring occasionally. Add the
    cabbage300 g
    with the
    bacon200 g
    , and cook for 5-10 mins until
    cabbage300 g
    is wilted and tender. Serve with extra Dijon
    mustard1 tbsp
    on the side.
