Sweet potato & spinach bake

1 hr 15 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

If you're after some substantial comfort food on a budget then this vegetarian bake is just the thing. Add lamb chops for the meat eaters.

Ingredients

  • 300 ml single cream (or double cream for a really creamy texture)
  • 1 clove garlic, peeled
  • 2 sprigs thyme or rosemary
  • 250 g bag frozen spinach
  • freshly grated nutmeg
  • butter for greasing
  • 850 g sweet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced (about 3 mm thick)
  • 25 g grated hard cheese, such as cheddar Parmesan or veggie alternative

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Put the
    cream300 ml
    ,
    garlic1 clove
    and
    herb2 sprigs
    sprigs into a small saucepan and slowly bring to just below boiling. Turn off the heat, season and leave to infuse.
  2. Put the
    spinach250 g
    into a colander, pour over a kettle of boiling water and leave to drain for a few mins. Then squeeze out as much water as possible. Season with salt, pepper and freshly grated nutmeg.
  3. Grease an ovenproof dish generously with butter and spread half the
    sweet potato850 g
    slices across the bottom. Top with a layer of
    spinach250 g
    , then the remaining potato. Pour over the
    cream300 ml
    mixture, through a sieve to remove the
    garlic1 clove
    and herbs, then sprinkle with
    cheese25 g
    . Bake for 45-55 mins until golden and tender.
