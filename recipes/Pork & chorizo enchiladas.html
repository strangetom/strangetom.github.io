Pork & chorizo enchiladas

his Mexican one-pot dish is a delicious and easy, make-ahead family meal - serve half and freeze the rest for another day.

1 hr 40 mins
8 servings

  • kcal 812
  • fat 41 g
  • saturates 18 g
  • carbs 60 g
  • sugar 19 g
  • fibre 8 g
  • protein 46 g
  • salt 3.2 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large onions, halved and thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 2 heaped tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 red chillies, halved deseeded and sliced
  • 500 g pork mince
  • 2x 200 g packs cooking chorizo sausages, removed from their skins
  • 680 g bottle passata
  • 1 pork or chicken stock cube
  • 4 peppers - 2 red and 2 green, deseeded, quartered and sliced
  • 2x 400 g cans borlotti beans, drained
  • 30 g pack coriander, chopped
  • 500 g tub fromage frais (not fat-free)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2x pack of 8 soft corn tortillas
  • 140 g mature cheddar, grated
  • green salad, to serve

  2. Tip in the
    pork500 g
    and
    chorizo2x 200 g packs
    , turn up the heat and fry the meat, stirring and breaking it down until it changes colour. Pour in the
    passata680 g
    and 300ml water, then crumble in the
    stock cube1
    .
  3. Pile in the
    peppers4
    , stir, cover and simmer over a low heat for 30 mins until the meat and
    peppers4
    are tender. Stir in the
    beans2x 400 g cans
    and two-thirds of the
    coriander30 g pack
    .
  4. Meanwhile, tip the
    fromage frais500 g tub
    (with any liquid in the tub) into a bowl, and beat in the
    egg1 large
    , remaining
    coriander30 g pack
    and seasoning. Get out 2 ovenproof and freezer-proof dishes.
  5. Spoon the meat onto the centre of the
    tortillas2x pack of 8
    , roll up and arrange 8 in each dish. Spoon half of the
    fromage frais500 g tub
    mixture on top and smooth it to cover the
    tortillas2x pack of 8
    . Scatter each with half the
    cheese140 g
    and remaining chillies.
  6. Heat oven to 170°C fan/Gas 5 and bake for 25 mins until golden, then serve with a green salad.
