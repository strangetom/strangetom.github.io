Mango chicken salad

Super quick and easy recipe. The curry paste is not too spicy for children to try. If you want, you can add more spice to taste, but not too much or you will drown the other more subtle flavours. Gluten-free: make sure the curry paste is gluten-free.

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Gluten Free
