Mango chicken salad

  • Chicken

  • 25 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

Super quick and easy recipe. The curry paste is not too spicy for children to try. If you want, you can add more spice to taste, but not too much or you will drown the other more subtle flavours. Gluten-free: make sure the curry paste is gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 dessert spoon of mild curry past
  • juice of lemon
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Dressing
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 150 ml natural yoghurt
  • 1 desert spoon mango chutney
  • 1 desert spoon mango chutney
  • 100 g watercress
  • 1/2 cucumber, cut into thing strips
  • 6 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 medium cos lettuce, sliced
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and cut into slices

Instructions

  1. Put the chicken breast pieces in a bowl and add the curry paste and lemon juice. Mix together and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, mix the together the dressing ingredients and set aside until needed.
  3. Prepare the salad ingredients and divide between the plates.
  4. Heat the oil in a large frying pan and fry the chicken pieces on a medium heat, taking care not to burn the curry paste. They should take no longer than 5 minutes.
  5. Serve on the salad with the dressing drizzled over.
