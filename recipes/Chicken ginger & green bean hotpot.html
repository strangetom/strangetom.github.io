Chicken, ginger & green bean hotpot

10 mins Serves 2

Ingredients

  • ½ tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cm knob of ginger, peeled and cut into matchsticks
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ½ onion, thinly sliced into half moons
  • 1 tbsp Thai fish sauce
  • ½ tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 250 g skinless boneless chicken thighs, trimmed of all fat and cut in half
  • 125 ml chicken stock
  • 50 g green beans, trimmed and cut into 2.5 cm lengths
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaf
  • steamed rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil½ tbsp
    in a pan over a medium heat. Add the
    ginger2 cm
    ,
    garlic1 clove
    and
    onion½
    and stir fry for about 5 minutes, or until lightly golden. Add the
    fish sauce1 tbsp
    ,
    sugar½ tbsp
    ,
    chicken250 g
    and
    stock125 ml
    . Cover and cook over a medium heat for 15 minutes.
  2. For the final 3 minutes of cooking, add the
    green beans50 g
    . Remove from the the heat and stir in half of the
    coriander1 tbsp
    .
  3. Serve with steamed rice and the remaining
    coriander1 tbsp
    scattered over.

  • salt 2 g
