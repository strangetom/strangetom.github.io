Beef pot-fried rice

Serves 4-5     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

Source: Delicious Tonight

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • ½ yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1½ cup long-grain white rice, not rinsed
  • 2½ cup frozen mixed diced vegetables (carrots, peas, and corn)
  • 2½ cup low-sodium chicken broth
Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1½ tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1½ tsp dark soy sauce (or more light soy sauce)
  • ¼ tsp white pepper (or black pepper)
Sesame eggs
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 eggs, lightly whisked
  • ⅛ tsp kosher salt
To serve
  • 2 green onion stems, finely sliced
