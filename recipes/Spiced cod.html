Spiced cod

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ½ red onion
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tbsp greshly grated ginger
  • ½ fat red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 600 g cod fillets, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • 1½ mugs basmati rice
  • 200 g mangetout
  • 2 tbsp toasted flaked almonds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put the sauce ingredients in a blender and blitz.
  2. Put the
    fish600 g
    in an oven proof dish and pour over the sauce. Season with salt and pepper and leave to marinade for about 30 minutes. Meanwhile, cook the rice.
  3. Preheat the grill and place the
    fish600 g
    under the grill for about 5 minutes on a high heat.
  4. Slice the
    mangetout200 g
    lengthways into thin strips, add to the rice pan and leave to simmer for a further 2 minutes. Add the
    almonds2 tbsp
    and mix together. Serve with the
    fish600 g
    .
