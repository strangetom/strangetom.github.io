MaghrebiHarissa beef msemen

Serves 6-8    |     Prep 30 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

Pronounced miss-i-men, this flaky, buttery pancake can be found in different guises across North Africa and the Middle East. In Morocco, it is served with honey for breakfast, or found sizzling on hot plates in market stalls, stuffed with tomatoes or ground meat, like this version.

Source: Orange Blossom & Honey

Ingredients

    /    
    /

For the dough
  • 300 g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 60 g fine semolina flour
  • pinch salt
  • pinch sugar
  • ¼ tsp dried yeast
  • 200 ml warm water
  • 5 tbsp olive oil, 2 for greasing and the rest for frying
For the filling
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • ½ red pepper, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 200 g minced beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp harissa
  • Juice of ¼ lemon
  • Sea salt
For the harissa yogurt
  • 150 g Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp harissa
