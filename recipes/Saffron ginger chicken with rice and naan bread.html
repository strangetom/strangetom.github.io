Saffron ginger chicken with rice and naan bread

  • Chicken

  • 35 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • juice of lemon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions, cut into wedges
  • 400 ml can coconut milk
  • 1 pinch saffron or 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 fat red chilli, desseded and cut into slices
  • 1/2 chicken stock cube
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 2 mugs rice
  • 1 mug peas
  • 4 naan breads

Instructions

  1. Put the chicken in a bowl with the ginger, garlic, lemon, paprika and ground coriander. Leave overnight if possible. A minimum of 1 hour is OK.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok and fry the onions. Take the chicken from the marinade and fry until the meat is browned. Add the coconut milk, saffron (turmeric), chilli, stock and the marinade juices. Bring the boil, then turn down to simmer with a lid on for 40 minutes. Stir occasionally. Add the coriander just before serving.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, put 4 mugs of water in a saucepan. Bring to the boil and add the rice. Bring back to the boil, then turn down to simmer, with the lid on, for 10 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
  4. After the rice has been cooking for 7 minutes, add the peas on top.
  5. Heat the naan breads according to the instructions on the pack.
  6. Serve with a little yoghurt.
