Veggie

25 mins

4 servings

Spicy-vegetable chapatti wraps

Curry can be deceivingly high in fat - this version is packed with flavour and has only 7 g fat per serving.


Ingredients

  • 300 g sweet potatoes, peeled and roughly cubed
  • 400 g can peeled plum tomatoes
  • 400 g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 0.5 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp mild curry paste
  • 100 g baby leaf spinach
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 4 plain chapattis
  • 3 tbso fat-free Greek yogurt

Instructions

  1. Cook the sweet potato in salted boiling water for 10-12 minutes until tender.
  2. Meanwhile, put the tomatoes, chickpeas, chilli flakes and curry paste in another pan and simmer gently for about 5 minutes, stirring all the time.
  3. Heat grill to high. Drain the sweet potatoes and add to the tomato mixture. Stir in the spinach and cook for a minute until just starting to wilt. Stir in the coriander, season to taste and keep warm.
  4. Sprinkle the chapattis with a little water and grill 20-30 seconds each side. Spoon on the vegetable filling, top with yogurt and fold in half to serve.
