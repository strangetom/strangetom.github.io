Lamb carry

20 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 30 g butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • ½ tsp grated nutmeg
  • ½ tsp gound cinnamon
  • salt and pepper
  • 900 g lean lamb, cut into 3 cm chunks
  • 200 ml chicken stock
  • 400 ml can coconut milk
  • 150 ml double cream
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • rice and peas , to serve

Instructions

  1. Wash the meat and pat it dry with kitchen paper.
  2. Melt the
    butter30 g
    in a large, lidded casserole dish. Soften the
    onion1
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    in the
    butter30 g
    . Add the
    tomatoes400 g can
    ,
    thyme2 sprigs
    ,
    bay leaf1
    ,
    cloves2
    ,
    nutmeg½ tsp
    ,
    cinnamon½ tsp
    and seasoning. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes.
  3. Add the
    lamb900 g
    and cook for 3 minutes, stirring. Add the
    stock200 ml
    ,
    coconut milk400 ml can
    and
    cream150 ml
    . Cover, bring to the boil, then lower the heat and simmer for 2 - 2.5 hours until the meat is completely tender and cooked through.
  4. Add the
    lime juice2 tbsp
    , cook for another minute or two and check for seasoning. Serve with rice and peas.
