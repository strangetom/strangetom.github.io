BBQ bolognese

Serves 4    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 45 mins

We never get bored of finding fresh ways to make spag bol even better! BBQ seasoning and sweet smoked paprika makes a gorgeous, smoky-flavoured sauce that’ll transform one pack of mince into a batch-cookable family favourite. Enjoy it with the fun of twisting spaghetti around your fork or serve it with any pasta shape – you won’t regret wiping your plate clean with some crusty garlic bread …

Source: Pinch of Nom Express
