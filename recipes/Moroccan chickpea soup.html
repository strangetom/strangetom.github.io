Moroccan chickpea soup

25 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 sticks celery, chopped
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 600 ml vegetable stock
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes with garlic
  • 400 g chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 100 g frozen broad beans
  • juice and zest of 0.5 lemon
  • large handful coriander or parsley leaves
  • warm coriander naan, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan, then fry the
    onion1 medium
    and
    celery2 sticks
    gently for 10 minutes until softened, stirring frequently. Tip in the
    cumin2 tsp
    and fry for another minute.
  2. Turn up the heat, then add the
    stock600 ml
    ,
    tomatoes400 g can
    and
    chickpeas400 g
    , plus a good grind of black pepper. Simmer for 8 minutes. Throw in the
    broad beans100 g
    and lemon juice, and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  3. Season to taste then top with a sprinkling of lemon zest and chopped
    herbslarge handful
    . Serve with warm coriander naan.
