Sausage, sage and blackberry traybake

A sweet and sticky autumnal traybake for cosy evenings in

80 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free
Dairy Free

  • kcal 702
  • fat 42 g
  • saturates 11 g
  • carbs 48 g
  • sugar 34 g
  • fibre 13 g
  • protein 26 g
  • salt 2.2 g

Ingredients

  • 1 medium butternut squash, peeled, deseeded and cut into 2 cm wedges
  • 4 large parsnips, each peeled and cut lengthways into 6
  • 2 medium red onions, cut into wedges
  • 3 tbsp sage, finely chopped
  • 8 sausages - make sure these are GF if required
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tbsp clear honey
  • 4 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 225 g sweet blackberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C, Gas 7. Toss the vegetables, sage and sausages together in a large roasting tin with the oil and some seasoning. Spread out in the tin. Roast for 40 minutes, turning the sausages halfway through so that they colour evenly.
  2. Whisk the honey and mustard together. Scatter the blackberries over the sausage and veg then toss the honey-mustard mixture through the tin. Return to the oven for 15 minutes until the sausages are cooked through and the root veg are tender.
