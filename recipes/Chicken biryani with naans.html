Chicken biryani with naans

Servings: 2

Total: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2x 1" cube butter
  • 1 medium onion, cut into wedges
  • 4 boneless chicken thighs
  • 4 tbsp korma curry paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric or 1 pinch saffron
  • 1 mug rice
  • ½ chicken stock cube
  • 1 mug yoghurt
  • ½ mug raisins
  • 2 mugs water
  • ¼ mug toasted, flaked almonds
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 naan breads

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    butter2x 1" cube
    in a large pan or wok and cook the
    onion1 medium
    until they become soft. Add the
    chicken4
    and cook until the skins have browned a little.
  2. Add the
    korma paste4 tbsp
    and
    turmeric1 tsp
    or saffron and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the
    rice1 mug
    and stir well. Cook for 1 minute, allowing the
    rice1 mug
    to absorb the flavours.
  4. Add the
    stock½
    ,
    yoghurt1 mug
    ,
    raisins½ mug
    and
    water2 mugs
    . Bring to the boil. Turn down and simmer with a lid on for 25-30 minutes. Check every 10 minutes to make sure it is not sticking to the bottom. You may need to add a little more
    water2 mugs
    . Try not to stir too much as the
    rice1 mug
    will become mushy.
  5. At the end of the cooking time, add the
    coriander leaves1 tbsp
    and
    almonds¼ mug
    stirring as little as possible. Do no cook any more.
  6. Serve with warm
    naan breads2
    .
