Baked lamb, vegetables and rice

1 hr 55 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb off the bone
  • 2 large red onions
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • 1Â½ kg mixed vegetables, aubergines, courgettes, chard, carrots, peppers, green beans, pumpkin
  • 150 ml olive oil
  • 500 g tomatoes, tinned is fine
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp powdered chilli
  • 125 g risotto rice

Instructions

  1. Trim and cube the
    meat1 kg
    into bite-sized pieces. Skin and slice the
    onions2 large
    and
    garlic3-4 cloves
    . Hull, scrape and slice or chunk the vegetables as appropriate. Scaled the
    tomatoes500 g
    to loosen the skins then peel and chop.
  2. Warm half of the olive
    oil150 ml
    in a frying pan. Put in the
    onions2 large
    and
    garlic3-4 cloves
    and fry them until they turn pale gold, then push them to one side and add the
    meat1 kg
    . Fry gently for a moment and then season with
    salt1 tsp
    and
    chilli1 tsp
    .
  3. Transfer the
    meat1 kg
    and onion mixture with its
    oil150 ml
    to a wide shallow baking dish. Cover the
    meat1 kg
    with a layer of vegetables. Season again. Sprinkly in a layer of
    rice125 g
    . Finish with a layer of the sliced
    tomatoes500 g
    .
  4. Pour in enough boiling water to submerge to a depth of one finger. Trickle with the rest of the
    oil150 ml
    .
  5. Cover the dish and put it to bake in a moderate oven, 180°C/Gas 4 for 1.5 hours.
  6. Take off the lid for the final 20 minutes. The kitchen will fill with the enticing scent and the cooking liquid will be concentrated into a rich sauce.
