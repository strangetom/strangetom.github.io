Tuna & tomato rice

Serves 4 Share

Ingredients

  • 225 g long grain rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 rashers smoked streaky bacon, trimmed of fat, chopped
  • 175 g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1x 200 g can tuna in spring water, drained
  • generous handful flat-leaf parsley leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  • kcal 346
  • fat 6 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 57 g
  • sugar 3 g
  • fibre 6 g
  • protein 19 g
  • salt 3.52 g
