Garlicky mushroom penne

Add protein to a vegan pasta dish by using a low-fat houmous in the sauce. With the mushrooms and wholemeal penne, you have a healthy and filling dinner

Servings: 2

Total: 35 mins

Vegetarian

Vegan

Ingredients

  • 210 g can chickpeas, no need to drain
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 1 tsp vegetable bouillon
  • 2 tsp tahini
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 115 g wholemeal penne
  • 2 tsp rapeseed oil
  • 2 red onions, halved and sliced
  • 200 g closed cup mushrooms, roughly chopped
  • generous handful chopped parsley
  • ½ lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. To make the houmous, tip the
    chickpeas210 g can
    and their liquid into a bowl and add the
    lemon juice1 tbsp
    ,
    garlic1 large clove
    ,
    bouillon1 tsp
    ,
    tahini2 tsp
    and ground
    coriander½ tsp
    . Blitz to a wet paste with a hand blender, still retaining some texture from the
    chickpeas210 g can
    .
  2. Cook the
    pasta115 g
    according to pack instructions. Meanwhile, heat the
    oil2 tsp
    in a non-stick wok or large frying pan and add the
    onions2
    and
    mushrooms200 g
    , stirring frequently until softened and starting to caramelise.
  3. Drain the
    pasta115 g
    and tip in with the
    mushrooms200 g
    , then take off the heat and stir through the houmous and parsley. Toss together lightly, squeeze over the
    lemon juice1 tbsp
    and serve, adding a dash of water to loosen the mixture a little if needed.
