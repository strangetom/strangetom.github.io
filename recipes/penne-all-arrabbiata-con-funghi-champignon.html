ItalianPenne all'arrabbiata con funghi champignon

Serves 4     |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 25 mins

Penne all'arrabbiata with champignon mushrooms are a really tasty and spicy vegetarian first course. Made with tomato and chilli!

Source: ricette.giallozafferano.it

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 400 g penne rigate
  • 350 g Champignon mushrooms
  • 500 g tomato puree
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 fresh red chillies, spicy
  • 1 sprig parsley
  • extra virgin olive oil, to taste
  • salt, to taste
