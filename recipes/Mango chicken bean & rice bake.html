Mango chicken, bean & rice bake

Drumsticks are ideal for an all-in-one traybake - the rice, beans and spicy seasoning make it a little like Caribbean jerk chicken.

90 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 753
  • fat 16 g
  • saturates 3 g
  • carbs 109 g
  • sugar 32 g
  • fibre 6 g
  • protein 39 g
  • salt 1.2 g

Ingredients

  • spring onions, chopped
  • thumb-sized piece ginger, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded
  • freshly chopped coriander
  • 1 tbsp thyme leaf
  • zest and juice 1 lime
  • 1 lime cut into wedges to serve
  • 1.5 tsp ground allspice
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 8 chicken drumstick
  • 300 g long grain rice
  • 400 g can kidney bean, rinsed and drained
  • 500 ml chicken stock
  • 1 ripe mango, peeled, stoned and cut into bite-sized chunks
  • 200 g mango chutney, plus extra to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Put the spring onions, ginger, garlic, chilli, coriander stalks, thyme, lime zest and juice, allspice and oil in a food processor, then blend to a paste. Pour over the chicken drumsticks and leave to marinate for at least 1 hr, or preferably up to 1 day.
  2. Heat oven to 180°C/Gas 4. Tip the rice and beans into a large roasting tin with deep sides.
  3. Remove the chicken from the marinade and set aside. Mix the stock into the marinade in the bowl and stir well. Pour the stock over the rice and beans, then put the chicken drumsticks and the mango pieces on top. Cover the tray tightly with foil and bake for 30 mins.
  4. Take the tray out of the oven and remove the foil. Increase the temperature to 220°C/Gas 7. Spoon the
    mango chutney200 g
    over the drumsticks and return to the oven, uncovered, for 35-40 mins, to brown the chicken pieces and allow the rice to absorb all the liquid.
  5. Before serving, fluff up the rice a little with a fork and scatter with the coriander leaves. Serve with lime wedges and extra
    mango chutney200 g
    .
