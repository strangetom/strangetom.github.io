Turkey bolognese

40 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 546
  • fat 10 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 76 g
  • sugar 0 g
  • fibre 6 g
  • protein 43 g
  • salt 0.75 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 500 g turkey mince
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 300 ml chicken or beef stock
  • 250 g spaghetti
  • 1 large courgette, finely chopped
  • 6 tomatoes, deseeded and chopped
  • small handful chopped flat-leaf parsley leaves, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a large pan and fry the
    onion1 large
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    for 4-5 minutes over a low heat until softened. Stir in the
    turkey mince500 g
    and cook for 5 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in the
    chopped tomatoes400 g can
    ,
    tomato puree2 tbsp
    and
    stock300 ml
    . Bring to the boil then simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the
    spaghetti250 g
    according to the pack instructions. Stir the
    courgette1 large
    and fresh
    tomatoes6
    into the sauce and simmer for 5-6 minutes. Season.
  3. Drain the pasta and divide amonst four plates. Spoon over the sauce and serve scattered with
    parsleysmall handful
