Thai prawn curry

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 mugs rice
  • pilau rice seasoning
  • 2x 180 g pack of cooked prawns
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 400 g tin coconut milk
  • 200 g pack of mangetout and baby sweetcorn, both cut in half length ways
  • 6 spring onions

Instructions

  1. Make the pilau rice (1:2 ratio of rice:water).
  2. Place the prawns of sime kitchen towel to absorb any extra moisture from them.
  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Add the curry paste and cook for 30 seconds stirring frequently. Add the coconut milk and baby sweetcorn. Cook on a high heat for 3-4 minutes, the sauce should thicken slightly.
  4. Add the prawns, spring onions and the mange tout and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. When the rice is cooked, serve the curry on top of the rice.
