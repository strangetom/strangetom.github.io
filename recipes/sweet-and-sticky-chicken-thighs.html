Sweet and sticky chicken thighs

Serves 4     |     Prep 25 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Source: www.itv.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
For the chicken thighs
  • 2 heaped tbsps tomato purée
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsps garam masala
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fenugreek powder
  • 125 ml tomato ketchup
  • 4 tbsps runny honey
  • 8 skin-on boneless chicken thighs
For the red cabbage coleslaw
  • 1 small red cabbage, quartered, cored and finely shredded
  • 2 small red onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 large red chilli, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 150 g raisins
  • 1 bunch of fresh coriander, leaves and stalks roughly chopped
  • 4 tbsps salad dressing (see below)
  • Pinch of salt, to taste
For the dressing
  • 100 ml vegetable oil
  • ½ tsps black mustard seeds
  • 20 g date molasses
  • 2 tbsps white wine vinegar
  • Pinch of salt
  • ½ tsps caster or granulated sugar
