Spaghetti carbonara

20 mins Serves 3

Ingredients

  • 250 g spaghetti
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1x 200 g pack pancetta lardons
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • a little freshly grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Put the spaghetti on to cook.
  2. Heat a little oil in a wok and fry onions and garlic until the onions are soft. Add the pancetta lardons and cook until they begin to brown.
  3. Put the eggs in a large bowl and beat well.
  4. Drain the pasta, but retain a little of the cooking liquid (about 2 tablespoons).
  5. Stir all the ingredients, except the basil, into the bowl containing the beaten egg. The heat from the spaghetti and lardons should cook the eggs and create a creamy sauce.
  6. Add the chopped basil.
