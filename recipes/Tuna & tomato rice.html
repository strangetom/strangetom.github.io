Tuna & tomato rice

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 225 g long grain rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 rashers smoked streaky bacon, trimmed of fat, chopped
  • 175 g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 200 g can tuna in spring water, drained
  • generous handful flat-leaf parsley leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the
    rice225 g
    in salted boiling water for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a pan and fry the
    garlic2 cloves
    ,
    onion1
    and
    bacon2 rashers
    for 5 minutes, stirring often. Add the
    mushrooms175 g
    and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Stir in the
    tomatoes2x 400 g cans
    and
    tuna200 g can
    and season well. Heat through for 5 minutes.
  3. Drain the
    rice225 g
    , stir it until the tomato sauce with the
    parsleygenerous handful
    , mixing gently, then divide among 4 bowls to serve.
