Chicken with apples and cider

35 mins Serves 4

This simple recipe uses only 6 ingredients. It also works well with pork steaks.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 onion, cut into wedges
  • 2 eating apples, peeled cored and cut into 8 wedges
  • 300 ml dry cider
  • 150 ml chicken stock
  • rice or mashed potato, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan with a lide and fry the chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes
     on each side until golden. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside. Lower the temperature slightly and add the onion. Fry, stirring, for 2-3 minutes
     until tinged brown. Add the apple and cook over a high heat for 5 minutes
     until golden.
  2. Still over a high heat, pour in the cider and bubble for 2 minutes
     to reduce slight. Add the stock, stirring to scrape the bits from the bottom of the pan. Lower the heat
  3. Return the chicken to the pan, cover and simmer for 5 minutes
     until it is almost cooked. Remove the lid and simmer for 3-4 minutes
     to thicken the sauce a little. Serve with rice or mashed potato.
