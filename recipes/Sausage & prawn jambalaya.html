Sausage & prawn jambalaya

45 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 6 good quality sausages
  • oil to fry
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 250 g long grain rice
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400-500 ml chicken stock
  • 260 g cooked and peeled king prawns

Instructions

  1. Fry the
    sausages6
    in a large, deep, lidded frying pan until golden all over, then remove and set aside.
  2. Heat a little oil in the pan and gently cook the
    onion1
    for 5 minutes until it is soft.
  3. Add the
    garlic2 cloves
    ,
    pepper1
    and
    paprika1 tbsp
    and cook for a few minutes more, then stir in the
    rice250 g
    , mixing to coat all the grains well. Tip in the
    tomatoes400 g can
    and enough
    stock400-500 ml
    to cover the
    rice250 g
    . Simmer with the lid on the pan for 10-12 minutes until the
    rice250 g
    is tender. Add more
    stock400-500 ml
    if you need during cooking.
  4. About 5 minutes before the end of the cooking time, slice the
    sausages6
    and return them to the pan. Cover and continue to cook until the
    rice250 g
    is tender.
  5. Stir through the
    prawns260 g
    , put the lid on and leave to heat through. Season and sever immediately.
