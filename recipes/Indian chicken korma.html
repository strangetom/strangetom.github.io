Indian chicken korma

Creamy, spiced Chicken Korma is the stuff dreams are made of. Loosen up those pants and make this delectable Indian dish at home!

Servings: 6

Total: 3 hr 25 mins

Ingredients

For the chicken marinade
  • 3 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
For the sauce
  • 2 white onions, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 4 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp ground coriander seed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ⅛ tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 tbsps extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 large tomatoes, diced small
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger root
  • ½ cup ground raw almonds
  • 1 cup unsweetened canned coconut milk
  • 1½ cups plain low-fat yogurt
  • ½ a small red chilli, de-seeded and minced (dried or fresh)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (packed)

Instructions

  1. Drizzle the
    chicken3 lbs

    and pepper. Massage into the meat and cover, leaving to marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the
    chicken3 lbs
    for 5-6 minutes per side, until cooked through.
  3. Meanwhile, make the sauce: place the
    onions2
    ,
    garlic6 cloves
    , and 1 cup of water in the bowl of a blender. Puree until smooth.
  4. Measure out the spices (
    curry powder2 tsp

    oil2 tsp
    over medium heat. Once the
    oil2 tsp
    is shimmering, add the pureed onion mixture and cook for a 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until it begins to darken in color.
  6. Add the
    tomatoes3 large
    ,
    ginger1 tbsp
    ,
    ground almonds½ cup
    ,
    coconut milk1 cup
    ,
    yogurt1½ cups
    ,
    red chilli½
    , pre-measured spices, and
    brown sugar1 tbsp
    . Stir well.
  7. Turn the heat down to low and simmer 30 minutes. Cut the
    chicken3 lbs
    into bite-sized pieces and add to the pan; simmer for an additional 15 minutes. Serve with steamed basmati rice and/or naan.
