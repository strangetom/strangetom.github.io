Indian chicken korma

Creamy, spiced Chicken Korma is the stuff dreams are made of. Loosen up those pants and make this delectable Indian dish at home!

205 mins
6 servings

  • kcal 734
  • fat 33 g
  • saturates 12 g
  • carbs 29 g
  • sugar 17 g
  • fibre 5 g
  • protein 80 g
  • salt 0.9 g

Ingredients

  • For the chicken marinade
  • 3 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • For the sauce
  • 2 white onions, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 6 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 4 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander seed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 0.5 teaspoon cumin
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 0.125 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 large tomatoes, diced small
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger root
  • 0.5 cup ground raw almonds
  • 1 cup unsweetened canned coconut milk
  • 1.5 cups plain low-fat yogurt
  • 0.5 a small red chili, de-seeded and minced (dried or fresh)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (packed)

Instructions

  1. Drizzle the chicken with the oil and sprinkle on the

    ,

    salt1 teaspoon
    and pepper. Massage into the meat and cover, leaving to marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side, until cooked through.
  3. Meanwhile, make the sauce: place the onions,
    garlic6 cloves
    , and 1 cup of water in the bowl of a blender. Puree until smooth.
  4. Measure out the spices (
    curry powder4 teaspoons
    through nutmeg) into a small bowl.
  5. In a large saucepan heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Once the oil is shimmering, add the pureed onion mixture and cook for a 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until it begins to darken in color.
  6. Add the
    tomatoes3 large
    , ginger, ground almonds, coconut milk, yogurt, red chili, pre-measured spices, and
    brown sugar1 tablespoon
    . Stir well.
  7. Turn the heat down to low and simmer 30 minutes. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and add to the pan; simmer for an additional 15 minutes. Serve with steamed basmati rice and/or naan.
