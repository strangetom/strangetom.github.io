Broccoli lemon chicken

25 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 372
  • fat 13 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 15 g
  • sugar 6 g
  • fibre 3 g
  • protein 48 g
  • salt 0.69 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 340 g pack mini chicken breast fillets
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 200 g pack Tenderstem brocolli, stems halved if very long
  • 200 ml chicken stock
  • 1 heaped tsp cornflower
  • 1 tbsp clear honey
  • grated zest 1/2 lemon
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • large handful roasted cashew nuts

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok. Add the chicken and fry for 3-4 minutes until golden. Remove from the pan to a plate and add the garlic and brocolli. Stir-fry for a minute or so, then cover and cook for 2 minutes more, until tender.
  2. Mix the stock, cornflour and honey well, then pour into the pan and stir until thickened. Tip the chicken back into the pan and let it heat through, then add the lemon zest and juice and the cashew nutes. Stir, then serve straight away.
