Chicken

20 mins

4 servings

Chicken and mushrooms curry with coconut milk


	
					
Ingredients

					

						
    
							
  • 1.5 mugs basmati rice
    • 
							
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 
							
  • 1 onion, sliced
    • 
							
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 
							
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
    • 
							
  • 4 tbsp korma curry paste
    • 
							
  • 400 g tin coconut milt
    • 
							
  • 250 g mushrooms, sliced
    • 
							
  • Peshwari nan to serve
    • 
							

					

				
					
Instructions

					

						
    
							
  1. Put the rice on to cook in 3 mugs of boiling water. Once cooked, remove from the heat and leave with a lid on until needed.  
    2. 
							
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok. Add the onion and fry until it begins to soften.  
    3. 
							
  3. Put the naan in the oven, see packet instructions.  
    4. 
							
  4. Add the chicken to the frying pan and fry until no longer pink. Add the curry paste and mix well.  
    5. 
							
  5. Add the flour and mix well.  
    6. 
							
  6. Add the coconut milk and the mushrooms. Bring to the boil and then turn down to simmer for 2 minutes.
    7.
