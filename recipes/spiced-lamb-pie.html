Spiced lamb pie

Serves 4    |     Prep 30 mins     |     Cook 4 hr 20 mins

This stunning filo pastry pie is a twist on a lamb tagine. A hearty main meal that wouldn't look out of place on any dinner-party table

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 800 g lamb shoulder, fat removed diced
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp ras el hanout
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • good pinch of ground cloves
  • good pinch of saffron (optional)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp pomegranate molasses
  • 1x 270 g pack filo pastry (4-6 sheets)
  • 50 g butter, melted
  • 50 g flaked almonds
  • 50 g pomegranate seeds
