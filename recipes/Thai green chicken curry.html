Thai green chicken curry

Servings: 8

Total: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 6 tsp Thai green curry paste
  • 2x 400 ml cans coconut milk
  • 450 g new potatoes, scrubbed and cut into chunks
  • 200 g pack trimmed green beans, halved
  • 4 tsp Thai fish sauce, or to taste
  • 2 tsp caster sugar
  • 6 bonesless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bitesized chunks
  • 3 wide strips lime zest
  • large handful basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a large wok, drop in the
    garlic2 cloves
    and stir until just golden. Add the
    curry paste6 tsp
    and stir for a couple of minutes, then pour in the
    coconut milk2x 400 ml cans
    and bring to the boil.
  2. Add the
    potatoes450 g
    and simmer for 10 minutes, then add the
    beans200 g
    and simmer for 5 minutes more. Both the
    potatoes450 g
    and
    beans200 g
    should be just tender by now - if not, cook a little longer.
  3. Stir in the
    fish sauce4 tsp
    and
    sugar2 tsp
    , then add the
    chicken6
    . Cover and simmer for 10 minutes until tender.
  4. Before serving, stir in the
    lime zest3 wide strips
    , followed by the
    basillarge handful
    .
