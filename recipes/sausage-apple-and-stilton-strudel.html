Sausage, apple and stilton strudel

Serves 6    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 45 mins

The strudel can be served as a starter or main, sliced up for buffet-type parties, or even made into bite-size treats.'

Source: www.sainsburysmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /

  • 1x 400 g pack Taste the Difference Lincolnshire sausages
  • 2 Pink Lady apples
  • 200 g Stilton, crumbled
  • ½x 20 g pack sage, leaves shredded
  • 1x 220 g pack filo pastry
  • 100 g butter, melted
  • 25 g mixed seeds
