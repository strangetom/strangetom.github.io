Spicy chicken pasties

Serves 6 Share

Often used for spicy Jamaican beef patties and some fried doughs, turmeric pastry makes a colourful change from regular shortcrust for pasties. The filling in these is tender chicken marinated in a zingy yoghurt mix and then grilled, with ready-made mango chutney too add moisture.

Ingredients

For the pastry
  • 225 g plain flour
  • good pinch of salt
  • ½ tsp ground turmeric
  • 125 g unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 4 tbsp icy water
For the filling
  • 175 g skinless boneless chicken breast, cut into 1.5 cm cubes
  • 3 tbsp natural yoghurt
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 cm piece root ginger, peeled and grated
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp ground coriander
  • good pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp mango chutney
  • beaten egg, to glaze
