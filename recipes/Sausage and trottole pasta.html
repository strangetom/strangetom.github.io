Sausage and trottole pasta

A brilliant weekday alternative to the normal spag bol. The chilli is optional if you have younger children. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta, stock cube and sausages.

25 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 500 g sausages
  • 1 mug water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 fat red chilli, sliced
  • 2 mugs trottole pasta
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped basil
