Poached chicken with chilli sauce

25 mins Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 mug water
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 mugs water
  • 1 tsp pilau rice seasoning
  • 1 mug basmati rice
Sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • Â¼ fat red chilli, thinly sliced
  • juice 2 limes
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips

Instructions

  1. Put the mug of water into a saucepan, add a pinch of salt and bring to the boil. Once boiling, add the chicken breasts. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes with a lid on the pan. Take off the heat and leave to stand for 15 minutes.
  2. Make the pilau rice. Once cooked, set aside with the lid on.
  3. To make the sauce, heat the oil in a frying pan, add the onion, ginger and garlic and fry for 30 seconds. Add the rest of the sauce ingredients and bring to the boil. Once the sauce has boiled, turn off the heat and set aside until needed.
  4. When the chicken is cooked, take outof the water and cut into slices.
  5. Arange the slices on a bed of rice. Put the cucumber strips alongside and pour some of sauce over the chicken.
