Poached chicken with chilli sauce

25 mins
2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 mug water
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 mugs water
  • 1 tsp pilau rice seasoning
  • 1 mug basmati rice
  • Sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 4 spring onions, sliced
  • 2 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 fat red chilli, thinly sliced
  • juice 2 limes
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips

Instructions

  1. Put the mug of
    water2 mugs
    into a saucepan, add a pinch of salt and bring to the boil. Once boiling, add the chicken breast. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes with a lid on the pan. Take off the heat and leave to stand for 15 minutes.
  2. Make the pilau rice. Once cooked, set aside with the lid on.
  3. To make the sauce, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a frying pan, add the onion, ginger and
    garlic2 cloves
    and fry for 30 seconds. Add the rest of the sauce ingredients and bring to the boil. Once the sauce has boiled, turn off the heat and set aside until needed.
  4. When the chicken is cooked, take outof the
    water2 mugs
    and cut into slices.
  5. Arange the slices on a bed of rice. Put the
    cucumber1
    strips alongside and pour some of sauce over the chicken.
