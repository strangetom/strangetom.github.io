Lamb & chilli gratinata

20 mins Serves 4 Gluten Free

Good, inexpensive midweek dish. You could exchange the passata for a tin of tomatoes and a tablespoon of tomato puree, if you like. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta, stock cube and breadcrumbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 mugs penne pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 500 g lamb mince
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 150 g mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ red chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 500 g jar of passata
  • ½ mug water
  • 1 lamb stock cube
  • 75 g olives
  • 100 g fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 2 slices wholemeal break, made into breadcrumbs
  • 200 g mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Grease a large casserole dish.
  2. Cook the
    pasta2 mugs
    . Drain, return to the pan and set to one side.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large frying pan or wok and fry the onions until they begin to soften.
  4. Add the
    mince500 g
    to the pan and fry until it is no longer pink.
  5. Add the
    garlic2 cloves
    ,
    mushrooms150 g
    ,
    chilli½
    ,
    passata500 g jar
    ,
    water½ mug
    and
    stock1
    , bring to the boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Season well with salt and pepper.
  6. Add the
    olives75 g
    ,
    spinach100 g
    and
    pasta2 mugs
    to the pan and mix together. Pour into the casserole dish.
  7. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the top and then distribute the
    mozzarella200 g
    over the top.
  8. Place in the oven for 15-20 minutes or until the top is nicely browned.
