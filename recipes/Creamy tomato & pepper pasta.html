Creamy tomato & pepper pasta

50 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 440
  • fat 10 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 71 g
  • sugar 12 g
  • fibre 4 g
  • protein 13 g
  • salt 0.38 g

Ingredients

  • 900 g ripe tomatoes
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 350 g pasta quills or shells
  • 4 tbsp half-fat creme fraiche
  • grated Parmesan, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Quarter the
    tomatoes900 g
    , roughly chop the
    pepper1
    (discard the seeds) and finely chop the
    garlic2 cloves
    . Put the veg in a roasting tin and drizzle over the oil. Season well.
  2. Roast for 30-35 minutes, stirring halfway through, until softened and slightly browned. Meanwhile, cook the
    pasta350 g
    in a large pan of salted boiling water for 10-12 minutes until just tender.
  3. Remove the vegetables from the oven and stir in the
    creme fraiche4 tbsp
    a spoonful at a time. Gently reheat on the stove, taste and season if necessary. Stir in the drained
    pasta350 g
    and serve with grated Parmesan.
