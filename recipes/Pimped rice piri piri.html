Pimped rice piri piri

This is a great way to send four chicken breasts a long way - all the way to the red pepper heart of Portugal.

Servings: 2

Total: 1 hr 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 280 g long-grain white rice, rinsed and drained
  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 2 yellow peppers, deseeded and sliced
  • 4-8 red chillies, sliced
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 l chicken stock
  • 3½ tbsp red wine vinegar
  • a few coriander leaves
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 4.
  2. Put the
    rice280 g
    in an even layer in the bottom of an oven proof dish.
  3. Season the
    chicken4
    with salt and pepper. Heat 2 tbsp of the
    oil3 tbsp
    in a frying pan over a high heat and brown the
    chicken4
    quickly on all sides, then put it on top of the
    rice280 g
    in the oven proof dish.
  4. Add the remaining
    oil3 tbsp
    to the pan and fry the onions and
    garlic3 cloves
    . Once softened, add the peppers and as many of the chillies as you can bear. Fry for about 5 minutes over a medium-low heat until the peppers are soft.
  5. Add the
    thyme1 tsp
    ,
    oregano½ tsp
    ,
    smoked paprika1 tbsp
    , chopped
    tomatoes2
    and
    tomato puree1 tbsp
    . Stir briefly and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Now add the
    stock1 l
    and
    red wine vinegar3½ tbsp
    , bring to the boil, then simmer for 3 minutes. Pour this mixture over the
    chicken4
    and
    rice280 g
    . Cover tightly and transfer to the oven for 45 minutes until the
    rice280 g
    and
    chicken4
    are tender. Sprinkle with
    coriander leavesa few
