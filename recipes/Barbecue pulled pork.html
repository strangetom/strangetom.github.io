Barbecue pulled pork

10 hr 15 mins Serves 4

Tuck into this healthy version of an all-American classic. In this video recipe lean pork is slow-cooked then shredded or 'pulled' and layered with barbecue sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1.5-2 kg pork shoulder, all fat removed
  • 5 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp mustard powder
  • 500 g passata
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 3 tbsp sweetener
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

  3. Remove the
    pork1.5-2 kg
    from the slow cooker and place on a cutting board. Allow the meat to cool for approximately 15 minutes, then shred into bite-sized pieces using two forks.
  4. Remove the sauce from the pan and set aside to drizzle on the meat later.
