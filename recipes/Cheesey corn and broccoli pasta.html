Cheesey corn and broccoli pasta

A quick and easy supper idea that children will love as much as adults.

20 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 620
  • fat 31 g
  • saturates 18.2 g
  • carbs 63 g
  • sugar ? g
  • fibre 4.5 g
  • protein 26 g
  • salt 1.75 g

Ingredients

  • 100 g farfelle (pasta bows)
  • 140 g broccoli florets
  • 198 g can sweetcorn, drained and rinsed
  • 25 g butter
  • 100 ml milk
  • 100 g cheddar cheese grated

Instructions

  1. Cook the
    pasta100 g
    in a large pan of salted boiling water for 8-10 minutes, until tender. Add the
    broccoli140 g
    and
    sweetcorn198 g can
    to the water for the last 4 minutes.
  2. While the
    pasta100 g
    is cooking, melt the
    butter25 g
    in a medium saucepan with the
    milk100 ml
    , then bring to the boil and remove from the heat. Tip in the
    cheese100 g
    and stir until melted.
  3. Drain the
    pasta100 g
    and vegetables well, return to the pan and pour in the sauce. Toss to mix well and season to taste before serving.
