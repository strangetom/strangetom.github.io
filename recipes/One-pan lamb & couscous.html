One-pan lamb & couscous

Whip up a deliciously flavoursome lamb dish in one pot and within half an hour.

25 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 610

Ingredients

  • 2 lamb steaks, approx 140 g each or 4 lamb chops trimmed of excess fat
  • 1-2 tsp harissa paste

  • handful dried fruit and nuts
  • 85 g couscous
  • 400 g can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 100 ml hot chicken stock or lamb stock (from a cube is fine)
  • handful mint leaves, torn
