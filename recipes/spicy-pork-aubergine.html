Spicy pork & aubergine

Serves 4    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Source: BBC Good Food: Low-fat Feasts

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 small aubergine, trimmed and diced
  • 500 g lean pork fillet, trimmed of any fat and sliced
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cut into chunky stips
  • 2-3 tbsp mild curry powder
  • 1x 400 g can plum tomatoes
  • basmati rice, to serve
