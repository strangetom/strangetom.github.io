Pasta and lentils (pasta e lenticchie)

Serves 6 Vegetarian Share

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsps extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for serving
  • 1 yellow onion, coarsely chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, smashed and peeled
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 cup brown or green lentils
  • 3 sprigs thyme (optional)
  • 3 fresh or dried bay leaves (optional)
  • 1x 28 oz can whole peeled tomatoes, cut in the can with scissors
  • 10 oz tubular or ridged pasta, like penne or radiatore (or use long noodles broken into pieces)
  • 0.5 cup freshly grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
