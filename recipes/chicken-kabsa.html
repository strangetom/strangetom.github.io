Chicken kabsa

2 hr 15 mins Serves 6 Middle Eastern

Feast like a king with this Arabian style flavorful rice and grilled chicken

Ingredients

Chicken kabsa
  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 3 onions, sliced
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 green chili
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 6 cloves
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • ¼ tsp ground cumin
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • 3 medium carrots, thinly sliced
  • 200 g canned diced tomatoes (or 3 tomatoes chopped)
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 1.5 kg whole chicken, cut into 6 pieces
  • 3 cups basmati rice, rinsed
  • ¼ cup raisins
  • Water
  • Salt to taste
  • Raisins, to garnish (optional)
  • Slivered almonds, to garnish (optional)
Kabsa spice mix
  • ¼ tsp ground cardamom
  • ¼ tsp ground white pepper
  • ¼ tsp saffron
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground all spice
  • ½ tsp dried whole lime powder

Instructions

  1. To make kabsa spice mix, combine together cardamom, white pepper, saffron, cinnamon, all spice and lime powder in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large heavy bottomed pan over medium-high heat. Add onions, ginger, garlic and green chilies. Saute for 2-3 minutes or until the onions turn golden brown. Add bay leaves, cloves, cardamom pods and cinnamon stick. Fry for a minute.
  3. Stir in tomato puree. Add ground nutmeg, ground black pepper, ground cumin, ground coriander and kabsa spice. Season with salt. Fry the spices for a minute. Add carrots and diced tomatoes. Stir, and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add chicken stock cubes and chicken pieces. Brown chicken, turning occasionally, for about 30 minutes. Remove the chicken pieces from the pan. Set aside.
  5. Add rice and raisins. Pour in 4 cups water. Season with salt. Bring it to the boil. Reduce the heat, cover with lid, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Preheat grill. Grill chicken for 10-15 minutes or until cooked through. Serve rice with grilled chicken.
