Spicy pork & aubergine

35 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 1 small aubergine, trimmed and diced
  • 500 g lean pork fillet, trimmed of any fat and sliced
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cut into chunky stips
  • 2-3 tbsp mild curry powder
  • 400 g can plum tomatoes
  • basmati rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large non-stick frying pan with a lid. Tip in the
    onions2
    and
    aubergine1 small
    and fry for 8 minutes, stirring frequently until soft and golden brown.
  2. Tip in the
    pork500 g
    and fry for 5 minutes, stirring occassionally until it starts to brown. Mix in the
    pepper2
    stips and stir fry for about 3 minutes until soft.
  3. Sprinkle in the
    curry powder2-3 tbsp
    . Stir fry for a minute, then pour in the
    tomatoes400 g can
    and 150 ml water. Stir vigorously, cover th pan and leave the curry to simmer for 5 minutes until the
    tomatoes400 g can
    break down to form a thick sauce. Season and serve with basmati rice.
