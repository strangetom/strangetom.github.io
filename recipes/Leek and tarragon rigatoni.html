Leek and tarragon rigatoni

Gluten-free: just use gluten-free pasta.

25 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 2 mugs rigatoni
  • 50 g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large leeks, chopped
  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • 300 ml single cream
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1/4 mug grated parmesan
