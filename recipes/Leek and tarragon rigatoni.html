Veggie

25 mins

4 servings

Leek and tarragon rigatoni


Ingredients

  • 2 mugs rigatoni
  • 50 g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 large leeks, chopped
  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 1 tsp dried tarragon
  • 300 ml single cream
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1/4 mug grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Put the pasta on to cook. Once cooked, drain and return to the pan, with a drizzle of olive oil, until needed.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the butter and oil in a wok. Fry the leeks until soft and beginning to broken. Add the mushrooms and fry for a further 2 minutes.
  3. Add the tarragon, cream, stock and cook for 2 minutes. Season to taste.
  4. Stir in the pasta and the parmesan.
