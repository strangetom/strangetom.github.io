Chicken & chorizo rice pot

1 hr 40 mins Serves 4

A one-pot chicken casserole for sharing, flavoured with Spanish sausage and slow-cooked with rice - an alternative paella.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 8 chicken pieces or 1 whole chicken, jointed
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and chopped into large chunks
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 225 g chorizo, skinned and sliced
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 tbsp thyme leaf, chopped
  • 150 ml white wine
  • 850 ml chicken stock
  • 400 g long grain rice
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large flameproof casserole dish and brown the
    chicken8
    pieces on all sides - you may have to do this in batches. Remove from the dish and put to one side.
  2. Lower the heat, add the
    onion1 large
    and
    pepper1
    , and gently cook for 10 mins until softened. Add the
    garlic3 cloves
    and
    chorizo225 g
    , and cook for a further 2 mins until the
    chorizo225 g
    has released some of its oils into the dish. Stir in the
    tomato purée1 tbsp
    and cook for 1 min more.
  3. Return the
    chicken8
    pieces to the dish along with the
    thyme1 tbsp
    , white
    wine150 ml
    and
    stock850 ml
    . Bring the liquid to a boil, cover the dish with a tight-fitting lid and lower the heat. Cook for 30 mins.
  4. Tip in the
    rice400 g
    and stir everything together. Cover, set over a low heat and cook for a further 15 mins, or until the
    rice400 g
    is cooked and has absorbed most of the cooking liquid. Remove from the heat and leave the dish to sit for 10 mins to absorb any remaining liquid. Season to taste and scatter with
    parsley2 tbsp
    to serve.
