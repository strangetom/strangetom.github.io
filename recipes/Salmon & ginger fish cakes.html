Salmon & ginger fish cakes

Create a complete superhealthy supper with these light, Asian-style fish cakes and sweet potato chips.

45 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 463
  • fat 24 g
  • saturates 4 g
  • carbs 33 g
  • sugar 10 g
  • fibre 4 g
  • protein 31 g
  • salt 0.32 g

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato, cut into chips
  • 4 tsp olive oil
  • 2x 140 g skinless salmon fillets
  • thumbnail-size piece ginger, grated
  • zest 1 lime, plus wedges to serve
  • bunch spring onions, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 200°C/180°C fan/Gas 6. Toss the chips in a roasting tin with 1 tsp oil. Season and bake for 20-25 mins.
  2. Chop the
    salmon2x 140 g
    as finely as you can and place in a bowl with the
    gingerthumbnail-size piece
    , lime zest and seasoning. Heat 1 tsp oil in a non-stick pan and soften the
    spring onionsbunch
    for 2 mins. Stir into the
    salmon2x 140 g
    , mix well and shape into 4 patties.
  3. Heat remaining oil in the pan and cook the patties for 3-4 mins each side until golden and cooked through. Cover with a lid and leave to rest for a few mins. Serve 2 patties each with the chips, mayo and lime wedges for squeezing.
