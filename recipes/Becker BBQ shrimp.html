Becker BBQ shrimp

Servings: 3

Total: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice
  • 450 g raw shrimp
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp whole peppercorns
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup beer

Instructions

  1. In a medium pot, bring the
    rice2 cups
    and 4 cups of water to a boil with a lid. Once it reaches a boil, reduce the heat to low and let simmer for about 40 minutes or until all of the liquid is absorbed and the
    rice2 cups
    is tender.
  2. While the
    rice2 cups
    cooks, combine all of the dry spices (
    thyme2 tsp
    ,
    oregano1 tsp
    ,
    crushed red pepper1 tsp
    ,
    paprika1 tsp
    ,
    peppercorns1 tsp
    and
    salt1 tsp
    ) in a small food processor, coffee grinder or blender. The goal is to grind the mixture into a powder. My small food processor wasn’t quite powerful enough to pulverize the spices but the dish still turned out great, whole
    peppercorns1 tsp
    and all.
  3. In a large skillet, melt the
    butter4 tbsp
    over medium heat. Add the minced
    garlic4 cloves
    and ground spice mixture. Stir and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Turn up the heat to medium/high. Add the
    shrimp450 g
    , stir and cook for 2-3 minutes more or until all of the flesh on the
    shrimp450 g
    has turned white and the tails have turned pink. Remove the
    shrimp450 g
    from the skillet and place them in a bowl as soon as they are cooked (this happens quickly) and save for later.
  5. Add the
    chicken broth½ cup
    and
    beer½ cup
    (or just 1 cup broth) to the skillet and let it simmer with the spices for another 2-3 minutes or until it has reduced by half. Add the
    shrimp450 g
    back in to warm them through. Place some
    rice2 cups
    in a bowl, top with 5-6
    shrimp450 g
    and spoon some of the sauce over top. Garnish with lemon and parsley if desired. Enjoy with an ice cold beverage!
