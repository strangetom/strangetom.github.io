MediterraneanBaked cod with lemon and garlic

Serves 5    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 12 mins

Best baked cod recipe out there! Prepared Mediterranean style with a few spices and a mixture of lemon juice, olive oil and lots of garlic. Bakes in 15 mins!

Source: www.themediterraneandish.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1½ lbs cod fillet, 4-6 pieces
  • ¼ cups fresh parsley leaf, chopped
  • 5 tbsps fresh lemon juice
  • 5 tbsps extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsps melted butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ¾ tsps sweet Spanish paprika
  • ¾ tsps ground cumin
  • ¾ tsps salt
  • ½ tsps black pepper
