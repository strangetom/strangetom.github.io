Spring vegetable tagliatelle with lemon sauce

This pasta dish, topped with the new season's spring veg, looks and tastes good enough for a casual supper with friends.

Servings: 4

Total: 30 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 450 g mixed spring vegetables, including green beans, asparagus, broad beans, peas.
  • 400 g tagliatelle
  • juice of 1 lemon, zest of half
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard, mustard
  • 1 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 3 tbsp snipped chives
  • shaved Parmesan, to serve

  1. Halve the green beans and cut the asparagus into three pieces on the diagonal. Cook the

    chives3 tbsp
    , then add the pasta mix, tossing everything together well. Divide among four shallow bowls and top each with a grinding of black pepper, a few shavings of cheese and the remaining
    chives3 tbsp
