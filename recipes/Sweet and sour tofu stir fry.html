Sweet and sour tofu stir fry

The sweet and sour stir fry has become a very well known westernized Cantonese dish, which is especially popular when ordering take-out. It is quick and very easy to make, so especially good after a long and busy day.

30 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free
Vegan

  • saturates 10 g
  • carbs 22 g
  • sugar 5 g

Ingredients

    Tofu
  • 450 g block extra firm tofu, cut into ½ inch cubes
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 30 g cornstarch
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
    • Sweet & sour sauce
  • 120 ml pineapple juice, from a can of pineapple chunks
  • 60 ml rice vinegar
  • 55 g tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp freshly grated ginger
    • Stir fry
  • 285 g rice, washed, drained
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 100 g canned pineapple chunks

    cubes and
    soy sauce2 tbsp

    cornstarch30 g

    , and

  2. Heat a large pan over medium-high heat, and add in the
    oil1 tbsp

    tofu450 g
    and cook the
    tofu450 g
    for 7-10 minutes, or until it's crispy and golden on all sides. Don't disturb the
    tofu450 g

  3. While the
    tofu450 g

  4. If choosing to enjoy this stir fry with
    rice285 g
    , now is the time to start cooking it according to the package instructions.
  5. When the
    tofu450 g
    is golden, transfer it to a bowl and set it aside.
  6. Returning the same large pan to the heat, add the
    oil1 tbsp
    ,

  7. Then add the
    bell pepper1
    and
    pineapple chunks100 g
    , and cook for another minute.
  8. Add the sweet & sour sauce to the veggies in the pan, along with the
    tofu450 g
    . Let it simmer for 3 minutes, or until the sauce thickens a bit.
  9. Remove it from the heat, and enjoy it on its own, or serve with
    rice285 g
    and garnish with green onions.
