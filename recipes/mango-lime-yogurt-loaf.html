Mango & lime yogurt loaf

Serves 10    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 1 hr

Give your baking a tropical spin with this zesty loaf cake

Source: www.sainsburysmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 100 g dried mango
  • finely grated zest and juice 2 limes, plus 1.5-2 tbsp juice for the icing (about 2 extra limes)
  • 175 g plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp fine sea salt
  • 250 g full-fat Greek-style yogurt
  • 125 ml sunflower oil, plus extra for greasing
  • 175 g caster sugar, plus 2 tbsp
  • 3 large eggs
  • 100 g icing sugar
