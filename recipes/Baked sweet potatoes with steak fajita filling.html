Baked sweet potatoes with steak fajita filling

Servings: 4

Total: 1 hr 10 mins

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, plus extra to rub on the potatoes
  • juice 1 lime
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 500 g lean beef steak, cut into medium-sized strips
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 peppers, a mixture of orange, yellow and red, thinly sliced
  • small handful coriander, chopped
  • 2 avocados, halved, stoned and peeled

    sweet potatoes4

    onions2
    and
    peppers4
    to the other bowl and mix to ensure everything is evenly coated. Cover both bowls in cling film and chill in the fridge until needed.

    steak500 g
    fajita mixture and a dollop of mashed avocado. Enjoy!
