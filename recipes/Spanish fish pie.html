Spanish fish pie

This Spanish twist on a classic fish pie has little chunks of chorizo and a crispy potato topping with saffron butter for a comforting weekend one pot.

60 mins
6 servings

  • kcal 486
  • fat 19 g
  • saturates 8 g
  • carbs 42 g
  • sugar 10 g
  • fibre 5 g
  • protein 34 g
  • salt 1.5 g

Ingredients

  • 700 ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2x 250 g pack skinless cod, cut into large chunks
  • 1x 180 g pack raw peeled king prawns
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1x 150 g pack diced chorizo
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 60 g plain flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • pinch of saffron
  • 800 g Maris Piper or King Edward potatoes, peeled
