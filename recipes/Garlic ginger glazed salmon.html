Garlic ginger glazed salmon

45 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 3 tbsp honey
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • ½ inch cube ginger, grated
  • 500 g salmon, skinned, de-boned

Instructions

  1. Combine all liquid ingredients in a sauce pan, add grated
    ginger½ inch cube
    and pressed
    garlic3-4 cloves
    . Stir, bring to a boil on medium-high heat and allow to simmer until the glaze is reduced and thickened, about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile sprinkle the fish with cracked black pepper on both sides. Heat the skillet with 1 tablespoon oil over high heat, add the
    salmon500 g
    and cook until golden on both sides, covering with lid in between. Depending on the
    salmon500 g
    thickness you might need to lower the heat and cook it longer in order for it to cook through in the middle.
  3. Transfer the
    salmon500 g
    fillet to a serving dish and drizzle the sauce over top. Eat hot, right away.
  4. Serve with rice or rice noodles as a side dish.
