Pepper and egg pizza

Servings: 4

Total: 27 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 150 g red and yellow peppers in oil
  • 8 tbsp passata
  • 4 small wheat-free pizza bases
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 125 g watercress, washed and stalks removed

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 7 and preheat two large baking sheets, big enough to hold two pizzas each.
  2. Drain the
    peppers150 g
    , reserving the oil. Chop into thin strips. Spoon 2 tbsp
    passata8 tbsp
    over each
    pizza base4 small
    and scatter the strips of the chopped pepper around the edges. Make a dip in the
    passata8 tbsp
    in the middle of each pizza and break an egg into it. Carefully slide the pizzas on to the preheated baking sheets. Place in the oven and cook for 12 minutes until the egg is thoroughly cooked.
  3. Top the pizzas with the
    watercress125 g
    , drizzle with a little of the reserved oil from the
    peppers150 g
    and serve.
