Tomato and rosemary pearl barley risotto

1 hr Serves 4 Vegetarian Italian

Step-by-step method for making Tomato and rosemary pearl barley risotto yourself.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 sticks celery, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 200 g pearl barley
  • 800 ml hot reduced-salt veg stock
  • 2x 390 g carton chopped tomatoes
  • Â½ tsp sugar
  • 5 sprigs rosemary, leaves only, finely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 25 g whole almonds
  • 125 g ricotta

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large, heavy-based pan. Add the onion and celery; cook gently for 8-10 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
  2. Stir in the barley; cook for 1 minute. Add 600 ml of the stock, tomatoes, sugar, rosemary and bay leaf; bring to the boil. Simmer for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the rest of the stock and cook for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, heat a dry frying pan, add the almonds and shake over a low heat until toasted. Cool slightly; chop.
  4. Season the risotto and serve in bowls with the ricotta and toasted almonds.
