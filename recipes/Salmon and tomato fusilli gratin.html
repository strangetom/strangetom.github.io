Salmon and tomato fusilli gratin

This speedy salmon pasta bake has a crunchy topping from Parmesan, parsley and breadcrumbs

30 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 565
  • fat 20.0 g
  • saturates 4.0 g
  • carbs 66.0 g
  • sugar 8.0 g
  • fibre 7.0 g
  • protein 26.0 g
  • salt 0.6 g

Ingredients

  • 1x 240 g pack salmon fillets
  • 300 g dried fusilli
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 large cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 600 g fresh tomatoes, deseeded and roughly chopped
  • 50 g pitted green olives in brine, sliced
  • 1x 30 g pack flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped
  • 30 g fresh white breadcrumbs
  • 30 g Italian-style hard cheese or Parmesan, finely grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 7.
  2. Place the
    salmon1x 240 g pack
    on a baking tray, season and roast in the oven for 8-10 minutes until cooked but still with a slightly pink centre. Cool slightly then flake into large chunks. Switch the oven to its grill setting.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a pan of salted water to the boil and cook the
    pasta300 g
    until al dente, drain and return to the pan.
  4. While the
    pasta300 g
    is cooking, heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a deep frying pan or shallow casserole, over a low heat. Add the
    onion1 small
    and
    garlic3 large cloves
    , and fry for 3-4 minutes before increasing the heat to high and adding the
    chopped30 g
    tomatoes600 g
    ,
    olives50 g
    and half the parsley. Cook for a further 5 minutes until the
    tomatoes600 g
    start to break down slightly. Add the
    pasta300 g
    and flaked
    salmon1x 240 g pack
    and mix until well combined. If the handles of your pan aren't ovenproof, tip the
    pasta300 g
    into a baking dish.
  5. Mix together the remaining parsley, the
    breadcrumbs30 g
    and grated cheese and sprinkle over the
    pasta300 g
    . Grill for 5 minutes until golden and slightly crisp.
