Dessert

15 mins

10 servings

Starbucks lemon loaf


Ingredients

  • 192 g plain flour
  • 128 g sugar
  • 5 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  • 8 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 5 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp lemon zest
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 256 g icing sugar
  • 4-5 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and lightly grease a 9×5-inch loaf dish with butter or non-stick spray.
  2. In a large bowl or mixer, beat eggs and sugar together, then add in Greek yogurt and vegetable oil. Whisk in lemon juice and lemon zest.
  3. In a medium bowl, toss together flour, baking powder and salt, then add them to the wet ingredients until just incorporated. Don’t over mix.
  4. Pour batter into loaf pan and smooth out the top, then place in oven and bake for 50 minutes, or until toothpick inserted in center comes out mostly clean. If necessary, cover loaf with aluminum foil during the last 5-10 minutes to prevent browning.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool.
  6. While loaf is baking, whisk together the icing sugar and 4 tablespoons lemon juice together to create glaze. Add 1-3 more teaspoons lemon juice to achieve desired thickness.
  7. Once loaf has cooled, extract it from baking dish and pour glaze over the top. Let glaze set and serve fresh or store, refrigerated, in an air-tight container.
