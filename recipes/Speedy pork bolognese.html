Speedy pork bolognese

The prep for this is so quick - just pop everything in and let the food processor do all the work. If you don't have a food processor, just chop everything finely. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta and stock cube.

25 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red apple, cored
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 500 g minced pork
  • 500 g passata
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1 tbsp sun-dried tomato puree
  • spaghetti or pasta
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped parsley
  • Parmesan to serve
