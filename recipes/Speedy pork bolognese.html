Speedy pork bolognese

  • Pork

  • 25 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

The prep for this is so quick - just pop everything in and let the food processor do all the work. If you don't have a food processor, just chop everything finely. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta and stock cube.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 sticks celery
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red apple, cored
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 500 g minced pork
  • 500 g packet of passata
  • 1 veg stock cube
  • 1 tbsp sun-dried tomato puree
  • spaghetti or pasta
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped parsley
  • Parmesan to serve

Instructions

  1. Put the onion, celery, carrot, apple and garlic in a food processor and blitz until it is evenly chopped.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok or large saucepan. Add the veggies and cook on a fairly high heat for 5 minutes. Keep stirring.
  3. Add the mice and cook until it is no longer pink,
  4. Add the passata, veg stock cube and tomato puree. Season well and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti or pasta. Drain and leave to one side until needed.
  6. Add the parsley to the bolognese and heat through for 30 seconds.
  7. Mix the pasta and sauce together. Grate the Parmesan on top to serve.
