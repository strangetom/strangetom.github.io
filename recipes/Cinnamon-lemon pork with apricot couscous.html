Cinnamon-lemon pork with apricot couscous

Step-by-step method for making Cinnamon-lemon pork with apricot couscous yourself.

40 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 423
  • fat 11.0 g
  • saturates 3.0 g
  • carbs 49.0 g
  • sugar 12.0 g
  • fibre 5.0 g
  • protein 30.0 g
  • salt 0.2 g

Ingredients

  • zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 350 g pork fillet, trimmed
  • 2 tbsp seed mix
  • 1 tsp vegetable oil
  • 200 g couscous
  • 100 g dried apricots, chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped mint, plus extra leaves to garnish
  • 4 tbsp Greek-style yogurt, to serve

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Mix the lemon zest and juice with the
    cinnamon½ tsp
    and seasoning. Drizzle half the mixture over the
    pork350 g
    , rub in well; set aside for 5 minutes.
  2. Toast the
    seeds2 tbsp
    in a dry frying pan until they pop; put in a bowl.
  3. Add the
    oil1 tsp
    to the frying pan; sear the
    pork350 g
    for 4 minutes, turning to brown it on all sides. Transfer to the oven (on a baking tray if your frying pan doesn't have an ovenproof handle); roast for 25 minutes until cooked. Let the
    pork350 g
    rest for 5 minutes then slice.
  4. Meanwhile, stir the
    couscous200 g
    and
    apricots100 g
    into the rest of the lemon-
    cinnamon½ tsp
    mixture in the bowl. Add a pinch of salt and 400 ml boiling water. Stir, cover the bowl with a plate; set aside for 5 minutes. Stir in the
    mint2 tbsp
    and most of the
    seeds2 tbsp
    . Serve with the
    pork350 g
    , with a dollop of
    yogurt4 tbsp
    . Scatter over the rest of the
    seeds2 tbsp
    and a few
    mint2 tbsp
    leaves. Lovely with green beans.
