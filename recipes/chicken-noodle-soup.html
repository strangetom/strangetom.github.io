Chicken noodle soup

Serves 2    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

Mary Cadogan's aromatic broth will warm you up on a winter's evening - it contains ginger, which is particularly good for colds, too.

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 900 ml chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 boneless skinless chicken breast, about 175g
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh root ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 50 g rice or wheat noodles
  • 2 tbsp sweetcorn, canned or frozen
  • 2-3 mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 2 spring onions, shredded
  • 2 tsp soy sauce, plus extra for serving
  • mint or basil leaves and a little shredded chilli (optional), to serve
