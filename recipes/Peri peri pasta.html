Peri peri pasta

Such an easy one. Don't know why peri peri and pasta isn't a bigger thing. It works a treat.

Servings: 4

Total: 1 hr 0 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 peppers
  • bag of sweet peppers
  • red chilli
  • red wine vinegar
  • oregano
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 lemon
  • garlic
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • bunch coriander
  • smoked paprika
  • 500 g tagliatelle

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Add two chopped red
    peppers2
    , a quartered
    red onion1
    and a halved
    lemon1
    (cut side down) to a tray. Drizzle with olive oil. Roast in oven for 40 minutes.
  3. Add roast vegetables to a blender with the pulp of the roast
    lemon1
    , a tablespoon and a half of red wine vinegar, 2 cloves of garlic, 1 red chilli, a heaped teaspoon of oregano, a heaped teaspoon of smoked paprika, salt, pepper, olive oil. Blitz until smooth.
  4. Dice up your
    chicken4
    and slice up your sweet
    peppers2
    . Add to a pan with a drizzle of olive oil. Cook on a low heat. Once the
    chicken4
    is white, add a heaped teaspoon of smoked paprika. Stir it in.
  5. Get your
    pasta500 g
    on (follow pack instructions).
  6. Pour in your peri peri sauce. Add a large handful of chopped
    coriander bunch
    . Stir it in.
  7. Drain
    pasta500 g
    , reserve some of the water. Add to the peri peri pan. Stir everything together. Serve in bowls while hot. If you have some cheddar, grate that on top!
