Spiced mushroom & lentil hotpot

A vegan winter warmer the whole family will love - this healthy potato-topped bake is low in calories and fat, high in fibre and 3 of your 5-a-day.

45 mins
4 servings

Vegan

  • kcal 312
  • fat 7 g
  • saturates 1 g
  • carbs 44 g
  • sugar 13 g
  • fibre 12 g
  • protein 12 g
  • salt 0.7 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 300 g mini Portobello mushrooms or chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1½ tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2x 400 g cans green lentils, drained and rinsed (drained weight 240g)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled and very thinly sliced
  • 1 large potato, very thinly sliced
  • 1 sprig thyme, leaves picked
