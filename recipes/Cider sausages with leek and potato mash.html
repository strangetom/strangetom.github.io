Cider sausages with leek and potato mash

25 mins Serves 4 Gluten Free

Excellent way of dressing up 'bangers and mash' and making it a bit more special. Gluten-free: make sure the sausages are gluten-free and use gluten-free flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 sausages
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 2 mugs cider
Mash
  • 5 potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 cm chunks
  • 50 g butter
  • 3 leeks, sliced

Instructions

  1. Simmer the
    potatoes5
    for 10 minutes. Drain and return to the pan. Mash and put the lid on to keep warm.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the
    butter50 g
    in a large frying pan and fry the
    leeks3
    until the begin to brown. Add to the mashed potato and mix together. Set to one side with the lid on until needed.
  3. Using the same frying pan, heat 1 tbsp
    olive oil1 tbsp
    . Take the skins off the
    sausages8
    and pinch each one into 4 'medallions'. Fry the
    sausages8
    and
    onion1
    until they are browned. Remove the sausges from the pan.
  4. Add the flour to the frying pan and mix. Add the
    cider2 mugs
    and stir. The sauce should thickens. Put the
    sausages8
    back in the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Season well with salt and pepper. Serve with the mash.
