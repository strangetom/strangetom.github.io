Za'atar chicken & new potato bake with tzatziki

Squidgy roast new potatoes, crispy chicken skin, big dollops of fresh tzatziki, all in one dish. Bish Bash Bosh.

1 hr 10 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless chicken thighs, skin on
  • 750 g new potatoes
  • 1 bulb garlic
  • fresh rosemary
  • za'atar
  • 1x 500 g pot Greek yoghurt
  • 1 cucumber
  • fresh mint
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6.
  2. Place the
    chicken8
    thighs on a baking dish, skin side up. Add the new
    potatoes750 g
    to the pan. Break up the bulb of
    garlic1 bulb
    and add the cloves, skin on, to the pan. Tear 10-20 rosemary leaves off a bunch of rosemary and throw them in.
  3. Add 2 headped teaspoons of za'atar to the pan. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Rub the spices and seasoning into the thighs and the
    potatoes750 g
    , making sure all the
    chicken8
    remain skin-side up.
  4. Place the pan in the hot oven for 1 hour, making sure you mix it around every 20 minutes or so to ensure even cooking.
  5. Tzatziki time. Add the
    yoghurt1x 500 g pot
    to a bowl. Grate a
    cucumber1
    , squeeze out the gratings to remove excess moisture, and add to the bowl. Add a handful of chopped mint, season with salt and pepper, squeeze in the juice of a
    lemon1
    and mix everything together.
  6. After 1 hour, the
    chicken8
    should be crispy and cooked through and the
    potatoes750 g
    golden. Remove the pan from the oven and dollop the tzatziki over everything. Serve hot!
