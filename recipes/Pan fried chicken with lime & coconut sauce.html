Pan fried chicken with lime & coconut sauce

30 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1½ mugs basmati rice
  • 1 tsp pilau seasoning
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 3 chicken breasts
    • Lime and cocunut sauce
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • juice of a lime
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 400 ml can coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 4 pak choi, sliced

Instructions

  1. Put the
    rice1½ mugs
    on the cook in 3 mugs of boiling water. Add the
    pilau seasoning1 tsp
    and simmer for 12 minutes. Just before you serve the
    rice1½ mugs
    , add the chopped
    coriander2 tbsp
    .
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the
    chicken breasts3
    . Season well. Cook on a fairly high heat for 2 minutes each side. Turn down to medium heat and cook for a further 4 minutes each side, with a lid on the pan. If the
    chicken breasts3
    are smaller, then cook for less time. Remove from the pan and set to one side until needed.
  3. To make the sauce, heat the
    toasted sesame oil1 tbsp
    in a small saucepan and gently fry the
    garlic1 clove
    and
    ginger2 tbsp
    . Add the rest of the sauce ingredients and simmer gently for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Heat the other tablespoon of
    toasted sesame oil1 tbsp
    in the frying pan and stir-fry the
    pak choi4
    for 1 minutes, or until it begins to wilt slights; it needs to be quite 'al dente'.
