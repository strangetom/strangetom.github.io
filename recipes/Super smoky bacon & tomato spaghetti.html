Super smoky bacon & tomato spaghetti

Serve your pasta with a budget-busting tomato and paprika sauce. It's a healthy choice to boot

5 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 500
  • fat 12 g
  • saturates 3 g
  • carbs 80 g
  • sugar 11 g
  • fibre 7 g
  • protein 20 g
  • salt 1.2 g

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 120 g smoked streaky bacon, sliced into matchsticks
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 2x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • grated Parmesan, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pan of water to the boil and cook the
    spaghetti400 g
    following pack instructions. Meanwhile, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large non-stick frying pan and cook the
    bacon120 g
    for 3-4 mins until just starting to crisp. Stir in the
    onion1
    and cook for another 3-4 mins, then add the
    garlic1 clove
    and smoked paprika, and cook for 1 min more.
  2. Pour in the
    chopped tomatoes2x 400 g can
    , bring to the boil and bubble for about 5 mins until thickened, stirring every so often to stop it catching on the bottom. Drain the pasta and toss with the sauce. Serve with Parmesan.
