Sweet & sour turkey

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300 g turkey strips
  • 2x 200 g packs mixed baby carrots, sweetcorn and mangetout
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 225 g beansprouts
  • finely grated zest and juice of small orange
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • cooked rice or noodles, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok or frying pan and fry the
    turkey300 g
    for 3 minutes, stirring until it is browned.
  2. Add the
    baby vegetables2x 200 g packs
    and
    pepper1
    and fry for 4 minutes. Stir in the beanspouts.
  3. Mix the orange zest and juice,
    soy sauce3 tbsp
    ,
    honey1 tsp
    ,
    cornflour2 tsp
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    together. Pour over the stir fry and let it bubble, stirring. When the sauce had thickened, serve with rice or noodles.
