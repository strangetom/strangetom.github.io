Sweet & sour turkey

  • Turkey

  • 25 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 300 g turkey strips
  • 2 x 200 g packs mixed baby carrots, sweetcorn and mangetout
  • 1 red pepper, deseeded and sliced
  • 225 g beansprouts
  • finely grated zest and juice of small orange
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp clear honey
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • cooked rice or noodles to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or frying pan and fry the turkey for 3 minutes, stirring until it is browned.
  2. Add the baby vegetables and pepper and fry for 4 minutes. Stir in the beanspouts.
  3. Mix the orange zest and juice, soy sauce, honey, cornflour and garlic together. Pour over the stir fry and let it bubble, stirring. When the sauce had thickened, serve with rice or noodles.
