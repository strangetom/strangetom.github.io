Sweet potato, bacon & orzo pasta salad

Servings: 4

Total: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 250 g orzo pasta
  • 2 mugs frozen peas
  • 200 g streaky bacon
  • 6 spring onions
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint
  • 100 g feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Mix the sweet potato with the
    olive oil2 tbsp
    , season with salt and pepper and place on a baking tray. Put in the oven for 25 minutes, or until tender and lightly browned.
  3. Meanwhile put the
    pasta250 g
    in boiling water and simmer for 3 minutes. Add
    peas2 mugs
    for the last 2 minutes. Drain and return to pan.
  4. Fry or grill
    bacon200 g
    until crispy. Cut into bite sized pieces.
  5. Mix everything except the
    feta100 g
    together and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Crumble the
    feta100 g
    on top.
