Spaghetti with pea and mint pesto

Serves 4     |     Prep 30 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

Mary Cadogan's aromatic broth will warm you up on a winter's evening - it contains ginger, which is particularly good for colds, too.

Source: BBC Good Food: Pasta & Noodle Dishes

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 250 g shelled fresh peas
  • 2 fat cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 50 g pine nuts, toasted
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, chopped into small chunks
  • good handful fresh mint leaves
  • 6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 350 g spaghetti
To serve
  • freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • extra virgin olive oil
