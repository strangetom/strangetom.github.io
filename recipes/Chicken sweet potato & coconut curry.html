Chicken, sweet potato & coconut curry

Serve this mildly spiced curry with rice noodles, basmati rice or chappati breads and introduce your kids to curry.

30 mins
3 servings

  • kcal 291
  • fat 14 g
  • saturates 10 g
  • carbs 24.5 g
  • sugar 0 g
  • fibre 3.6 g
  • protein 19 g
  • salt 0.57 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 tsp mild curry paste
  • 2 large boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 medium-sized sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-size pieces
  • 4 tbsp red split lentils
  • 300 ml chicken stock
  • 400 ml can coconut milk
  • 175 g frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a deep frying pan or wok, stir in the
    curry paste2 tsp
    and fry for 1 minute. Add the
    chicken2 large
    ,
    sweet potatoes2
    and
    lentils4 tbsp
    and stir to coat in the paste, then pour in the
    stock300 ml
    and
    coconut milk400 ml can
    . Bring to the boil, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Tip in the
    peas175 g
    , bring back to the boil and simmer for a further 4-5 minutes. Season to taste before serving.
