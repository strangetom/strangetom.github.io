Sweet potato, bacon & orzo pasta salad

30 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 250 g orzo pasta
  • 2 mugs frozen peas
  • 200 g streaky bacon
  • 6 spring onions
  • 1 tbsp fresh mint
  • 100 g feta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C
  2. Mix the sweet potato with the olive oil, season with salt and pepper and place on a baking tray. Put in the oven for 25 minutes
    , or until tender and lightly browned.
  3. Meanwhile put the pasta in boiling water and simmer for 3 minutes
    . Add peas for the last 2 minutes
    . Drain and return to pan.
  4. Fry or grill bacon until crispy. Cut into bite sized pieces.
  5. Mix everything except the feta together and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Crumble the feta on top.
