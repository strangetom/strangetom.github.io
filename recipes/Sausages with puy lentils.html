  • 1 large onion, chopped small
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed

  • 2 tablespoons groundnut or other flavourless oil
  • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
