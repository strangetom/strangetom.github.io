AsianSpicy bacon broccoli

Serves 2    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 5 mins

A little bacon goes a long way.

Source: www.chinghehuang.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tbsp groundnut oil
  • 2 whole red dried chillis, torn
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 inch fresh root ginger, peeled and finely grated
  • 180 g smoky bacon lardons, diced
  • 200 g Tenderstem broccoli, washed, sliced to 1 inch pieces on the angle
  • 1 large organic carrot, sliced in half lengthwise in the middle, half again, then 2 inch pieces on the angle
  • 2 tbsp Shaosing rice wine
  • 2 tbsp Chinkiang black rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp low sodium light soy sauce
