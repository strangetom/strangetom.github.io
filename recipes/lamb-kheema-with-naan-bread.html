IndianLamb kheema with naan bread

Serves 4    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

You may have heard of kheema before in Indian restaurants, where it is a type of naan bread. Kheema is basically a traditional Indian dish that contains lamb mince. Gluten-free: make sure the stock cube is gluten-free and serve with rice instead of naans.

Source: Nosh: Quick & Easy
