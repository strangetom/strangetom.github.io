Chicken

25 mins

4 servings

Sweet chilli chicken


Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1/2 x 190 ml bottle sweet chilli sauce
  • 4 tbsp mirin or white wine vinegar
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 0.5 mug water
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1.5 mugs basaltic the rice
  • 200 g mangetout, cut into thin strips
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 fat red chilli, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix together the sauce ingredients. Add the chicken strips and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, put the rice on to cook. Once it's cooked, put the mangetout on top and replace pan lid. The mangetout will cook in the heat from the rice.
  3. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok. Add the chicken but not the sauce. Fry on a medium heat until the chicken is cooked, around 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add the sauce and bring to the boil. Simmer for 1 minute. Add the chilli now.
  5. Serve with the rice and mangetout.
