Sweet chilli chicken

25 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • 4 tbsp soft brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 0.5x 190 ml bottle sweet chilli sauce
  • 4 tbsp mirin or white wine vinegar
  • juice of 2 limes
  • ½ mug water
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into thin strips
  • 1½ mugs basmati rice
  • 200 g mangetout, cut into thin strips
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 fat red chilli, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix together the sauce ingredients. Add the
    chicken3
    strips and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, put the
    rice1½ mugs
    on to cook. Once it's cooked, put the
    mangetout200 g
    on top and replace pan lid. The
    mangetout200 g
    will cook in the heat from the
    rice1½ mugs
    .
  3. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large frying pan or wok. Add the
    chicken3
    but not the sauce. Fry on a medium heat until the
    chicken3
    is cooked, around 4-5 minutes.
  4. Add the sauce and bring to the boil. Simmer for 1 minute. Add the
    chilli1
    now.
  5. Serve with the
    rice1½ mugs
    and
    mangetout200 g
    .
