One pot taco beef rice skillet

One Pot Taco Beef Rice Skillet - delicious spicy cheesy taco beef, all cooked in one pan with rice and vegetables and on the table in 30 minutes for a perfect family meal.

Servings: 4

Total: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 455 g extra lean beef mince
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 180 ml passata
  • 1 red pepper, diced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 175 g frozen sweetcorn
  • 180 g uncooked long-grain rice
  • 600 ml water or stock
  • 4 spring onions, diced
  • 4-6 tbsp quark
  • 120 g cheddar cheese, grated
For the taco seasoning
  • 1½ tsp cumin
  • 1½ tsp paprika
  • 1½ tsp chilli powder
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp cayenne pepper (can add more or less depending on your preferred spice level)

Instructions

  1. Add the
    ground beef455 g
    and
    onion1 medium
    to a skillet over medium high heat and fry until browned.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and stir until meat mix is all coated.
  3. Add the chopped
    red1
    and
    green1
    peppers,
    passata180 ml
    ,
    corn175 g
    and
    rice180 g
    and mix to combine
  4. Pour in the
    water600 ml
    , give a little stir, bring to boil and then reduce heat and simmer until liquid is almost absorbed.
  5. Turn off heat, and leave covered for 10-12 minutes (the steam trapped underneath will continue to cook the
    rice180 g
    to perfection).
  6. Stir in the
    quark4-6 tbsp
    and cheddar until melted.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped
    spring onions4
    .
