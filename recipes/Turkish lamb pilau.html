Turkish lamb pilau

30 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • handful pine nuts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, halved and sliced
  • 2 sticks cinnamon, broken in half
  • 500 g lean fillet or leg of lamb, cubed
  • 250 g basmati rice
  • 1 lamb or vegetable stock cube
  • 12 ready to eat dried apricots
  • handful mint leaves, roughly chopped

Instructions

  1. Dry fry the
    pine nutshandful
    in a large pan until lightly toasted, then tip on to a large plate.

    to the pan, then fry the
    onion1 large
    and
    cinnamon2 sticks
    together until they start to turn golden. Turn up the heat, add the
    lamb500 g
    and cook until the
    lamb500 g
    changes colour, then add the
    rice250 g
    and cook for 1 minute, stirring all the time.
  3. Add 500 ml boiling water, crumble in the
    stock cube1
    , add the
    apricots12
    and season to taste.
  4. Turn down the heat, cover, and simmer for 12 minutes until the
    rice250 g
    is tender and the stock has been absorbed.
  5. Toss in the
    pine nutshandful
    , add the
    minthandful
    and serve.
