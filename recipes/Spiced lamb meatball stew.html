Spiced lamb meatball stew

Enjoy spiced lamb meatballs with chickpeas and pomegranate seeds in a harissa sauce for an easy midweek meal. Freeze any leftovers for busy weekdays

Servings: 4

Total: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 12 lamb meatballs
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 large clvoes garlic, crushed
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp rose harrisa
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 200 ml low-salt stock (lamb or beef)
  • 2x 400 g can chickpeas
  • small bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • ½ small bunch mint, chopped
  • 50 g pomegranate seeds

  2. Tip the

    garlic2 large clvoes
    ,
    cumin2 tsp
    ,
    coriander1 tsp
    and
    cinnamon½ tsp
    , and cook for 1 min. Add the harissa,
    tomatoes400 g can
    and lamb
    stock200 ml
    , and bring to a simmer. Stir though the
    chickpeas2x 400 g can
    and
    meatballs12
    , season and simmer for 10-15 mins until slightly reduced and the
    meatballs12
    are cooked through.
  3. Stir through half the herbs, then scatter the rest on top along with the
    pomegranate seeds50 g
