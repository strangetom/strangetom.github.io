Mackerel in curried couscous salad

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Smoked mackerel has a strong, distinct flavour. You can buy it vacuum packed and cooked, so no need to have a strong fish smell in the kitchen. Unopened, it will keep in the fridge for ages, so very handy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp rogan josh curry paste
  • 2 mugs boiling water
  • 1 mug couscous
  • 50 g sultanas
  • 250 g smoked mackerel fillets, skinned and flaked
  • 4 spring onions
  • 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped coriander
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • juice of lemon

Instructions

  1. Put the curry paste in a bowl, add the boiling water and stir. Add the couscous and sultanas and cover. Leave for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients and gently stir. Season with salt and pepper.
