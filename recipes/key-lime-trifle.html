Key lime trifle

Serves 8     |     Prep 35 mins     |     Cook 0 mins

Madeira sponge makes the base of this zesty festive trifle

Source: www.sainsburysmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 275 g Madeira sponge, cut into 2 cm cubes
  • juice of 4 limes, reserve some zest to serve
  • 5 tbsp Malibu (or 3 tbsp white rum)
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1-2 Hobnob biscuits, bashed to coarse crumbs
For the lime custard
  • zest of 4 and juice of 8 limes
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 4 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1½ tbsp cornflour
  • 450 ml whipping cream
For the cream
  • 450 ml whipping cream
  • 1½ tbsp icing sugar
  • 2 tbsp Malibu (or ½ tbsp white rum)
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste or extract
