Easy chicken tikka masala with basmati rice

1 hr 30 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 30 g fresh ginger, peeled and crushed
  • 5 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
  • 2 small onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 700 g chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 100 ml water
  • 1x 400 g tin tomato soup
  • large handful of fresh coriander, chopped
  • 375 g basmati rice
  • 830 ml water
  • 2 tbsp ghee, melted

Instructions

  1. Put the
    oil5 tbsp
    into a large pan and place on a medium heat. Add the crushed
    ginger30 g
    and
    garlic5 cloves
    and cook for about 1 minute.
  2. Add the
    onions2 small
    ,
    salt1 tsp
    and
    tomato puree1 tbsp
    and cook for about 5 minutes, until the
    onions2 small
    have softened.
  3. Add the
    chicken breasts700 g
    ,
    garam masala1 tbsp
    and
    curry powder1 tbsp
    . Cook for 15 miuntes. Add the
    water100 ml
    now, as the chicken will be starting to stick slightly. Cook until all the moisture has evapourated
  4. Now add the
    tomato soup1x 400 g tin
    , bring to the boil and leave to simmer on a low to medium heat for 25 minutes.
  5. Take off the heat and add the chopped coriander.
  6. To cook the
    rice375 g
    , put it into a large non-stick pan with the
    water100 ml
    . Bring to the boil, then allow to simmer for about 10 minutes, until all the
    water100 ml
    has evapourated.
  7. Turn the heat down completely, place a lid on the pan and allow the
    rice375 g
    to steam gently for about 10 minutes.
  8. Once the
    rice375 g
    is cooked, use a fork to flow it up. Then add the melted
    ghee2 tbsp
    and stir through. Serve with the tikka masala.
