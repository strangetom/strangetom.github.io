Prawn, chorizo & basil linguine

The naughtiest surf'n'turf combo about. Salty, crispy chorizo with plump, fresh prawns.

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 250 g chorizo ring
  • dried chilli flakes
  • 165 g raw king prawns
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 500 g passata
  • 500 g dried linguine
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice the
    chorizo250 g
    into bite-sized pieces. Add to a frying pan over a medium heat and cook until crispy.
  2. Once crispy, add a pinch of dried chilli flakes and the raw
    prawns165 g
    . Cook the
    prawns165 g
    until they are pink, then add a chopped clove
    garlic1 clove
    . Cook for a further 30 seconds, and then pour in the
    passata500 g
    .
  3. Get the linguine on (follow the instructions on the packet).
  4. Meanwhile, bubble the sauce down until it is nice and thick. Then throw in a handful of chopped basil (keep some whole leaves to garnish) and season well with salt and pepper.
  5. Drain your
    pasta500 g
    and pour it into the sauce. Mix everything together and serve, with a drizzle of olive oil and the basil leaves scattered on top.
