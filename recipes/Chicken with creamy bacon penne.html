Chicken

10 mins

2 servings

Chicken with creamy bacon penne


	
					
Ingredients

					

						
    
							
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 
							
  • 2 chicken breasts
    • 
							
  • 100 g smoked lardons
    • 
							
  • 4 tbsp dry white wine
    • 
							
  • 100 g frozen petite poits
    • 
							
  • 5 tbsp double cream
    • 
							
  • 250 g penne
    • 
							

					

				
					
Instructions

					

						
    
							
  1. Cook the pasta then set to oneside until needed.  
    2. 
							
  2. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and add the chicken breasts and scatter with the lardons. Leave to cook over a high heat for 4 minutes.  
    3. 
							
  3. Turn the chicken over in the pan, give the lardons a stir, then pur in the white wine and let it bubble over a high heat until it has mostly evapourated.  
    4. 
							
  4. Add the peas, cream and pasta, then season and stir well.  
    5. 
							
  5. Cover than pan and cook for 4 minutes until the chicken is cooked all the way through.  
    6. 
							
  6. Serve immediately.
