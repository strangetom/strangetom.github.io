Beef strips with orange and ginger

Stir-frying is one of the best ways to cook with the minimum of fat. it also one of the quickest ways to cook, provided you choose tender meat.

20 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 235
  • fat 13.3 g
  • saturates 4.6 g
  • carbs 3.1 g
  • sugar 1.8 g
  • fibre 0.5 g
  • protein 25.9 g
  • salt 0.35 g

Ingredients

  • 450 g lean beef steak, cut into thin strips
  • grated rind and juice of 1 orange
  • 15 ml light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 2.5 cm piece fresh root ginger, finely chopped
  • 15 ml sunflower oil
  • 1 large carrot, cut into thin strips
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • noodle or rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Place the beef strips in a bowl and sprinkle over the orange rind and juice. Leave to marinate for up to 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the liquid from the meat and step aside, then mix the meat with the soy sauce,
    cornflour1 tsp
    and ginger, making sure the meat is evenly covered.
  3. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan and add the beef. Stir-fry for 1 minute until lightly coloured, then add the
    carrot1 large
    strips and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the
    spring onions2
    and reserved liquid, then cook, stirring, until boiling and thickened. Serve hot with noodles or rice.
