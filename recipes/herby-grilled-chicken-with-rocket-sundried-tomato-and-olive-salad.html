Herby grilled chicken with rocket, sundried tomato and olive salad

Serves 4    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 16 mins

Source: Herbs: Delicious Recipes and Growing Tips to Transform Your Food

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 30 ml olive oil
  • 1 tbsp chopped rosemary leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • grated zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 skinless chicken breasts
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
Mustard dressing
  • 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 120 ml sunflower or groundnut oil
Rocket, sundried tomato and olive salad
  • 140 g rocket leaves
  • 1 small handful basil leaves, purple if available
  • 16 black olives, pitted
  • 10 sundried tomatoes in oil, chopped
  • 55 g Parmesan cheese, shaved
