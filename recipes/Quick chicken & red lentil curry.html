Chicken

25 mins

1 servings

Quick chicken & red lentil curry


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp Korma curry paste
  • 6 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 mug water
  • 1/2 mug red lentils
  • 100 g fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • rice and yoghurt to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok. Add the onions and fry until they begin to soften.
  2. Add the chicken and fry until it is no longer pink.
  3. Add the curry paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes, water and lentils. Bring the the boil, then turn down and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Put the rice on to cook. Set to one side until needed.
  6. Stir the spinach into the curry and cook for 30 seconds.
  7. Serve with the rice and yoghurt.
