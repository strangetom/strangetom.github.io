Quick chicken & red lentil curry

25 mins
1 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp Korma curry paste
  • 6 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 mug water
  • ½ mug red lentils
  • 100 g fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • rice and yoghurt to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok. Add the onions and fry until they begin to soften.
  2. Add the
    chicken3
    and fry until it is no longer pink.
  3. Add the
    curry paste2 tbsp
    and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the
    tomatoes6 large
    ,
    water1 mug
    and
    lentils½ mug
    . Bring the the boil, then turn down and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Put the rice on to cook. Set to one side until needed.
  6. Stir the
    spinach100 g
    into the curry and cook for 30 seconds.
  7. Serve with the rice and yoghurt.
