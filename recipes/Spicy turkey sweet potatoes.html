Spicy turkey sweet potatoes

These are low fat, even with a soured cream topping, the secret is the turkey mince. The filling would be delicious with normal baked potatoes, but sweet ones offer better nutrition.

50 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 464
  • fat 10 g
  • saturates 4 g
  • carbs 58 g
  • sugar 35 g
  • fibre 10 g
  • protein 31 g
  • salt 0.9 g

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 500 g turkey thigh mince
  • 500 g passata
  • 4 tbsp barbecue or hoisin sauce
  • 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 tbsp soured cream or yogurt
  • 0.5 pack chives, finely snipped

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Prick the potatoes, place on a baking tray and bake for 45 minutes or until really soft.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a frying pan, add the
    onion1
    and cook gently for 8 minutes until softened. Stir in the garlic, then tip in the mince and stir to break up. Cook over a high heat until any liquid has evapourated and the mince is browned, about 10 minutes. Pour in the
    passata500 g
    , then fill the carton a quarter full of water and tip that in too. Add the barbecue sauce and paprika, then lower the heat and simmer gently for 15 minutes, add a little extra water if needed
  3. When the potatoes are soft, split them down the centre and spoon the mince over the top. Add a dollop of soured cream or yoghurt and a sprinkling of chives.
