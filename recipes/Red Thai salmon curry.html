Red Thai salmon curry

You can easily experiment 5 May 202with this curry to suit your mod. Swap the Thai red curry paste for green or yellow, and the salmon for white fish or prawns.

Servings: 4

Total: 2 hr 35 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 250 ml reduced fat coconut milk
  • 2x 250 g skinless salmon fillets, cut into chunks
  • 200 g trimmed green beans
  • plain rice, to serve
