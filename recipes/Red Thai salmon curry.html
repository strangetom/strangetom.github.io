Red Thai salmon curry

  • Seafood

  • 155 mins

  • 4 servings

You can easily experiment with this curry to suit your mod. Swap the Thai red curry paste for green or yellow, and the salmon for white fish or prawns.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp Thai red curry paste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 250 ml reduced fat coconut milk
  • 2x 250 g skinless salmon fillets, cut into chunks
  • 200 g trimmed green beans
  • plain rice, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the slow cooker if necessary. Warm the oil in a large pan, then add the curry paste. Stir in the onion, then cook gently for about 5 minutes until softened.
  2. Transfer the onion to the slow cooker pot with the coconut milk, salmon chunks and beans. Cook on low for 1.5-2 hours until the fish flakes easily and the beans are cooked but have a little crunch. Serve with plain rice.
