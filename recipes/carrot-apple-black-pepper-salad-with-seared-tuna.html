Carrot, apple & black pepper salad with seared tuna

Serves 4    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 25 mins

Source: Fire and Spice

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp black pepper, coarsely ground
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • 4 tuna steaks, 150 g each
For the salad
  • 50 g unsalted peanuts
  • 2 tomatoes, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 2 apples, quartered, cored, finely sliced and drizzled in lime juice to stop them discolouring
  • 4 large carrots, peeled and grated
  • 3 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 2 red chillies, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 1 handful mint leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 large handful coriander leaves, roughly chopped
For the dressing
  • juice of 2 limes
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
