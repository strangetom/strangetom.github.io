Harissa chicken with chickpea salad

25 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 313
  • fat 5 g
  • saturates 1 g
  • carbs 25 g
  • sugar 6 g
  • fibre 7 g
  • protein 44 g
  • salt 1.06 g

Ingredients

  • 250 g punnet cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 small red onion
  • 400 g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • small bunch parsley, roughly chopped
  • juice 1 lemon
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts, halved lengthways through the middle
  • 1 tbsp harissa paste
  • fat-free natural yogurt and warmed wholemeal pitta bread, to serve

    chicken2

  3. Divide the salad between two plates, top with the
    harissa1 tbsp
    chicken2
    and serve with a dollop of yogurt and warmed pitta bread.
