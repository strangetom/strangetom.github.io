Smoked haddock fishcakes

Servings: 3

Total: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 450 g peeled potatoes, cut into chunks
  • 2 eggs
  • 225 g skinless smoked haddock fillets
  • 4 tbsp skimmed milk
  • knob butter
  • 175 g frozen peas
  • 100 g white breadcrumbs
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • salad leaves, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Boil the
    potatoes450 g
    and
    eggs2
    in salted water for 10-12 minutes. Meanwhile put the
    fish225 g
    ,
    milk4 tbsp
    and
    butterknob
    in a pan, season, cover and simmer for 4-5 minutes. Strain and reserve the liquid. Flake the
    fish225 g
    .
  2. Drain the
    potatoes450 g
    and
    eggs2
    . Shell the
    eggs2
    and mash then with the
    potatoes450 g
    . Add the liquid, season and stir in the
    fish225 g
    and
    peas175 g
    . Shape into 6 caked. Press into the
    breadcrumbs100 g
    , coating evenly.
  3. Pour the
    oil1 tbsp
    into a roasting tin. Heat for 5 minutes in the oven. Add the fishcakes, coat in the
    oil1 tbsp
    and cook for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through. Serve with salad leaves.
