Mustard chicken & pancetta gratin

Quick and easy to make. You could exchange the pancetta for 8 rashers of chopped up steaky bacon if you like.

20 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 5 medium potatoes, cut into ½ cm slices
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 200 g pancetta lardons
  • 250 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 300 ml single cream
  • 2 tbsp thyme leaves
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • ½ mug grated Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Add the
    potatoes5 medium
    to a pan of boiling water and simmer for 5 minutes until tender. Remove from the pan and set to one side until needed.
  2. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a large frying pan, or wok, and fry the
    onion1
    and
    pancetta200 g
    until it begins to brown.
  3. Add the
    mushrooms250 g
    and
    chicken3
    and fry until the
    chicken3
    is cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour into a large casserole dish and arrange the cooked
    potatoes5 medium
    over the top.
  5. Preheat the grill.
  6. Mix together the
    cream300 ml
    ,
    thyme2 tbsp
    leaves and
    wholegrain mustard1 tbsp
    . Pour over the
    potatoes5 medium
    and sprinkle the
    Parmesan½ mug
    over the top.
  7. Place under the grill for 5-8 minutes, or until the cheese has begun to brown.
