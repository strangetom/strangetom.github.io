Spanish meatball & butter bean stew

50 mins Serves 3 Gluten Free

This hearty one-pot is full of Mediterranean flavour, with pork, red onion, peppers and smoked paprika - an impressive 4 of your 5 a day

Ingredients

  • 350 g lean pork mince
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 large red onion, chopped
  • 2 peppers, sliced, any colour will do
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp sweet smoked paprika
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 400 g can butter beans, drained
  • 2 tsp golden caster sugar
  • small bunch parsley, chopped
  • crusty bread, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Season the
    pork350 g
    , working the seasoning in with your hands, then shape into small meatballs. Heat the
    oil2 tsp
    in a large pan, add the meatballs and cook for 5 mins, until golden brown all over. Push to one side of the pan and add the
    onion1 large
    and
    peppers2
    . Cook for a further 5 mins, stirring now and then, until the veg has softened, then stir in the
    garlic3 cloves
    and
    paprika1 tbsp
    . Stir everything around in the pan for 1 min, then add the
    tomatoes2x 400 g cans
    . Cover with a lid and simmer for 10 mins.
  2. Uncover, stir in the
    beans400 g can
    , the
    sugar2 tsp
    and some seasoning, then simmer for a further 10 mins, uncovered. Just before serving, stir in the
    parsleysmall bunch
    . Serve with crusty bread for dunking, if you like.
