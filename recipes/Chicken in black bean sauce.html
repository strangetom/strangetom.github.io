Chicken in black bean sauce

Gluten-free: make sure the stock cube, soy and black bean sauce are gluten-free.

20 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 278
  • fat 9 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 25 g
  • sugar 3.3 g
  • fibre 1 g
  • protein 23.2 g
  • salt 0.97 g

Ingredients

  • 1.5 mugs basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 bunch spring onions, cut into small pieces
  • 6 mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 fat red chilli, chopped
  • 1/2 mug water + 1 chicken stock cube, crumbled
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 0.5x 195 g bottle black bean sauce

Instructions

  1. Put the
    rice1.5 mugs
    on to cook. Once cooked leave the lid on the pan and set to one side until needed.
  2. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large frying pan, or wok. Add the
    chicken3
    and fry until it is no longer pink.
  3. Add the
    spring onions1 bunch
    ,
    mushrooms6
    and
    chilli1
    and fry for 1 minute.
  4. Mix together the
    water1/2 mug
    , stock cube,
    cornflour1 tsp
    ,
    soy1 tbsp
    and
    black bean sauce0.5x 195 g bottle
    . Add to the pan and bring to the boil. The sauce should thicken.
  5. Serve with the
    rice1.5 mugs
    .
