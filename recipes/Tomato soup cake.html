Tomato soup cake

45 mins Serves 12

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups flour
  • 1½ cups sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1½ tsp all spice
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ¾ tsp ground cloves
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cups water
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 tub Betty Crocker Rich and Creamy vanilla frosting

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease 2 loaf pans,
  2. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, all spice, cinnamon, and ground cloves together in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add the oil and beat the mixture.
  4. Add eggs, water and tomato soup and beat again.
  5. Divide equally into the 2 loaf pans
  6. Bake at 180°C for 25-30 minutes
  7. Allow to cool, then frost
  8. Enjoy the deliciousness of your tomato soup cake!
