Butternut & bacon fusilli

This easy pasta dish with pancetta, butternut squash and spinach makes a healthy weeknight dinner - fresh rosemary adds extra flavour.

Servings: 4

Total: 1 hr 0 mins

Ingredients

  • 0.5x 160 g pack pancetta di cubetti
  • 1½ tbsp olive oil
  • 750 g butternut squash, deseeded and cut into 2 cm cubes
  • 2 sprigs rosemary, leaves finely chopped
  • ⅓ tsp chilli flakes
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 350 g fusilli bucati (or fusilli)
  • 100 g young leaf spinach, roughly chopped
  • grated parmesan, to serve

    into a large frying pan set over a medium heat and cook for 5-8 mins until really crisp. Remove using a slotted spoon, leaving the fat in the pan. Add the oil to the pan, along with the
    squash750 g
    ,
    rosemary2 sprigs
    ,
    chilli⅓ tsp
    and
    garlic3 cloves
    . Cover and cook for 25 mins, stirring now and then, until the
    squash750 g
    is tender. Season well, and gently crush some of the butternut
    squash750 g
    with the back of a spoon.
  2. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil 15 mins before the
    squash750 g
    is ready, and cook the
    pasta350 g
    following pack instructions. Drain, reserving the cooking water. Add 1-2 ladles of
    pasta350 g
    water to the
    squash750 g
    and let it bubble for a few mins. Tip in the drained
    pasta350 g
    and toss together. Stir through the
    spinach100 g
    , then divide between plates. Sprinkle over the
    pancetta0.5x 160 g pack
    , some grated Parmesan and a good grinding of black pepper.
