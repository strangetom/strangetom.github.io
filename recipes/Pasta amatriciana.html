Pork

20 mins

4 servings

Pasta amatriciana


Ingredients

  • 2 mug pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 200 g pancetta lardons
  • 4 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 fat red chilli
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1/2 mug water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped basil
  • 1 mug grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta. Once cooked, drain and return to the pan.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan and add the onion and garlic. Fry until they begin to soften.
  3. Add the pancetta lardons to the pan and fry until they begin to brown.
  4. Add the tomatoes, chilli, tomato puree, water and stock and bring to the boil. Turn down and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the pasta, basil and Parmesan and mix.
  6. Serve immediately.
