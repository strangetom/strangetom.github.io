Pasta amatriciana

Amatriciana is a classic Italian dish. It usually has bacon, tomato and basil, but here we have replaced the bacon with pancetta, which makes it a bit simper to make. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta and stock cube.

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 2 mugs pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 200 g pancetta lardons
  • 4 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • ½ fat red chilli
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • ½ mug water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 2 tbsp freshly chopped basil
  • 1 mug grated Parmesan cheese

  1. Cook the
    pasta2 mugs
    . Once cooked, drain and return to the pan.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan and add the
    onion1
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    . Fry until they begin to soften.
  3. Add the
    pancetta lardons200 g
    to the pan and fry until they begin to brown.
  4. Add the
    tomatoes4
    ,
    chilli½
    ,
    tomato puree2 tbsp
    ,
    water½ mug
    and
    stock1
    and bring to the boil. Turn down and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the
    pasta2 mugs
    ,
    basil2 tbsp
    and
    Parmesan1 mug
    and mix.
  6. Serve immediately.
