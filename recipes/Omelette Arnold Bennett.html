Omelette Arnold Bennett

Contriving to be creamy and fluffy at the same time, this smoked haddock souffle omelette is delicious. No wonder the author Arnold Bennett loved it so much.

Servings: 2

Total: 14 mins

Ingredients

  • 175 g smoked haddock fillet
  • 50 g butter, diced
  • 175 ml double cream
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 40 g mature cheddar cheese
  • ground black pepper
  • watercress, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Put the
    haddock175 g
    in a shallow pan with water to cover and poach over a medium heat for 8-10 minutes or until the fish flakes easily. Drain well.
  2. Remove the skin and any bones from the
    haddock175 g
    and discard. Carefully flake the fish using a fork.
  3. Melt half the
    butter50 g
    with 60 ml of the
    cream175 ml
    in a fairly small non-stick pan, then add the flaked fish and stir together gently. Cover the pan and remove from the heat. Preheat the grill.
  4. Mix the
    egg4
    yolks with 15 ml of the
    cream175 ml
    . Season with pepper, then stir into the fish. Mix the
    cheese40 g
    and the remaining
    cream175 ml
    . Whisk the
    egg4
    whites until stiff, then fold into the fish mixture. Heat the remaining
    butter50 g
    in an omelette pan, add the fish mixture and cook until browned underneath. Pour the
    cheese40 g
    mixture over and grill until bubbling. Garnish and serve.
