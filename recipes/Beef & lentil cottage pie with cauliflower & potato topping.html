Beef & lentil cottage pie with cauliflower & potato topping

Try a new topping for your comforting bake by adding cauliflower and a little cheddar cheese - there'll be some leftover for a second meal, too.

Servings: 4

Total: 1 hr 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 250 g minced beef
  • 1 large carrot, coarsely grated
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 200 g red lentils
  • 600 ml beef stock
  • 140 g frozen peas
  • 1¼ kg potatoes, cubed
  • 1 large cauliflower (about 400 g), cut into florets
  • 150 ml milk
  • 50 g butter
  • 100 g mature cheddar, grated

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180°C/Gas 4. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large pan and add the
    minced beef250 g
    . Cook for 5 mins until browned all over, then add the
    carrot1 large
    and cook for 2 mins more.
  2. Stir in the
    tomato purée1 tbsp
    and add seasoning. Cook for a few mins, then add the
    lentils200 g
    and
    stock600 ml
    . Simmer for 20 mins, then stir in the
    peas140 g
    .
  3. Meanwhile, bring a pan of water to the boil and add the
    potatoes1¼ kg
    . Simmer for 15 mins, then add the
    cauliflower1 large
    and simmer for a further 10 mins until the veg is tender.
  4. Drain, then return the veg to the pan for a few mins to dry out. add the
    milk150 ml
    ,
    butter50 g
    and seasoning, and mash together. Finally, add the
    cheese100 g
    , reserving a handful, and mix well.
  5. Spoon the mince into a large roasting dish, about 30 x 20 cm, and spoon the mash over the top. Sprinkle with
    cheese100 g
    and bake until golden, about 30 mins.
