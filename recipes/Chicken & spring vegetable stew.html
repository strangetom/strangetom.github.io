Chicken & spring vegetable stew

Servings: 2

Total: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts, skin on
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200 g baby new potatoes, scrubbed and thinly sliced
  • 500 ml chicken stock
  • 200 g pack mixed spring vegetables (broccoli, peas, broad beans and sliced courgette)
  • 2 tbsp creme fraiche
  • handful tarragon leaves, roughly chopped or 0.5 tsp dried tarragon

Instructions

  1. Fry the
    chicken2
    in the
    olive oil1 tbsp
    in a wide pan with a lid for 5 minutes on each side. Throw in the
    potatoes200 g
    and stir to coat.
  2. Pour over the
    chicken stock500 ml
    , cover and simmer for 10 minutes until the
    potatoes200 g
    are almost cooked through.
  3. Remove the lid and turn the heat to high. Boil the stock down until it just coats the bottom of the pan. Scatter the
    vegetables200 g
    into the pan, cover again and cook for about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the
    creme fraiche2 tbsp
    to make a creamy sauce, season with salt and pepper to taste, then add the
    tarragonhandful
    . Serve straight from the pan.
