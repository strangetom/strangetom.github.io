Millionaire shortbread

30 mins
12 servings

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (227 g) butter
  • 3 tbsp caster sugar
  • 5.5 oz (156 g) plain flour
  • Tin (397 ml) of condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 3 oz (100 g) brown sugar
  • 8 oz (227 g) chocolate

Instructions

  1. Line (7"x11") tray
  2. Mix 3.5 oz (100 g) butter and caster sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Add flour, make soft dough: turn onto board, draw together, knead lightly
  4. Press into tin, prick with fork
  5. Bake at 180°C until light brown (~15 minutes)
  6. Mark out piece sizes with knife and allow to cool
  7. Melt remaining butter (4.5 oz, 127 g) and brown sugar over low heat
  8. Add honey and condensed milk
  9. Stir until mixture thickens (~20-30 minutes)
  10. Cool slightly and poor over shortbread
  11. Place in fridge to set
  12. Melt chocolate and poor over caramel.
  13. Return to fridge until set
  14. Use knife dipped in hot water to mark out pieces then cut.
