Very veggie lentil bake

Servings: 4

Total: 50 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 110 g red lentils
  • 75 g white rice
  • 650 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 small leek or ½ large leek, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced fairly small
  • 5 medium mushrooms, diced fairly small
  • 1 small courgette, or ½ large courgette diced fairly small
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • black pepper
  • 90 g grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil the
    lentils110 g
    and
    rice75 g
    for 15-20 minutes in the
    vegetable stock650 ml
    , stirring occasionally. You'll need to stir constantly during the last 5 minutes or so, to ensure they don't stick to the bottom of the pan. Once they're cooked, don't drain the water, but instead continue stirring over the heat until enough water has boiled off for the mixture to resemble porridge.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a frying pan, and add the chopped
    leek1 small
    ,
    pepper1
    ,
    mushrooms5 medium
    and
    courgette1 small
    . Cook over a medium heat for 5-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are soft.
  3. Heat the oven to 190°C (Gas Mark 5 / 375°F).
  4. Combine the cooked
    lentils110 g
    and vegetables in a large mixing bowl, and add the
    paprika1 tsp
    ,
    cayenne pepper½ tsp
    , and plenty of black
    pepper1
    . Mix well.
  5. Transfer half of the mixture to a baking dish, and sprinkle over half of the grated
    cheese90 g
    . Add the remaining lentil mixture, smooth out the top, and finish with the remaining
    cheese90 g
    .
  6. Bake for around 25-30 minutes, until the
    cheese90 g
    topping is golden brown and crispy.
