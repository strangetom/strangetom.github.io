The best vegan chow mein

Serves 3     |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

To effortlessly serve up this dish that outshines takeout, the secret is having all your vegetables chopped and prepped. Once you begin cooking, this recipe comes together swiftly, and with everything ready, the entire meal flows seamlessly.

Source: www.pickuplimes.com

Ingredients

Sauce
  • 3 tbsps dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsps water
  • 2 tbsps toasted sesame oil
  • ½ vegetable bouillon cube
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp agave syrup
  • 1 tsp sriracha hot sauce
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
Chow Mein Base
  • ½ tbsps vegetable oil
  • 168 g mock chicken pieces, torn into shreds
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 150 g vegan chow mein noodles
  • ¼ napa cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 2 carrots, julienned
  • 1 head of bok choy, thinly sliced, stems and leaves separated
Optional Garnishes
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • sliced green onion
  • crispy chilli oil
