Deb Henley's chicken enchiladas verdes

20 mins
4-6 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 790 g tin of tomatillos
  • 2 serrano or other medium-hot green chillis
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • bunch of fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • 100 ml chicken stock
  • 8-12 soft corn tortillas
  • 325 cooked chicken, shredded
  • 150 ml double cream
  • 50 g Manchego cheese, grated
  • sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 4.
  2. Drain the tomatillos and set them aside in a bowl.
  3. Pop the chillis into a small pan and add 125 ml water. Bring to the boil and simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from the heat. Put the chillis and their cooking water, the tomatillos, garlic and
    onion1 small
    into a blender and blitz until smooth.
  4. Heat the tomatillo mixture (salsa verde) and 2 tbsp of chopped coriander for about 5 minutes over a medium heat. Add the
    chicken stock100 ml
    , season with salt and cook until it thickens up a little - about 4 minutes.
  5. Wrap the tortillas in a clean tea towl and steam them, preferably in a tiered steamer, over boiling water for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Put a good portion of the shredded chicken on to each tortilla, roll them up like fat cigars and lay them side by side in a baking dish. Pour over the salsa, then the cream and finely sprinkle with the grated cheese. Bake in the oven for about 30 minutes.
  7. Serve sprinkled with the rest of the chopped coriander.
