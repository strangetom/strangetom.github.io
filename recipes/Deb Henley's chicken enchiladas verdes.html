Deb Henley's chicken enchiladas verdes

20 mins Serves 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1x 790 g tin tomatillos
  • 2 serrano or other medium-hot green chillis
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • bunch fresh coriander, finely chopped
  • 100 ml chicken stock
  • 8-12 soft corn tortillas
  • 325 g cooked chicken, shredded
  • 150 ml double cream
  • 50 g Manchego cheese, grated
  • sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 4.
  2. Drain the
    tomatillos1x 790 g tin
    and set them aside in a bowl.
  3. Pop the
    chillis2
    into a small pan and add 125 ml water. Bring to the boil and simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from the heat. Put the
    chillis2
    and their cooking water, the
    tomatillos1x 790 g tin
    , garlic and
    onion1 small
    into a blender and blitz until smooth.
  4. Heat the tomatillo mixture (salsa verde) and 2 tbsp of chopped
    corianderbunch
    for about 5 minutes over a medium heat. Add the chicken
    stock100 ml
    , season with salt and cook until it thickens up a little - about 4 minutes.
  5. Wrap the
    tortillas8-12
    in a clean tea towl and steam them, preferably in a tiered steamer, over boiling water for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Put a good portion of the
    shredded chicken325 g
    on to each tortilla, roll them up like fat cigars and lay them side by side in a baking dish. Pour over the salsa, then the
    cream150 ml
    and finely sprinkle with the grated
    cheese50 g
    . Bake in the oven for about 30 minutes.
  7. Serve sprinkled with the rest of the chopped
    corianderbunch
    .
