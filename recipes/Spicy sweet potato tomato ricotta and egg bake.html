Spicy sweet potato, tomato, ricotta and egg bake

50 mins
6 servings

Gluten Free

  • fat 15 g
  • saturates 5 g
  • carbs 22 g
  • sugar 8 g
  • fibre 4 g
  • protein 14 g

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C, Gas 6. Cut the
    sweet potatoes600 g
    into small wedges, leaving the skin on. Put in a large ovenproof baking dish or roasting tin and toss with the
    cayenne pepper½ tsp
    until evenly coated. Add the
    oil2 tbsp
    and mix.
  2. Put in the oven and roast for 20-25 minutes until just tender, then add the
    cherry tomatoes1x 270 g pack
    and continue to roast for a further 10 minutes, until the potatoes are golden and the tomatoes have softened.
  3. Dot spoonfuls of the
    ricotta1x 250 g pot
    on top of the sweet potato wedges and tomatoes, then crack the
    eggs6 large
    over the top. Season generously with salt and pepper, return to the oven and bake for a further 10 minutes until the
    eggs6 large
    are set. Scatter over the
    chives1x 25 g pack
    and serve with crusty bread, if you like.
