Lamb chops with pancetta salad & yoghurt dressing

Serves 4     |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

Gluten-free: use gluten-free bread for the salad.

Source: Nosh: Quick & Easy

Ingredients

    /    
    /

Dressing
  • juice of a lemon
  • ½ mug Parmesan cheese
  • 4 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper
Salad
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 slices wholemeal bread
  • 100 g sliced pancetta (or streaky bacon)
  • 125 g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 roasted red peppers, roughly chopped (from a jar)
  • 0.5x 100 g bag salad
  • 8 small lamb chops
