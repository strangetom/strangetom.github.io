Italian meatballs

Servings: 3

Total: 40 mins

Ingredients

Tomato sauce
  • oil to fry
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 400 g tin tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • ½ mug water
  • salt and pepper
  • 250 g spaghetti
Meatballs
  • 250 g beef mince
  • ½ small onion, finely chopped
  • salt and pepper
  • oil to fry

Instructions

  1. To make the sauce, heat a little oil in a saucepan and fry the
    onion1
    and
    garlic1 clove
    for 2-3 minutes. Add the rest of the sauce ingredients and bring to the boil, then turn down and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Once cooked, blitz with a hand-held blender.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the
    beef250 g
    ,
    onion1
    and seasoning together with your hands and make as smooth as possible.
  4. Take a dessertspoonful of mixture and form into balls and set aside. The mixture should make about 16 meatballs.
  5. Put the
    spaghetti250 g
    on to boil.
  6. Heat about ½" of oil in the bottom of a frying pan. When the oil makes the meatballs sizzle (test one), add the meatballs to the pan. As they begin to brown, turn them so all sides become browned. Once cooked take from the pan and drain on some kitchen paper.
  7. Drain the
    spaghetti250 g
    .
  8. Add the meatballs and sauce to the
    spaghetti250 g
    and mix. Garnish with basil and serve.
