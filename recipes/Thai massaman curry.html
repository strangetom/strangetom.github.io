Thai massaman curry

  • Beef

  • 10 mins

  • 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 400 g beef, cut into thin strips
  • 0.5 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 200 g potatoes, cubed
  • 1 red onion, roughly chopped
  • Blue dragon thai massaman curry pack
  • Fish sauce
  • pinch of caster sugar
  • 0.5 mug rice

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok. Fry the beef on a high heat until cooked through. Whilst the beef is cooking, cook the rice.
  2. Reduce the heat to medium and add the curry paste. Gently cook for a minute to release the flavours and aromas.
  3. Add the coconut milk and bring to the boil.
  4. Add the potatoes and other dry ingredients and simmer for about 10 minutes until they are all infused with the flavours and cooked through.
  5. Serve with the rice.
