Chocolate chilli con carne

A rich, creamy one-pot chocolate chilli. Get the mob over, get the pot on and wait for the magic to happen.

Servings: 4

Total: 50 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 red peppers
  • 1 brown onion
  • 1 red chilli
  • 500 g beef mince
  • paprika
  • ground cumin
  • dried oregano
  • 1x 400 g tin kidney beans
  • sugar
  • 2x 400 g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 400 g basmati rice
  • dark (bittersweet) chocolate
  • fresh coriander
  • creme fraiche
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Finely chop your
    garlic2 cloves
    ,
    peppers2
    ,
    brown onion1
    and
    chilli1
  2. Add a splash of olive oil to a large frying pan and heat it over a medium heat until hot. Add the
    beef mince500 g
    and, once it has browned, remove it and add the sliced veg to the pan, along with 2 teaspoons of paprika, 2 teaspoons of cumin and 2 teaspoons of oregano. Stir it in.
  3. Once the onions have softened, reintroduce the
    beef mince500 g
    , along with the drained
    kidney beans1x 400 g tin
    and 1 heaped teaspoon of sugar. Mix everything together, and then pour in the
    chopped tomatoes2x 400 g tin
    . Bring the
    chilli1
    to the boil and then cook, covered, for 30 miuntes.
  4. Get the
    rice400 g
    on (following the instructions on the packet).
  5. After 30 minutes, remove the lid from the
    chilli1
    . It should be nice and thick. At this point, add 60 g of chopped dark chocolate along with a large pinch of salt and pepper. Stir the chocolate in. Once it has melted, stir in a large handful of chopped coriander.
  6. Serve the
    chilli1
    on top of your steaming hot
    rice400 g
    , with a nice dollop of creme fraiche on top. Enjoy!
