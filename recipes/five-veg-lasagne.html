ItalianFive-veg lasagne

Serves 4    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 40 mins

This vegetarian main is a great source of vitamins and iron and a simple way to get your 5-a-day.

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 aubergine, cut into small chunks
  • 150 g mushroom, chopped
  • 4 roasted red peppers, chopped
  • 700 g passata with onions and garlic
  • 8-10 lasagne sheets
  • 400 g frozen spinach, defrosted
  • 1x 250 g tub ricotta
  • 25 g grated Parmesan, grated (or vegetarian alternative)
  • 25 g pine nuts
