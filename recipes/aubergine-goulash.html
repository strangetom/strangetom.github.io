Aubergine goulash

1 hr 5 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian Hungarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 large aubergine, cut into 1 inch dice
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • Â¼ white cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 400 g good quality tinned tomatoes
  • 500 ml vegetable stock
  • 1x 400 g tin kidney beans, drained
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 70 g uncooked pasta
  • black pepper
  • sour cream, crusty bread, to serve (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a very large saucepan or casserole dish, and add the diced aubergine, sliced carrot, and thinly sliced cabbage. Cook over a medium heat for a few minutes, until slightly softened.
  2. Add the tinned tomatoes, vegetable stock, kidney beans, and smoked paprika, and mix well to combine. Add a lid to the pan, and leave to simmer gently for around 30 minutes
    , until the vegetables are soft.
  3. Add the uncooked pasta to the pan, and leave to cook for a further 15 minutes
    , or until the pasta is cooked to your liking. You can add an extra dash of water if you'd like the stew to be a bit more soupy. Season generously with black pepper, and serve with a dollop of sour cream (optional) and some crusty bread.
