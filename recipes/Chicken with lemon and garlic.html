Chicken with lemon and garlic

Chicken strips spiced with paprika make an unusual course for four, and only need to be cooked at the last moment. Serve them as a main course with fried potatoes.

8 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 139
  • fat 6.5 g
  • saturates 1 g
  • carbs 1.5 g
  • sugar 1.1 g
  • fibre 0.8 g
  • protein 18.6 g
  • salt 0.05 g

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless chicken breast fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • fresh flat leaf parsley, to garnish
  • lemon wedges, to serve

Instructions

  1. Remove the little fillet from the back of each breast portion. If the breast still looks fatter than a finger, bat it with a rolling pin to make in thinner. Slice all the chicken meat into strips.
  2. Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Stir fry the chicken strips with the
    shallot1
    ,
    garlic4 cloves
    and
    paprika1 tsp
    over a high heat for about 3 minutes until cooked through.
  3. Add the lemon juice and parsley and season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with lemon wedges, garnished with flat leaf parsley.
