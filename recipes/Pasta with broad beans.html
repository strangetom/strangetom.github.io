Pasta with broad beans

Meat eaters can tip in some crispy bacon as well. Alternatively, use asparagus or green beans instead of the broad beans.

30 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 713
  • fat 25 g
  • saturates 4 g
  • carbs 104 g
  • sugar ? g
  • fibre 12 g
  • protein 25 g
  • salt 0.2 g

Ingredients

  • 500 g short pasta shapes
  • 6 tbsp extra virgin olive oil, plus extra for sprinkling
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 0.5 tsp chilli flakes
  • 500 g fresh or frozen broad beans, thawed if frozen
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated vegetarian Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pan of salt boiling water until tender.
  2. At the same time, heat the oil in a large frying pan. Fry the garlic and
    chilli flakes0.5 tsp
    over a medium to high heat for 1 minute. Add the broad beans with 3 tbsp water and cook for 3-5 minutes until the beans are tender and the water has evaporated.
  3. Drain the pasta and tip into the broad beans with the parsley and Parmesan. Toss well and season. Serve sprinkled with olive oil and grated Parmesan.
