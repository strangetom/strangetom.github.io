Minty lime bars

Serves 16    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 45 mins

Buttery. Minty. Perfectly tangy. My Minty Lime Bars recipe is a refreshing, stress-free take on classic lemon squares using fragrant mint and rich lime curd. It is a simple and stress-free dessert recipe that requires only one bowl, a whisk, and less than 10 minutes to prepare!

Source: themindfulmeringue.com

Shortbread crust
  • ¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1-2 tbsps lime zest, finely grated
  • 2 tbsps fresh mint, finely chopped
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cups unsalted butter, softened
  • pinch of salt
Lemon curd
  • 1½ cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsps all-purpose flour
  • pinch of salt
  • ½ cups fresh lime juice (~4 limes)
  • ⅛ cups fresh lemon juice (~1 medium lemon)
  • 1-2 tbsps icing sugar, for decorating
