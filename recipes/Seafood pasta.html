Seafood pasta

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 l chicken stock
  • 300 g spaghetti, roughly broken
  • 240 g pack mixed frozen seafood, defrosted
  • freshly chopped parsley
  • 4 lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok or large frying pan, then cook the
    onion1
    and
    garlic1 clove
    over a medium heat for 5 minutes until soft.
  2. Add the
    paprika1 tsp
    ,
    tomatoes400 g can
    and
    stock1 l
    , then bring to the boil.
  3. Turn down the heat to a simmer, stir in the
    pasta300 g
    and cook for 7 minutes, stirring occassionally to stop the
    pasta300 g
    from sticking.
  4. Stir in the
    seafood240 g
    , cook for 3 minutes more until it's all heated through and the
    pasta300 g
    is cooked. Season to taste.
  5. Garnish with the parsley and serve with the
    lemon wedges4
    .
