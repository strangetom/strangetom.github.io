Seafood pasta

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 l chicken stock
  • 300 g spaghetti, roughly broken
  • 240 g pack mixed frozen seafood, defrosted
  • freshly chopped parsley
  • 4 lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan, then cook the onion and garlic over a medium heat for 5 minutes until soft.
  2. Add the paprika, tomatoes and stock, then bring to the boil.
  3. Turn down the heat to a simmer, stir in the pasta and cook for 7 minutes, stirring occassionally to stop the pasta from sticking.
  4. Stir in the seafood, cook for 3 minutes more until it's all heated through and the pasta is cooked. Season to taste.
  5. Garnish with the parsley and serve with the lemon wedges.
