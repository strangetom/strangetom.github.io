Jerk pork with mango and bean rice

Servings: 4

Total: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 500 g loin of pork
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 tbsp jerk seasoning
  • 150 g fine green beans
  • 250 g long-grain rice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 400 g can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 mango, coarsely grated
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest and juice only
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Brush the
    pork500 g
    with the
    oil2 tbsp
    , coat in the
    jerk seasoning2 tbsp
    and roast for 20 minutes, until cooked through.
  2. Cook the
    rice250 g
    according the packet instructions.
  3. Meanwhile cook the
    green beans150 g
    in a pan for boiling water of 2 minutes, then drain.
  4. Heat the remaining
    oil2 tbsp
    in a pan and stir-fry the
    onion1
    for 5 minutes until softened. Add the cooked
    green beans150 g
    and
    rice250 g
    ,
    oregano1 tsp
    ,
    kidney beans400 g can
    and
    mango1
    , and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until piping hot.
  5. Whisk together the
    lime1
    zest, juice and
    honey1 tbsp
    .
  6. Divide the
    rice250 g
    between 4 serving dishes, top with slices of cooked jerk
    pork500 g
    and drizzle with the
    lime1
    dressing.
