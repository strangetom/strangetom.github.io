Jerk pork with mango and bean rice

  • Pork

  • 30 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 500 g loin of pork
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 tbsp jerk seasoning
  • 150 g fine green beans
  • 250 g long-grain rice
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 400 g can red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 mango, coarsely grated
  • 1 lime, finely grated zest and juice only
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Brush the pork with the oil, coat in the jerk seasoning and roast for 20 minutes, until cooked through.
  2. Cook the rice according the packet instructions.
  3. Meanwhile cook the green beans in a pan for boiling water of 2 minutes, then drain.
  4. Heat the remaining oil in a pan and stir-fry the onion for 5 minutes until softened. Add the cooked green beans and rice, oregano, kidney beans and mango, and stir-fry for 5-6 minutes until piping hot.
  5. Whisk together the lime zest, juice and honey.
  6. Divide the rice between 4 serving dishes, top with slices of cooked jerk pork and drizzle with the lime dressing.
