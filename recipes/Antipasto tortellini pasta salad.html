Antipasto tortellini pasta salad

Everyone goes crazy over this Antipasto Tortellini Pasta Salad! Itâ€™s a flavor-packed potluck favorite, and leagues better than anything you can get from the grocery store.

28 mins
8 servings

Ingredients

  • 20 oz cheese tortellini
  • 1½ cups grape or cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 1½ cups mixed pitted olives
  • 5 oz chopped pepperoni
  • 5 oz chopped sopressata
  • 1 cup mozzarella pearls
  • 5 oz chopped sharp provolone cheese
  • ¾ cup sliced roasted red peppers
  • ¼ red onion, diced
  • 8 whole pepperoncini peppers, optional
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, sliced into thin ribbons
  • For the dressing
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • kosher salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the
    tortellini20 oz
    in salted water according to package instructions, until al dente. Strain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  2. Add the
    tortellini20 oz
    and remaining pasta salad ingredients to a large bowl. Set aside.
  3. In a large jar or blender, combine the dressing ingredients and shake or blend until well-combined. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to evenly distribute. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
