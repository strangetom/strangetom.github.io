Moroccan spiced fish with ginger mash

Spice up white fish fillets with a flavoured butter and serve with fluffy sweet potato mash.

30 mins
2 servings

Gluten Free

  • kcal 445
  • fat 7 g
  • saturates 3 g
  • carbs 65 g
  • sugar 17 g
  • fibre 0 g
  • protein 36 g
  • salt 0.67 g

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potato, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 2 tsp butter, softened
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 0.5 -1 tsp harissa
  • zest 1 lemon
  • small handful coriander, most chopped, rest left whole
  • fingertip-size piece fresh root ginger, finely grated
  • 2 skinless white fish fillets

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 180°C/Gas 6. Cook the sweet potatoes in boiling, salted water for about 10 mins or until just tender when pierced with a knife.
  2. Meanwhile, mix together the butter with the garlic, harissa, lemon zest, chopped coriander and some seasoning. When the potatoes are ready, drain thoroughly, mash with the ginger and seasoning, then keep warm.
  3. Place the fish in a roasting tin, season, then spread half the flavoured butter over each fillet. Roast for about 8 mins until just cooked through. Serve with the ginger mash and some green veg.
