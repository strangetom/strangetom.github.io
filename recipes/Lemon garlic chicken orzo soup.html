Lemon garlic chicken orzo soup

1 hr 5 mins Serves 6

Inspired by Greek “Avgolemono”, this Lemon Garlic Chicken Orzo Soup recipe is thickened with a mixture of lemon juice, pureed garlic, and egg to create a flavor-packed, citrusy, velvety soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 20 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • ⅔ cup dry orzo
  • 3 eggs
  • Juice of 1 large lemon, plus lemon slices to garnish
  • fresh chopped dill
  • crusty bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. Season the
    chicken1 lb
    all over with salt and pepper. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a pot over medium-high heat. Add the
    chicken1 lb
    to the pot and cook until deeply golden browned, flipping once halfway through, about 15 minutes total. Transfer the
    chicken1 lb
    to a cutting board and let cool.
  2. Reduce the heat to low. Add the
    garlic20 cloves
    and shake the pan to gently cook for 3 minutes. Don't let the
    garlic20 cloves
    brown, it should just become fragrant and soften slightly.
  3. Pour in the
    chicken stock6 cups
    . Let simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Remove the
    garlic20 cloves
    from the
    chicken stock6 cups
    and add to the bowl of a blender or food processor along with the
    eggs3
    and lemon juice. Process to combine. Continue to run the blender or food processor while slowly streaming in one cup of the hot stock until fully combined and smooth. Set aside.
  5. Bring the
    chicken stock6 cups
    in the pot to a boil. Add in the orzo, stir, and cook for 7 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
  6. Add the egg mixture to the soup and continue to cook over low heat until thickened, about 3 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, shred the
    chicken1 lb
    . Add the shredded
    chicken1 lb
    back to the soup. Season with dill, salt, and pepper, to taste. Garnish with additional dill and lemon slices, if desired, and serve with crusty bread.
