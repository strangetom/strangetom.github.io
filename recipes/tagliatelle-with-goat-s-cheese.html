Tagliatelle with goat's cheese

Serves 2    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

Mushrooms add a deliciously nutty flavour and juicy texture to this veggie-friendly pasta.

Source: BBC Good Food: Pasta & Noodle Dishes

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 175 g tagliatelle
  • 25 g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 100 g firm goat's cheese
  • vegetarian Parmesan, freshly shaved, to serve
