Spaghetti carbonara

20 mins
3 servings

Ingredients

  • 250 g spaghetti
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 200 g pack pancetta lardons
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • a little freshly grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil

    spaghetti250 g
