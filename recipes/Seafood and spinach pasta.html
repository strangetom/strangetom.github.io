Seafood and spinach pasta

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 3 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 mug fusilli
  • 6 pieces frozen spinach, defrosted and drained of most of the water
  • 1 mug grated cheddar
  • 1/2 mug grated cheese for sprinkling over the top
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 1/2 mugs milk
  • 1" cube butter
  • 1/4 paprika
  • 2 small pices of salmon steak, cut into chunks
  • 10 large prawns
  • 2 spring onions
  • 4 mushrooms, sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Grease an oven proof dish or individual dishes.
  2. Boil water in a saucepan for the pasta. Once boiling, cook the pasta, drain, then stir in the spinach. Set aside in a bowl until needed.
  3. Put one mug of grated cheese into a saucepan and add the flour. Mix evenly. Add the milk and the butter and heat gently. Stir all the time and the sauce will thicken. Add the paprika and stir.
  4. Put the salmon, prawns, chopped spring onions and mushrooms into the cheese sauce and stir. Add the cooked pasta and spinach. Pour into the casserole dish or individual dishes.
  5. Sprinkle the 1/2 mug of cheese on top.
  6. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes. The top should be browned.
