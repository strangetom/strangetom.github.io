Mushroom stroganoff

This creamy mixed mushroom sauce taste great and is ideal for a dinner party. Serve it with toasted buckwheat, brown rice or a mixture of wild rices.

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 25 g butter
  • 900 g mixed mushrooms, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 350 g white long grain rice
  • 350 ml white wine sauce
  • 250 ml sour cream
  • chopped chives, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Melt the
    butter25 g
    a large, heavy pan and add the
    mushrooms900 g
    . Cook over a medium heat until the
    mushrooms900 g
    give up their liquid. Continue cooking until they are tender and beginning to brown
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pan of lightly salted water to the boil. Add the
    rice350 g
    , partially cover the pan and cook over a medium heat for 13-15 minutes until the
    rice350 g
    is just tender.
  3. Add the
    white wine sauce350 ml
    to the cooked
    mushrooms900 g
    in the pan and bring to the boil, stirring. Stir in the
    sour cream250 ml
    and season to taste. Drain the
    rice350 g
    will, spoon on to warm plates, top with the sauce and garnish with chives.
