Tuna burgers with cucumber salsa

Serves 2     |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

Transform a tin of tuna into delicious fishcakes for midweek

Source: www.sainsburysmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2½ burger buns
  • 1x 160 g tin tuna in spring water, drained
  • zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 3 tbsp coriander
  • 3 tsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp sriracha
  • 3 tbsp light mayonnaise
  • ½ cucumber
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • A handful of iceberg lettuce, shredded
