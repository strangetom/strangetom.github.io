Black bean chilli

Serves 4    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 25 mins

Source: BBC Good Food: Low-fat Feasts

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely choppped
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 tbsp sweet pimenton or mild chilli powder
  • 3 tbsp ground cumin
  • 3 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2x 400 g cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • boiled long grain rice, plus some, or one, of the following toppings, to serve: crumbled feta, chopped spring onions, sliced radishes, avocado chunks
