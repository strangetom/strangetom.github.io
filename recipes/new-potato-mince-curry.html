New potato & mince curry

Source: BBC Good Food: Low-fat Feasts

Ingredients

    /

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp coarsely grated ginger
  • 1 red chilli, deseed and finely sliced
  • 2 tsp each of ground cumin and coriander
  • 1 tbsp korma curry paste
  • 500 g new potatoes, halved
  • 100 g spinach leaves
  • 150 g 0% fat Greek yogurt
  • chapattis or naan bread, to serve
