One pot veggie pasta

Serves 4     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

This one pot veggie pasta is an easy, satisfying, and quick dinner. Use whatever vegetables you have on hand and make it your own!

Source: www.budgetbytes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • ½ lb rotini (not cooked)
  • ½ tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 24 oz pasta sauce
  • 4 oz mozzarella, shredded (optional)
