Samosa pie

  • Lamb

  • 35 mins

  • 4 servings

Cover spiced lamb mince, stewed potato and peas with crispy filo pastry for a low-fat, Indian-inspired dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 500g lamb mince
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 large sweet potato (about 300g), peeled and grated
  • 100g frozen peas
  • handful coriander, roughly chopped
  • juice 0.5 lemon
  • 3-4 sheets filo pastry
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 160°C/Gas 4. Heat 1 tbsp of the oil in a frying pan. Cook the onion and mince for about 5 mins until the meat is browned. Stir in the garlic, curry powder, sweet potato and 300ml water. Cook for 5-8 mins until the potato has softened. Stir in the peas, coriander and a squeeze of lemon juice, then season.
  2. Spoon the mixture into a baking dish. Brush the sheets of filo with the remaining oil and scrunch over the top of the mince. Sprinkle with cumin seeds and bake for 10-15 mins or until the top is crisp.
