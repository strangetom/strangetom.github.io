Samosa pie

Cover spiced lamb mince, stewed potato and peas with crispy filo pastry for a low-fat, Indian-inspired dinner.

35 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 308
  • fat 9 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 43 g
  • sugar 14 g
  • fibre 8 g
  • protein 9 g
  • salt 0.4 g

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 500 g lamb mince
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 large sweet potato (about 300g), peeled and grated
  • 100 g frozen peas
  • handful coriander, roughly chopped
  • juice 0.5 lemon
  • 3-4 sheets filo pastry
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

    peas100 g
    ,

  2. Spoon the mixture into a baking dish. Brush the sheets of
    filo3-4 sheets
    with the remaining
    oil2-3 tbsp
    and scrunch over the top of the
    mince500 g
    . Sprinkle with
    cumin seeds1 tsp
    and bake for 10-15 mins or until the top is crisp.
