AsianSpicy-sweet sambal pork noodles

Serves 6    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 55 mins

These weeknight noodles are inspired by the silky richness of meaty tomato sauce and the fiery, tangy-sweet flavors of pad kee mao (a.k.a. drunken noodles). It's full-on explosive flavor in under an hour.

Source: www.bonappetit.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 kg ground pork, divided
  • 2 inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin matchsticks or finely chopped
  • 8 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 sprigs basil, plus more for serving
  • ⅓ cup hot chilli paste (such as sambal oelek)
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup unsweetened rice vinegar
  • 750 g fresh ramen noodles or 450 g dried spaghetti
  • kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
