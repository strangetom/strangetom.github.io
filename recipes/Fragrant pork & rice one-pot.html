Fragrant pork & rice one-pot

Deliciously spiced pork meatballs and rice are simmered together in this stew, saving on washing-up

Servings: 4

Total: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 4-6 good quality sausages
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 tsp ground coriander and cumin
  • 140 g long grain rice
  • 850 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tin tomatoes
  • coriander leaves to garnish

Instructions

  1. Split the sausage skins, squeeze out the meat, then roll it into small meatballs about the size of a large olive. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large non-stick saucepan, then brown the meatballs well on all sides until cooked – you might need to do this in batches. Set the meatballs aside.
  2. Add the
    onion½
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    to the pan. Soften for 5 mins, stir in the spices and
    rice140 g
    , then cook for another min. Pour in the
    stock850 ml
    and
    tomatoes1 tin
    .
  3. Bring to a simmer, scraping up any sausagey bits from the bottom of the pan. Simmer for 10 mins until the
    rice140 g
    is just cooked, then stir in the meatballs with some seasoning.
