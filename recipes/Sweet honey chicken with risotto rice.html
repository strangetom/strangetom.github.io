Sweet honey chicken with risotto rice

35 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

Marinade
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp fresh grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 dessertspoon dried chives
  • 2 chicken breasts
Risotto rice
