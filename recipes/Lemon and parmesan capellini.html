Lemon and parmesan capellini

In this recipe, cream is thickened with parmesan and flavoured with lemon to make a superb sauce for pasta.

17 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 610
  • fat 30.5 g
  • saturates 18.4 g
  • carbs 67 g
  • sugar 4.8 g
  • fibre 3.3 g
  • protein 21.1 g
  • salt 0.57 g

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh herbs
  • 0.5 granary baguette
  • 225 g dried or fresh capellini
  • 250 ml single cream
  • 75 g parmesan cheese, grated
  • finely grated rind of 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200/Gas 6. Put the
    butter50 g
    in a bowl and beat with the
    garlic1 clove
    and
    herbs2 tbsp
  2. Cut the granary
    baguette0.5
    into thick slices and spread them thickly with the flavoured
    butter50 g
    .
  3. Reassemble the
    baguette0.5
    . The
    garlic1 clove
    and herb
    butter50 g
    will help hold in together. Wrap in foil, support on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, cook the
    pasta225 g
    in a large pan of lightly salted boiling water. Dried
    pasta225 g
    will take 10-12 minutes, fresh about 3 minutes.
  5. Pour the
    cream250 ml
    into another pan and bring to the boil. Stir in the
    parmesan75 g
    and lemon rind. The sauce should thicken in 30 seconds or so.
  6. Drain the
    pasta225 g
    , return in to the pan and toss with the sauce. Season to taste and spinkle with a little chopped fresh parsley and more grated lemon rind. Serve with the hot herb bread.
