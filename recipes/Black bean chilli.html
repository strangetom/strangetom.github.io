Black bean chilli

40 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely choppped
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 tbsp sweet pimenton or mild chilli powder
  • 3 tbsp ground cumin
  • 3 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2x 400 g cans black beans, drained and rinsed
  • boiled long grain rice, plus some of the following toppings: crumbled feta, chopped spring onions, sliced radishes, avocado chunks

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    and fry the
    garlic4 cloves
    and
    onions2 large
    for 5 minutes until almost softened. Add the pimenton or
    chilli powder3 tbsp
    and
    cumin3 tbsp
    , cook for a few minutes, then add the
    vinegar3 tbsp
    ,
    sugar2 tbsp
    ,
    tomatoes2x 400 g cans
    and some seasoning. Cook for 10 minutes.
  2. Pour in the
    black beans2x 400 g cans
    and cook for another 10 minutes. Serve with rice and the accompaniments of your choice.
