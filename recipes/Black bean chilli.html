Veggie

40 mins

4 servings

Black bean chilli


	
					
Ingredients

					

						
    
							
  • 2 tbsp olive or rapeseed oil
    • 
							
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely choppped
    • 
							
  • 2 large onions, chopped
    • 
							
  • 3 tbsp sweet pimenton or mild chilli powder
    • 
							
  • 3 tbsp ground cumin
    • 
							
  • 3 tbsp cider vinegar
    • 
							
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
    • 
							
  • 2 x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
    • 
							
  • 2 x 400 g cans black beans, drained and rinsed
    • 
							
  • boiled long grain rice plus some of the following toppings: crumbled feta, chopped spring onions, sliced radishes, avocado chunks
    • 
							

					

				
					
Instructions

					

						
    
							
  1. In a large pan, heat the oil and fry the garlic and onions for 5 minutes until almost softened. Add the pimenton or chilli powder and cumin, cook for a few minutes, then add the vinegar, sugar, tomatoes and some seasoning. Cook for 10 minutes.  
    2. 
							
  2. Pour in the black beans and cook for another 10 minutes. Serve with rice and the accompaniments of your choice.
    3.
