Tagliatelle with goat's cheese

20 mins Serves 2 Vegetarian

Mushrooms add a deliciously nutty flavour and juicy texture to this veggie-friendly pasta.

Ingredients

  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 175 g tagliatelle
  • 25 g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling
  • 100 g firm goat's cheese
  • freshly shaved vegetarian Parmesan, to serve

Instructions

  1. Slice the
    mushrooms250 g
    and finely chop the
    onion1 small
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    . Cook the
    pasta175 g
    in plenty of salted boiling water according to the packet instructions.
  2. Heat the
    butter25 g
    and
    oil1 tbsp
    in a frying pan until the
    butter25 g
    has melted. Add the
    onion1 small
    and cook until golden, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in the
    garlic2 cloves
    and
    mushrooms250 g
    and cook, stirring, until the
    mushrooms250 g
    are golden brown.
  3. Drain the
    pasta175 g
    reserving 4 tablespoons of the water. Return the
    pasta175 g
    to its pan with the water and stir in the
    mushrooms250 g
    mixture. Roughly break the
    goat's cheese100 g
    into pieces and gently stir it into the
    pasta175 g
    so it starts to melt. Serve sprinkled with black pepper, a drizzle of olive
    oil1 tbsp
    and a few shavings of Parmesan.
