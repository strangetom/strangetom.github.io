Chicken in black bean sauce

Serves 4     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Gluten-free: make sure the stock cube, soy and black bean sauce are gluten-free.

Source: Nosh: Quick & Easy

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1½ mugs basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1 bunch spring onions, cut into small pieces
  • 6 mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 fat red chilli, chopped
  • ½ mug water + 1 chicken stock cube, crumbled
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 0.5x 195 g bottle black bean sauce
