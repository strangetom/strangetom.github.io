Beef & salsa burgers

  • Beef

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 300 g lean minced beef
  • 50 g wholemeal breadcrumbs
  • 50 g carrot grated
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • small handful chopped parsley leaves
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 wholemeal burger buns
  • handful salad leaves
  • tomato salsa to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the grill to medium. In a large bowl, mix together the first six ingredients, then season well. Shape the mixture into four bugers and put on a baking sheet.
  2. Grill the burgers for 3-4 minutes on each side, until cooked through, then keep them warm. Split each burger bun in half and put under the grill, cut side up and lightly toast for 1 minute.
  3. Serve each burger in a bun with some salad leaves and top each burger with a good dollop of tomato salsa.
