Creamy tomato orzo

Serves 4    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

This simple and speedy recipe for creamy orzo uses fresh cherry tomatoes to create a flavorful base, and is topped off with crunchy roasted pine nuts, making it the perfect meal for busy days.

Source: www.pickuplimes.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 450 g cherry tomatoes
  • 500 ml water
  • 170 g dry orzo pasta
  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube
  • ½ tbsps Italian seasoning
  • ⅛ tsps ground black pepper
  • 100 g frozen green peas
  • ¾ cups fresh basil, torn
Toppings
  • 1 tbsp roasted pine nuts
  • ¼ tsps chili flakes (optional)
