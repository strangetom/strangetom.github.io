Pork patties with chilli dipping sauce

40 mins Serves 3-4

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • Â½ mug water
  • 1 fat red chilli, chopped
  • 1 spring onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 mug rice
  • 1 tsp pilau rice seasoning
  • 1 cucumber, sliced diagonally
Patties
  • 500 g pork mince
  • 2 pork sausages, skins removed
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped finely
  • zest of lemon
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp oil, to fry

Instructions

  1. In a small saucepan, mix the cornflour with the water. Add the rest of the dipping sauce ingredients and bring to the boil, stirring frequently. Turn down the heat and simmer for 1-2 minutes
    . Leave to cool.
  2. Make the pilau rice.
  3. Mix together the patty ingredients, use your hand to create a smooth paste. Form into small patties, making them quite flat so they are easier to cook. The mixture should make about 12.
  4. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Once the oil is hot, add the patties. Cook on a medium/high heat for 2-3 minutes
     each side.
  5. Serve with the rice, cucumber and dipping sauce.
