Easy beef burritos

45 mins Serves 4

Make these easy beef burritos for a simple midweek supper. The meat filling is also great stuffed into a crispy taco, or as an easy chilli con carne with rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, very finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • small pinch cayenne pepper, more if you want it spicier
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 500 g beef mince
  • pinch golden caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp wine vinegar or cider vinegar
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 400 g can black beans or kidney beans, with the can water
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas
  • 500 g cooked rice
  • a selection of sliced avocado or guacamole, chopped tomatoes, soured cream, shredded lettuce, sliced red onion, grated cheddar, sliced red chilli and lime halves, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a large pan - a casserole is ideal. Fry the onions for 8 mins, then add the
    garlic4 cloves
    , spices and
    oregano1 tsp
    and cook for 1 min. Crumble over the
    mince500 g
    and sizzle for 5 mins, stirring, until browned. Stir in the
    sugarpinch
    and leave for a minute, then splash in the
    vinegar1 tbsp
    and pour in the
    tomatoes400 g can
    .
  2. Simmer for 5 mins then tip in the
    beans400 g can
    and the water from the can. Season, stir and simmer everything for 20 mins until the beef is in a thick gravy. The sauce can be prepared up to 2 days ahead, chilled and reheated with a splash of water or frozen for 6 months.
  3. To make the burritos, heat the
    tortillas8
    following pack instructions. Pile some
    rice500 g
    and beef sauce along each tortilla and scatter over your choice of topping. Fold over the ends and roll up to seal. Secure by wrapping with foil if you want. Eat immediately.
