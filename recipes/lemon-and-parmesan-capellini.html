Lemon and parmesan capellini

17 mins Serves 2 Vegetarian

In this recipe, cream is thickened with parmesan and flavoured with lemon to make a superb sauce for pasta.

Ingredients

  • 50 g butter, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh herbs
  • Â½ granary baguette
  • 225 g dried or fresh capellini
  • 250 ml single cream
  • 75 g parmesan cheese, grated
  • finely grated rind of 1 lemon
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200/Gas 6. Put the butter in a bowl and beat with the garlic and herbs
  2. Cut the granary baguette into thick slices and spread them thickly with the flavoured butter.
  3. Reassemble the baguette. The garlic and herb butter will help hold in together. Wrap in foil, support on a baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, cook the pasta in a large pan of lightly salted boiling water. Dried pasta will take 10-12 minutes
    , fresh about 3 minutes
    .
  5. Pour the cream into another pan and bring to the boil. Stir in the parmesan and lemon rind. The sauce should thicken in 30 seconds
     or so.
  6. Drain the pasta, return in to the pan and toss with the sauce. Season to taste and spinkle with a little chopped fresh parsley and more grated lemon rind. Serve with the hot herb bread.
