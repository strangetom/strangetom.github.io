Honey mustard glazed pork with mash

35 mins Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 2 leeks
  • 1 savoy cabbage, shredded
  • 4 pork loin fillet
  • honey
  • wholegrain mustard
  • Tracklements apple and sage jelly

Instructions

  1. Chop the
    potatoes1 kg
    into 2cm chunks (no need to peel!). Bring a large saucepan of water to the boil with 0.5 tsp of salt for the
    potatoes1 kg
    . When boiling add the
    potatoes1 kg
    and cook until you can easily slip a knife through, 15-20 mins. Trim the root and the dark green leafy part from the leek. Halve lengthways then thinly slice widthways.
  2. Heat a saucepan over medium heat with a drizzle of oil and some butter if you have some. Add the leek and cook until soft, 6-7 mins, stirring regularly. Add the
    cabbage1
    with a splash of water. Season with salt and pepper, cover with a lid and cook until soft, 5-6 mins.
  3. While the
    cabbage1
    is cooking, heat a frying pan over high heat with a drizzle of oil. Season the
    pork4
    with salt and pepper, then lay the
    pork4
    in your hot pan and cook until golden brown, 4-5 mins each side. IMPORTANT; The
    pork4
    is cooked when it is no longer pink in the middle.
  4. Once the
    pork4
    is cooked, add the honey and mustard to the pan and turn to coat the
    pork4
    in it until sticky, 30 seconds. Remove from the heat. Once cooked, transfer the
    pork4
    to a chopping board, cover with foil and leave to rest. Remove the pan from the heat but don't wash the pan up!
  5. Drain the
    potatoes1 kg
    in a colander then return to the pan with a knob of butter and a splash of milk (if you have some). Roughly mash and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Return the frying pan you cooked the
    pork4
    in to medium heat add the apple and sage jelly with a splash of water and bring to a simmer and remove from the heat. Warm the mashed
    potatoes1 kg
    and vegetables up if you need to then serve the
    pork4
    with the mash, the greens and sauce drizzled over. Enjoy!
