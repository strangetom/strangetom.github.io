Pancetta penne all'arrabbiata

These warming and hearty Penne all'Arrabbiata with pancetta are bursting full of flavours and make the perfect dinner night option.

40 mins
2 servings

  • kcal 815
  • fat 23 g

  • carbs 114 g
  • sugar 20 g

Ingredients

  • 2 red pepper
  • 2 shallot
  • 120 g pancetta lardons
  • 2x 400 g tin finely chopped tomatoes with basil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 400 g penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 2 ciabatta
  • 150 g grated Italian style hard cheese
  • 1 bunch chives
  • 1 pinch chilli flakes
