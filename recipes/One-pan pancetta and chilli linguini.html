One-pan pancetta and chilli linguini

Throw together our one-pan pancetta and chilli linguini - the silky pasta cooks in its own sauce!

Servings: 2

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1x 80 g pack smoked cubetti pancetta
  • 1 red chilli, sliced and deseeded if you wish
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely sliced
  • 1x 300 g pack vine tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 500 ml vegetable or chicken stock
  • 200 g dried linguini
  • 0.5x 28 g pack fesh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp Parmesan, grated, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large heavy-based frying pan, about 23 cm base measurement; cook the
    pancetta1x 80 g pack
    for a few minutes until starting to turn golden. Add the
    chilli1
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    and cook for just 1 minute. Tip in the
    tomatoes1x 300 g pack
    and
    stock500 ml
    and stir together well, bringing the sauce to the boil.
  2. Add the linguini to the pan, submerging it underneath the sauce. Cover the pan and simmer gently for 2-3 minutes, then, removing the lid and using a fork, stir the
    pasta200 g
    , separating the strands so they don't stick together. Cook the
    pasta200 g
    uncovered, at a fairly active bubble, for a further 10-12 mins, stirring every now and then, until it is cooked and tender, and the sauce has reduced and coats the
    pasta200 g
    well.
  3. Scatter in the fresh basil leaves, add the
    Parmesan2 tbsp
    if using it, and serve in warmed bowls with a good grinding of black pepper.
