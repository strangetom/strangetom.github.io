Spicy sausage & bean one-pot

A saucy, warming casserole with Cumberland sausages, chilli gravy and kidney beans. It's budget-friendly and ready in less than 30 minutes too.

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, thickly sliced
  • 8 Cumberland sausages
  • 1 fat clove garlic, crushed
  • 2x 400 g cans kidney beans in chilli sauce
  • 2-3 sprigs curly parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large frying pan. Cook the
    onion1
    and
    sausages8
    over a fairly high heat for 8-10 mins, turning the
    sausages8
    often so they brown all over.
  2. Add the
    garlic1 fat clove
    to the pan with the
    kidney beans2x 400 g cans
    and their sauce. Half-fill one of the cans with water, swirl and then add this to the pan. Stir everything together and bring to the boil. Turn down to simmer and cook for 10 mins, or until the
    sausages8
    are cooked through. Season and sprinkle with the
    parsley2-3 sprigs
    .
