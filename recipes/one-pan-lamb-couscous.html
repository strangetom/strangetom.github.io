BritishOne-pan lamb & couscous

Serves 2    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Whip up a deliciously flavoursome lamb dish in one pot and within half an hour.

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 2 lamb steaks, or 4 lamb chops trimmed of excess fat
  • 1-2 tsp harissa paste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • handful dried fruit and nuts
  • 85 g couscous
  • 1x 400 g can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 100 ml hot chicken stock or lamb stock (from a cube is fine)
  • handful mint leaves, torn
