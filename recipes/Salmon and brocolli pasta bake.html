Salmon and broccoli pasta bake

1 hr 0 mins Serves 4

The perfect dish for a spoil-yourself Saturday night supper - it's easy to cook, tastes really special and only needs a simple salad to go with it.

Ingredients

  • 250 g penne
  • 300 g broccoli florets
  • 25 g butter
  • 25 g plain flour
  • 600 ml milk
  • 100 g mascarpone cheese
  • 6 sun-dried tomatoes in oil, drained and thickly sliced
  • 10 large leaves fresh basil, roughly torn
  • 4 fresh salmon fillets, halved widthways
  • 50 g mature cheddar cheese, finely grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 170°C/Gas 5. Cook the
    pasta250 g
    in salted boiling water for 6 minutes, add the
    broccoli300 g
    and boil for another 4 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, put the
    butter25 g
    ,
    flour25 g
    and
    milk600 ml
    in a pan and simmer, whisking, to make a thick smooth sauce. Remove from the heat and stir in the
    mascarpone100 g
    ,
    tomatoes6
    and
    basil10 large leaves
    .
  3. Drain the
    pasta250 g
    and
    broccoli300 g
    , mix with the sauce and season well. Put the
    salmon4
    in a single layer in an oven proof dish, spoon the
    pasta250 g
    mixture on top and scatter with the
    cheese50 g
    . Bake for 30 minutes until golden.
