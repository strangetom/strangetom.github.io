Macaroni chili

25 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian Vegan Dairy Free Nut Free

The coziness of pasta and chili, all in one recipe. Protein-packed with both a soy veggie ground and kidney beans. Batch-make this recipe to enjoy in the coming days, and it's a recipe that freezes rather well too.

Ingredients

  • 285 g dry whole wheat macaroni, gluten free if desired
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400 g diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube
  • 1 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 200 g soy veggie ground
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced
  • 310 g kidney beans, rinsed
  • 300 g fresh spinach

Instructions

  1. Cook
    macaroni285 g
    according to package directions, but decrease cooking time by 1 - 2 minutes. Once nearly al dente, drain.
  2. While noodles are boiling, heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a large pot on medium-high heat and sauté the
    onion1
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    until the onions are soft, about 3 - 5 minutes. Add splashes of water as needed to deglaze the pan.
  3. Add the
    diced tomatoes400 g
    , and rinse out the can with water (about 400 ml) adding this to the pot as well, along with the
    tomato paste2 tbsp
    ,
    bouillon cube1
    , and spices. Bring to a boil.
  4. Once bubbling away, add the
    soy ground200 g
    ,
    carrots2 medium
    ,
    bell pepper1
    , and
    kidney beans310 g
    . Cook until the
    carrots2 medium
    are soft, about 5 minutes.
  5. Lastly, add the drained noodles and
    spinach300 g
    . Cook for another minute, or until the
    spinach300 g
    wilts and the pasta is al dente. Serve with desired garnish, and enjoy!
