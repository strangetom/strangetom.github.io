Lemon garlic chicken orzo soup

Serves 6 Greek Share

Inspired by Greek “Avgolemono”, this Lemon Garlic Chicken Orzo Soup recipe is thickened with a mixture of lemon juice, pureed garlic, and egg to create a flavor-packed, citrusy, velvety soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 20 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • ⅔ cup dry orzo
  • 3 eggs
  • Juice of 1 large lemon, plus lemon slices to garnish
  • fresh chopped dill
  • crusty bread, to serve
