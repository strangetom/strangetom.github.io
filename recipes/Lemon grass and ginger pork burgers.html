Lemon grass and ginger pork burgers

Servings: 4

Total: 13 mins

Ingredients

  • 450 g minced pork
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger root
  • 1 stalk lemon grass, outer layers discarded and centre chopped finely
  • 30 ml sunflower oil
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Put the
    pork450 g
    in a bowl and stir in the
    ginger1 tbsp
    and chopped
    lemon grass1 stalk
    . Season with salt and pepper. Shape the mixture into 4 burgers and chill for about 20 minutes
  2. Heat the
    oil30 ml
    in a large, non-stick frying pan and add the burgers. Fry for 3-4 minutes on each side over a gently heat, until cooked through. Remove from the pan with a metal spatula and drain on kitchen paper, then serve with sliced tomato, lettuce and chilli sauce.
