Pan roasted chicken with spicy fried rice

25 mins Serves 3

Ingredients

  • 1 mug basmati rice cooked in 2 mugs water
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • ½ red pepper, cut into fine dice
  • 1 tsp freshly grated ginger
  • ½ fat red chilli
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 spring onions

Instructions

  1. Put the
    rice1 mug
    on to cook.
  2. Pan roast the
    chicken1
    , once cooked cut into thin slices.
  3. Add a little oil to the pan, pour in the
    eggs2
    and swish around the pan to form a very thin omelette. Cook for about 1 minute. Taken out of the pan and leave to one side. Cut into thin strips.
  4. Heat a little oil in the frying pan and add the onions,
    garlic1 clove
    ,
    peppers½
    and
    ginger1 tsp
    . Cook until the onions are soft.
  5. Add the
    chilli½
    ,
    oyster sauce2 tbsp
    ,
    soy sauce1 tbsp
    and
    water2 tbsp
    and heat for 1 minute.
  6. Stir in the cooked
    rice1 mug
    and heat through.
  7. Add the sliced
    chicken1
    and egg and mix together.
  8. Serve garnished with the
    spring onions2
    .
