Paella

Think paella's too much of a challenge? Think again, this easy recipe makes it foolproof and is full of fantastic flavour.

30 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 518
  • fat 12 g
  • saturates 0.4 g
  • carbs 75 g
  • sugar 5 g
  • fibre 5 g
  • protein 32 g
  • salt 1.29 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 leek or onion, sliced
  • 110 g chorizo, chopped
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 300 g long grain rice
  • 1 l hot fish or chicken stock
  • 200 g frozen peas
  • 400 g seafood mix

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a deep frying pan, then soften the
    leek1
    for 5 mins without browning.
  2. Add the
    chorizo110 g
    and fry until it releases its oils.
  3. Stir in the turmeric and
    rice300 g
    until coated by the oils, then pour in the
    stock1 l
    . Bring to the boil, then simmer for 15 mins, stirring occasionally.
  4. Tip in the
    peas200 g
    and cook for 5 mins, then stir in the
    seafood400 g
    to heat through for a final 2 mins cooking or until
    rice300 g
    is cooked.
  5. Check for seasoning and serve immediately with lemon wedges.
