New potato & mince curry

1 hr 0 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 315
  • fat 10 g
  • saturates 3 g
  • carbs 25 g
  • sugar 4 g
  • fibre 2 g
  • protein 32 g
  • salt 0.55 g

Ingredients

  • 450 g minced turkey
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp coarsely grated ginger
  • 1 red chilli, deseed and finely sliced
  • 2 tsp each of ground cumin and coriander
  • 1 tbsp korma curry paste
  • 500 g new potatoes, halved
  • 100 g spinach leaves
  • 150 g 0% fat Greek yogurt
  • chapattis or naan bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan and dry-fry the
    mince450 g
    . Brown it all over, stirring to beak it up. Remove from the pan to a plate. Add the
    oil1 tbsp
    and
    onion1 small
    to the pan, and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in the
    garlic3 cloves
    ,
    ginger1 tbsp
    ,
    chilli1
    , spices and
    curry paste1 tbsp
    . Stir-fry for 1 minute. Add the
    mince450 g
    ,
    potatoes500 g
    and 600 ml water, bring to the boil, cover, then simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Stir in the
    spinach100 g
    and simmer for 1 minute uncovered, until wilted. Swirl through the
    yogurt150 g
    and serve with the naan bread.
