Turkey

60 mins

4 servings

New potato & mince curry


Ingredients

  • 450 g minced turkey
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp coarsely grated ginger
  • 1 red chilli, deseed and finely sliced
  • 2 tsp each of ground cumin and coriander
  • 1 tbsp korma curry paste
  • 500 g new potatoes, halved
  • 100 g spinach leaves
  • 150 g 0% fat Greek yogurt
  • chapattis or naan bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan and dry-fry the mince. Brown it all over, stirring to beak it up. Remove from the pan to a plate. Add the oil and onion to the pan, and cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in the garlic, ginger, chilli, spices and curry paste. Stir-fry for 1 minute. Add the mince, potatoes and 600 ml water, bring to the boil, cover, then simmer for 30 minutes. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Stir in the spinach and simmer for 1 minute uncovered, until wilted. Swirl through the yogurt and serve with the naan bread.
