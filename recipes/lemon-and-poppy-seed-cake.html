Lemon and poppy seed cake

Serves 8 Share

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 225 g caster sugar
  • 120 ml double cream
  • 75 g unsalted butter, cubed, plus extra for greasing
  • 10 g poppy seeds
  • finely grated zest of 3 lemons
  • 170 g plain flour
  • 1¼ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
Glaze
  • 100 g icing sugar, sifted
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
