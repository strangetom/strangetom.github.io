Vegetable tagine

  • Veggie

  • 70 mins

  • 4 servings

Add some Moroccan flare to your mealtimes with this delicious tagine. Dried fruit is often used in this dish to offset the spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 1 tsp finely grated fresh root ginger
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp artificial sweetener
  • 250 ml water or stock
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 200 g baby courgettes, cut into 2Â½cm/1in slices
  • 250 g baby aubergines, halved lengthways
  • 200 g baby turnips, trimmed and scrubbed

Instructions

  1. Place a medium non-stick frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, turmeric, chilli flakes, tomatoes, sweetener and water or stock. Bring to the boil, reduce the heat to low, then cover and simmer for 25 minutes.
  2. Stir in the vegetables and cook over a medium heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through and tender. Season well and garnish with chopped coriander.
