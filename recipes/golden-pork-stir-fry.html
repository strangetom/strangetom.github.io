Golden pork stir-fry

Serves 4    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

Inspired by my love of the Thai dish pad krapow moo, this spicy little stir-fry is really quick to come together and great served simply on lettuce leaves. It’s also delicious with rice or noodles, and a fried egg on top makes it very nearly perfect. You can use any minced meat, but I do love pork and it allows the spicing to turn it the colour of warm summer sunshine.

Source: Persiana Everyday

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • 4 fat cloves garlic, crushed
  • 500 g minced pork
  • 3 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes, finely chopped, or 2 long red chillies, if preferred
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce (alternatively season to taste with salt)
  • 30 g fresh coriander, roughly chopped
  • black pepper, freshly ground
  • rice or lettuce leaves, to serve
