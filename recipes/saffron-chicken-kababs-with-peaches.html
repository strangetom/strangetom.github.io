IranianSaffron chicken kababs with peaches

Serves 4    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Also known as Joojeh Kabab ba Holu, an Iranian barbecue would be incomplete without the dizzying scent of saffron chicken kababs on the grill.

Source: cooking.nytimes.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1½ tsp saffron threads
  • pinch of granulated sugar
  • 1 large lemon, zested, plus 0.333 cup fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 large cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 4 tsp kosher salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 12 oz cherry tomatoes
  • 3 large ripe but firm yellow peaches, cut into 0.75 inch wedges
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted, for basting
  • Lavash bread, for serving (optional)
  • Lime wedges, for serving
  • Sumac, for serving
