Prawn pasta

15 mins Serves 4 Caribbean

Ingredients

  • 100 g soft butter, at room temperature
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 hot red chilli, deseeded and thinly sliced
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 16-20 cooken peeled king prawns
  • 320 g tagliatelle
  • juice and zest of ½ lemon

Instructions

  1. Mix the butter, garlic, chilli and salt in a bowl. Add the prawns, cover and leave in the fridge for at least an hour.
  2. Put a large pan of salted water on to boil. Add the pasta and let it cook in the boiling watr for 8-10 miuntes.
  3. For the last 4 minutes of pasta cooking time, put the prawns with their flavoured butter in a saucepan and gently heat so the butter melts and the prawns heat through.
  4. Toss the cooked tagliatelle with the melted butter and prawns and arrange on 4 plates. Squeeze over the lemon juice and sprinkle over the lemon zest. Garnish with black pepper and a sprinkling of chives.
