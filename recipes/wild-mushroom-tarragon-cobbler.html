Wild mushroom & tarragon cobbler

Serves 4 Vegetarian Share

This straightforward recipe makes a perfect comforting dinner. Serve with a simple dressed green salad.

Ingredients

  • 50 g dried porcini mushrooms
  • 50 g unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely grated
  • 800 g mixed mushrooms, chopped into roughly 2 cm chunks
  • 2 tbsps brandy
  • 150 g full-fat cream cheese
  • 1½ tsps tarragon, chopped
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 250 g self-raising flour
  • 100 g cold butter, coarsely grated
  • 100 ml whole milk
  • 1 egg, beaten
