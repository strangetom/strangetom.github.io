Prawn jambalaya

25 mins Serves 4 Gluten Free

Jambalaya originates in the Caribbean, but has a strong Spanish influence, quite like a paella. It usually is a mix of chicken, sausage, fish, vegetables and rice. We have missed out the chicken here!. Gluten-free: make sure the chorizo and stock cube are GlutenFree.

Ingredients

  • 1½ mugs rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ½ green pepper, chopped
  • 100 g diced chorizo
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 6 tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ mug water + 1 chicken stock cube
  • 350 g cooked prawns
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Cook the
    rice1½ mugs
    and leave in the pan with the lid on until needed.
  2. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a wok or large frying pan. Add the
    onion1
    ,
    garlic1 clove
    and pepper and cook until they being to soften. Add the
    chorizo100 g
    and fry until it begins to brown.
  3. Add the
    chilli powder1 tsp
    ,
    turmeric1 tsp
    ,
    tomatoes6
    ,
    water½ mug
    and stock cube and bring to the boil. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the
    prawns350 g
    and
    coriander1 tbsp
    and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the cooked
    rice1½ mugs
    and serve.
