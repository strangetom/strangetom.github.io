Seafood

25 mins

4 servings

Prawn jambalaya


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 mugs rice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, chopped
  • 100 g diced chorizo
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 6 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 mug water + 1 chicken stock cube
  • 350 g cooked prawns
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and leave in the pan with the lide on until needed.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok or large frying pan. Add the onion, garlic and pepper and cook until they being to soften. Add the chorizo and fry until it begins to brown.
  3. Add the chilli powder, tumeric, tomatoes, water and stock cube and bring to the boil. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the prawns and coriander and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the cooked rice and serve.
