Veggie shepherd's pie with sweet potato mash

The secret to this shepherd's pie’s filling is to choose big carrots so they don’t lose their texture when cooked

Servings: 4

Total: 45 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, halved and sliced
  • 2 large carrots (500 g in total), cut into sugar-cube size pieces
  • 2 tbsp thyme, chopped
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes
  • 410 g can green lentils
  • 950 g sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 25 g butter
  • 85 g vegetarian mature cheddar, grated

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a frying pan, then fry the
    onion1 large
    until golden. Add the
    carrots2 large
    and all but a sprinkling of
    thyme2 tbsp
    . Pour in the
    wine200 ml
    , 150ml water and the
    tomatoes400 g can
    , then sprinkle in the
    stock cubes2
    and simmer for 10 mins. Tip in the can of
    lentils410 g can
    , including their juice, then cover and simmer for another 10 mins until the
    carrots2 large
    still have a bit of bite and the
    lentils410 g can
    are pulpy.
  2. Meanwhile, boil the
    sweet potatoes950 g
    for 15 mins until tender, drain well, then mash with the
    butter25 g
    and season to taste.
  3. Pile the lentil mixture into a pie dish, spoon the mash on top, then sprinkle over the
    cheese85 g
    and remaining
    thyme2 tbsp
    . The pie can now be covered and chilled for 2 days, or frozen for up to a month.
  4. Heat oven to 170°C/Gas 5. Cook for 20 mins if cooking straight away, or for 40 mins from chilled, until golden and hot all the way through. Serve with broccoli.
