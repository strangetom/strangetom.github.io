Harissa and manchego omelettes

25 mins
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 85 ml olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced (250 g)
  • 12 large eggs, lightly whisked
  • 100 ml whole milk
  • 4.5 tbsp rose harissa (80 g)
  • 2 tsp nigella seeds
  • 15 g coriander, roughly chopped
  • 110 g Manchego, coarsely grated
  • 2 limes, halved, to serve
  • salt and black pepper

Instructions

  2. Put 3 tablespoons of

    onion1 large
    and cook for 15 minutes, stirring from time to time, until the
    onion1 large
    has caramelised and is a deep golden-brown. Tip into a large bowl and add the
    eggs12 large
    ,
    milk100 ml
    ,
    harissa4.5 tbsp
    ,
    nigella seeds2 tsp
    , half the
    coriander15 g
    , 0.5 teaspoon of salt and a good grind of black pepper. Whisk to combine and set aside.
  3. Wipe clean the pan you cooked the onions in, increase the heat to medium high, and add 2 teaspoons of
    oil85 ml
    . Pour in a quarter of the egg mixture, swirling it around so that the mixture is evenly spread. After 1 minute, sprinkle over a quarter of the
    Manchego110 g
    and place under the grill for 1 minute, for the cheese to melt and the
    eggs12 large
    to puff up and finish cooking. Using a spatula, ease around the edges of the omelette to slide it out of the pan and on to a plate. Keep somewhere warm while you continue with the remaining egg mix in the same way, adding more
    oil85 ml
    with each batch, to get four omelettes.
  4. Serve at once, with the remaining
    coriander15 g
    sprinkled on top and a wedge of lime alongside.
