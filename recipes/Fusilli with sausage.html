Fusilli with sausage

Getting sausages, pasta and tomato sauce ready at the right moment takes a bit of juggling, but the result is a delicious dish that every member of the family will love.

20 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 709
  • fat 39.5 g
  • saturates 13.3 g
  • carbs 72.2 g
  • sugar 10.5 g
  • fibre 4.6 g
  • protein 20.9 g
  • salt 0.77 g

Ingredients

  • 400 g spicy pork sausages
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried mixed herbs
  • 1-2 tsp chilli sauce
  • 1 large yellow pepper, deseeded and cut into strips
  • 400 g can Italian plum tomatoes
  • 250 ml vegetable stock
  • 300 g dried fusilli
  • salt ad ground black pepper
  • freshly grated Pecorino cheese, to serve

Instructions

  1. Grill the
    sausages400 g
    for 10-12 minutes until they are browned on all sides.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large pan, add the
    onion1 small
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    and cook for 3 minutes. Add the
    paprika1 tsp
    ,
    herbs1 tsp
    and
    chilli sauce1-2 tsp
    to taste, then the
    yellow pepper1 large
    . Cook for 3 minutes more, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the canned
    tomatoes400 g can
    , breaking them up with a wooden spoon, then add salt and pepper to taste and stir well. Cook over a medium heat for 10-12 minutes, adding the
    vegetable stock250 ml
    gradually. At the same time, cook the
    pasta300 g
    in a large pan of slightly salted water for 10-12 minutes.
  4. While the tomato sauce and
    pasta300 g
    are cooking, drain the cooked
    sausages400 g
    on kitchen paper, and, when cool enough to touch, cut each one diagonally into 1cm pieces.
  5. Add the sausage pieces to the sauce and mix well. Drain the
    pasta300 g
    and add it to the pan of sauce. Toss well, then divide among four warmed bowls, sprinkled with the grated Pecorino.
