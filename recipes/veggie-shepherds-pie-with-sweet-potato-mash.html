BritishVeggie shepherd's pie with sweet potato mash

Serves 4    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 1 hr

The secret to this shepherd's pie’s filling is to choose big carrots so they don’t lose their texture when cooked

Source: www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, halved and sliced
  • 2 large carrots, cut into sugar-cube size pieces (500 g in total)
  • 2 tbsp thyme, chopped
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 1x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 vegetable stock cubes
  • 1x 410 g can green lentils
  • 950 g sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 25 g butter
  • 85 g vegetarian mature cheddar, grated
