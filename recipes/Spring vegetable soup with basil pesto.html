Spring vegetable soup with basil pesto

A former magazine cover star, this satisfying soup is special enough for entertaining and easy enough to size down

10 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 594
  • fat 31 g
  • saturates 6 g
  • carbs 56 g
  • sugar 19 g
  • fibre 16 g
  • protein 27 g
  • salt 2.35 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 leeks, washed and chopped
  • 100 g green bean, cut into lengths
  • 1 large courgette, diced
  • 1.2 l hot vegetable stock
  • 3 vine-ripe tomatoes, deseeded and chopped
  • 400 g can cannellini bean
  • 1 nest vermicelli (about 35 g)
  • For the pesto
  • 25 g pack basil
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 25 g pistachio nuts
  • 25 g vegetarian Parmesan-style cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil, then fry the leek until softened. Add the green beans and
    courgette1 large
    , then pour in the stock and season to taste. Cover and simmer for 5 mins.
  2. Meanwhile, make the pesto: put the basil,
    garlic1 clove
    , nuts, Parmesan, oil and 0.5 tsp salt in a food processor, then blitz until smooth.
  3. Stir the tomatoes, cannellini beans and vermicelli into the soup pan, then simmer for 5 mins more until the veg are just tender. The soup and pesto can now be chilled for up to a day.
  4. Reheat the soup, if necessary, then stir in half the pesto. Ladle into bowls and serve with the rest of the pesto spooned on top. Eat with chunks of crusty bread.
