Sausages with parsnip and apple mash

Serves 2     |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 25 mins

Add a touch of sweetness to your bangers and mash with the addition of parsnips and a cider-laced onion gravy

Source: www.sainsburysmagazine.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 200 g floury potatoes
  • 200 g parsnips
  • 4 sausages
  • 15 g butter
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 15 g plain flour
  • 200 ml dry cider
  • 100 ml chicken stock
  • 1 small apple, cored and cut into small dice
  • 3 tbsp semi-skimmed milk
