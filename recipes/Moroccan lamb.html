Moroccan lamb

Shopping for dinner doesn't have to be a chore, this five ingredient dish will have you in and out in no time, so there's more time to relax at home

Servings: 4

Total: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 500 g lamb neck fillets, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 3 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes with olive oil and garlic
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped parsley, plus extra to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat a large, non-stick frying pan. Cook the
    lamb500 g
    well on all sides without adding extra oil. Tip in the spices, then fry for 1 min more until aromatic.
  2. Pour in the
    chopped tomatoes2x 400 g cans
    and
    parsley1 tbsp
    , bring to the boil, then simmer gently, with a lid on, for 30 mins or until the
    lamb500 g
    is tender. Serve sprinkled with more
    parsley1 tbsp
    .
