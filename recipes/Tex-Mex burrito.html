Tex-Mex burrito

20 mins Serves 2 Vegetarian

A speedy vegetarian dish - ideal as a last-minute, midweek supper

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes, halved seeds scooped out, then chopped
  • 3 spring onions, chopped
  • 1 red chilli, sliced (deseeded if you like it milder)
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 ml milk
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 100 g cheddar, grated
  • 2 large wraps
  • soured cream and guacamole, to serve (optional)

Instructions

    oil1 tsp
    in a large non-stick pan and fry the remaining spring onion and chilli for 1 min, then pour in the egg mix. Gently scramble the
    eggs4
    by dragging the egg mixture as it sets into the middle of the pan. Cook to your liking, then take off the heat and throw on the
    cheese100 g
    . Stir through, then divide between the
    wraps2 large
    . Tuck up the top and bottom of each wrap and roll up, then slice in half and serve with the homemade tomato salsa, soured cream and guacamole, if you like.
