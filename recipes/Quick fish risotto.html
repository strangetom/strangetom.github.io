Quick fish risotto

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 vegetable or fish stock cube
  • 250 g risotto rice
  • 350 g smoked cod or haddock, skinned and cut into chunks
  • large cupful frozen peas
  • knob butter
  • 1 lemon, cut into 8 wedges, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Put the onion and garlic in a large heatproof, non-metallic bowl with the stock cube and 300 ml boiling water. Stir well, then cover and microwave on High for 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in the rice with another 300 ml boiling water and microwave on High for 10 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes.
  3. Stir the fish into the rice with the peas and another 300 ml boiling water. Cover and microwave on High for 4 minutes. Check the rice is cooked and leave to stand for 1-2 minutes for the liquid to be absorbed. Stir in the butter and season well. Serve hot with lemon wedges to squeeze over.
