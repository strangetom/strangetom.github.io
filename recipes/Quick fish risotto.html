Quick fish risotto

20 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 323
  • fat 4 g
  • saturates 2 g
  • carbs 56 g
  • sugar 3 g
  • fibre 3 g
  • protein 20 g
  • salt 3.1 g

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 vegetable or fish stock cube
  • 250 g risotto rice
  • 350 g smoked cod or haddock, skinned and cut into chunks
  • large cupful frozen peas
  • knob butter
  • 1 lemon, cut into 8 wedges, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Put the
    onion1
    and
    garlic1 clove
    in a large heatproof, non-metallic bowl with the
    stock cube1
    and 300 ml boiling water. Stir well, then cover and microwave on High for 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in the
    rice250 g
    with another 300 ml boiling water and microwave on High for 10 minutes, stirring after 5 minutes.
  3. Stir the
    fish350 g
    into the
    rice250 g
    with the
    peaslarge cupful
    and another 300 ml boiling water. Cover and microwave on High for 4 minutes. Check the
    rice250 g
    is cooked and leave to stand for 1-2 minutes for the liquid to be absorbed. Stir in the
    butterknob
    and season well. Serve hot with
    lemon1
    wedges to squeeze over.
