Speedy lamb naans

Serves 4    |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

These speedy lamb naans with lamb mince are an ideal quick supper dish that uses only five ingredients - lamb mince, sun-dried tomatoes, baby spinach leaves, plain naan bread, mozzerella cheese and some store cupboard ingredients.

Source: www.simplybeefandlamb.co.uk

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 375 g lean lamb mince
  • 3 teaspoons dried mixed herbs or dried oregano
  • 25 g sun-dried tomatoes, drained and finely chopped
  • 4 plain mini naans
  • 50 g baby spinach leaves
  • 50 g mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Olive oil, to drizzle
  • Dash good balsamic vinegar, optional
