Cashew nut chicken

  • Chicken

  • 25 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

Gluten-free: make sure the stock cubes are gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • Cashew nut paste
  • 1 onion, roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 50 g cashew nuts
  • 1 tbsp garam marsala
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 mug water
  • 1/2 mug basmati rice
  • 50 g cashew nuts
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 chicken breasts, cut into bitesized pieces
  • 14 ready-to-eat apricots, roughly chopped
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 3 tbsp natural yoghurt
  • 3 tbsp freshly chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Put the ingredients for the nut paste in a food processor and whiz to form a paste. If you do not have a food processor, chop everything very finely.
  2. Cook the rice and then set to one side, with the lid on the pan until needed.
  3. Meanwhile, heat a wok and dry roast the second 50 g cashew nuts. Keep the moving in the pan so they don't burn. Remove from the wok and leave to one side until needed.
  4. Heat the oil in a wok, add the chicken breasts and fry for 1 minute.
  5. Add the nut paste, apricots, stock cube and simmer for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Mix the coriander and yoghurt together in a small bowl and serve with the chicken and the rice.
