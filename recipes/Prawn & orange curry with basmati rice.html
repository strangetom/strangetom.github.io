Prawn & orange curry with basmati rice

Servings: 3

Total: 55 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 small green chilli, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp salt + more for seasoning
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 200 ml water
  • zest and juice of 1 large orange
  • 300 g raw shelled tiger prawns
  • handful of fresh coriander, chopped
  • 225 g basmati rice

Instructions

  1. Put the
    olive oil3 tbsp
    in a pan and place on a medium heat. Add the crushed
    garlic3 cloves
    and cook for a minute.
  2. Add the chopped
    onion1 small
    and
    green chilli1 small
    and cook for a further 3 minutes, allowing the onions to soften.
  3. Stir in the
    turmeric½ tsp
    ,
    salt½ tsp
    ,
    cumin1 tsp
    and
    paprika2 tsp
    . The dish will be very dry now becase of the spices, so add the 200 ml
    water200 ml
    . Then add the orange zest and juice, stir again, and allow the whole thing to cook gently for about 12-15 minutes.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, place the
    rice225 g
    in a medium pan so the grains have room to move while boiling. Add the
    water200 ml
    and bring to the boil over a high heat, stirring after 5 miuntes. Let it boil for another 5 minutes and then stir again. The
    water200 ml
    should have almost entirely evapourated.
  5. Put the pan of the lowest heat, cover and leave to steam for 5 minutes.
  6. The curry sauce should now have reduced and thickened. Check the seasoning, then add the
    prawns300 g
    . Cook for as little as 3-4 minutes or just until they have gone pink
  7. Take off the heat and stir in the coriander. Serve the curry with the hot
    rice225 g
