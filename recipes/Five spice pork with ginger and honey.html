Five spice pork with ginger and honey

This has a real takeaway feel, but with fresh ingredients and no additives. It is, at least, half the price and also much quicker than waiting for the takeaway! Gluten-free: make sure the soy sauce is GlutenFree.

20 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 1.5 mugs basmati rice
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 3 tsp 5 spice powder
  • 4 pork steaks, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 green or red pepper, chopped
  • 6 spring onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • Sauce
  • 1 mug water
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp soy
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 2 tbsp honey
