Five spice pork with ginger and honey

  • Pork

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

This has a real takeaway feel, but with fresh ingredients and no additives. It is, at least, half the price and also much quicker than waiting for the takeaway! Gluten-free: make sure the soy sauce is gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 mugs basmati rice
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 3 tsp 5 spice powder
  • 4 pork steaks, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 green or red pepper, chopped
  • 6 spring onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • Sauce
  • 1 mug water
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp soy
  • juice of 1/2 lime
  • 2 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Put the rice on to cook in 3 mugs of boiling water. Mix the sauce ingredients together.
  2. Mix the cornflour and 5 spice powder and place on a plate. Roll the pork slices in the mixture, making sure to cover all the pork. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a wok and fry the pork for 1-2 minutes. Remove from the pan as soon as the pork is cooked through.
  4. Add the peppers to the pan and fry for 1 minute. Add the onions and ginger and fry for a further minute.
  5. Add the sauce to the pan, bring to the boil and simmer for 1 minute.
  6. Serve with the rice.
