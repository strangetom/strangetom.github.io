Shredded bbq tofu wrap

Serves 4    |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 15 mins

These colourful wraps are packed with the perfect balance of textures and flavours: the tofu gets grated, providing a unique bite. Then, it gets cooked with smoked paprika and BBQ sauce, making it the perfect addition to the crisp cabbage and sweet corn. Make sure not to skip the chipotle mayo, it takes these wraps to the next level!

Source: www.pickuplimes.com

Wraps
  • 16 oz firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ tsps smoked sweet paprika powder
  • ⅓ cups white rice, uncooked
  • ⅓ cups barbecue sauce
  • 4 large soft tortilla
  • 1½ cups fresh spinach
  • 1½ cups red cabbage, shredded
  • ½ cups canned corn
  • 1 stalk green onion
Chipotle mayo
  • ⅓ cups vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp chipotle adobo sauce
