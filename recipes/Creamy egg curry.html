Creamy egg curry

t may seem like an unusual combination but this is delicious. Why not try it and see.

30 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 226
  • fat 24 g
  • saturates 5 g
  • carbs 11 g
  • sugar 7 g
  • fibre 3 g
  • protein 20 g
  • salt 1.02 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 heaped tbsp curry paste
  • 400 g can chopped tomato
  • 8 eggs
  • 140 g frozen pea
  • 4 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • rice and mango chutney to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, then fry the
    onions2
    over a low heat for 10 mins until golden. Add the curry paste and sizzle for 2 mins, stirring. Add the tomatoes and 200ml water, season to taste, then bring to the boil. Simmer for 10 mins until you have a rich sauce.
  2. Meanwhile, boil the
    eggs8
    for 8 mins, cool in cold water, then peel and halve. 3 Stir the peas and yogurt into the curry and simmer for another 2-3 mins. Put the
    eggs8
    into the pan, spoon the curry sauce over and leave for another 2 mins to heat through. Serve with rice and mango chutney.
