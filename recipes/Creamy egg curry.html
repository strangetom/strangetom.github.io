Creamy egg curry

t may seem like an unusual combination but this is delicious. Why not try it and see.

Servings: 4

Total: 30 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 heaped tbsp curry paste
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 8 eggs
  • 140 g frozen peas
  • 4 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • rice and mango chutney, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a pan, then fry the
    onions2
    over a low heat for 10 mins until golden. Add the
    curry paste2 heaped tbsp
    and sizzle for 2 mins, stirring. Add the
    tomatoes400 g can
    and 200ml water, season to taste, then bring to the boil. Simmer for 10 mins until you have a rich sauce.
  2. Meanwhile, boil the
    eggs8
    for 8 mins, cool in cold water, then peel and halve. 3 Stir the
    peas140 g
    and
    yogurt4 tbsp
    into the curry and simmer for another 2-3 mins. Put the
    eggs8
    into the pan, spoon the curry sauce over and leave for another 2 mins to heat through. Serve with rice and mango chutney.
