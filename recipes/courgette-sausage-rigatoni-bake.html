Courgette, sausage & rigatoni bake

Serves 2    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 50 mins

This thrifty and comforting pasta bake for two is made with a punchy garlic and chilli spiked tomato sauce, then topped with mozzarella.

Source: BBC Good Food: Slow Cooker Favourites

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 good quality pork sausages
  • 2 courgettes, sliced on the diagonal then chopped into batons
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely sliced
  • pinch chilli flakes
  • 1x 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 200 g rigatoni
  • 0.5x 150 g ball mozzarella, patted dry and torn into chunks
