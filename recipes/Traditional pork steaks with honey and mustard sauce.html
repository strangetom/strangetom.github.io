Traditional pork steaks with honey and mustard sauce

30 mins
2 servings

Ingredients

  • 12-14 new potatoes
  • 4 carrots, cut into sticks
  • 2 pork steaks
  • 200 g pack green beans
  • Sauce
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1½ tbsp grated ginger
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp flour
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 dessertspoon wholegrain mustard
  • ⅓ mug water
  • salt and pepper
  • oil to fry

Instructions

  1. Put the
    water⅓ mug
    on to boil ready for the vegetables. Simmer the
    potatoes12-14
    and
    carrots4
    for 10-15 minutes or until tender. After 10 minutes, put the
    beans200 g
    into the same pan.
  2. Heat a little oil in a small saucepan and fry the
    onion1
    until they begin to brown.
  3. Add the
    ginger1½ tbsp
    ,
    cumin½ tsp
    and
    cinnamon¼ tsp
    and fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the
    flour1 tsp
    and stir well. Cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add the rest of the sauce ingredients and bring to the boil. Simmer for 2 minutes. Taste and season appropriately.
  6. While the vegetables are cooking, heat a little oil in a frying pan. Add the
    pork2
    steaks. Cook on a high heat for 2 minutes each side. Turn down the heat, put a lid on the pan and cook for a further 3-4 minutes each side. Pork steaks should not be 'bloody' but can still look quite pink inside.
  7. Drain the vegetables and serve with the steaks and the sauce.
