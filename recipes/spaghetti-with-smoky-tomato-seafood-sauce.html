Spaghetti with smoky tomato & seafood sauce

20 mins Serves 4

Seafood may sound luxurious, but the frozen packs are excellent value, and better still they come prepared and ready to use.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 red chilli, deseeded and finely sliced
  • 1Â½ tsp fennel seeds
  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2x 400 g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1x 400 g pack frozen mixed cooked seafood, defrosted
  • small bunch parsley or basil, chopped

Instructions

  1. Boil the kettle and heat the oil in a large deep frying pan. Add the garlic, chilli and fennel seeds, and sizzle for a few minutes. Pour the boiling water into a large pan and cook the pasta following the pack instructions.
  2. Add the paprika and tomatoes to the frying pan and simmer for 8-10 minutes which the pasta cooks.
  3. Drain the pasta 1 min before the end of the cooking time, reserving a cup of the water. Add the pasta to the sauce with the seafood. Simmer for 1-2 minutes, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water if it looks too thick. Toss the pasta through the sauce as it cooks. Add the herbs and black pepper, then serve.
