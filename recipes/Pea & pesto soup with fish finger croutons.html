Pea & pesto soup with fish finger croutons

  • Seafood

  • 20 mins

  • 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 500 g frozen peas
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 1 litre hot vegetable stock
  • 300 g pack fish fingers
  • 3 tbsp pesto

Instructions

  1. Tip the peas and potatoes in a large pan then pour in the stock. Bring to the boil and simmer for about 10 minutes or until the potato chunks are tender.
  2. Meanwhile, grill the fish fingers according to the instructions on the pack. Cut into bite-sized chunks and keep warm.
  3. Take on third of the peas and potatoes out of the pan and set aside. Blend the rest of the soup with a blender or food processor until smooth, then stir in the pest with the reserved vegetables. Heat through and serve in bowls with the fish finger croutons scattered over the top.
