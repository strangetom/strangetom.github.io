Herby lamb burgers with sweet potato fries

Lamb and mint is a classic flavour combination, made even better with contrasting beetroot relish.

Servings: 4

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 400 g lamb mince
  • 1 small red onion, ½ grated ½ thinly sliced
  • handful parsley, roughly chopped
  • handful mint, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp olive oil, oil
  • 2 sweet potatoes, cut into chips
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 4 bread rolls
  • couple handfuls watercress

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 200°C/Gas 6. In a large bowl, toss the sweet potato chips with oil and soy sauce. Transfer to a shallow roasting tin and season with black pepper. Bake for 30-40 mins, until crisp.
  2. Meanwhile, mix the
    lamb400 g
    , grated
    onion1 small
    and herbs in a bowl with some seasoning, then divide the mix into 4 and shape into burgers.
  3. Heat a griddle pan until hot, rub the burgers with oil, and cook for 5-6 mins on each side, or until cooked through.
  4. Fill the
    bread rolls4
    with some watercress and a burger. Serve immediately.
