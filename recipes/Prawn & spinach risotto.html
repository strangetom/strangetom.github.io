Prawn & spinach risotto

  • Seafood

  • 25 mins

  • 3 servings

  • Gluten Free

Delightfully light, but tasty dish. Really quick and simple to make. The spinach is hardly cooked at all, so keeps all its glorious goodness.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 mug risotto rice
  • 2 mugs water
  • 2 fish stock cubes
  • 450 g king prawns
  • 200 g spinach, roughly chopped
  • 0.5 mug finely grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok, add the onions and fry until they begin to soften.
  2. Add the rice and fry for 1 minute, to allow the rice to absorb the juices in the pan.
  3. Add the water and the stock, bring to the boil, and then turn down to simmer, with a lid on the pan, for 10-12 minutes, or until the rice is tender. Stir the risotto occasionally and add more water is necessary. Risotto should not be dry, but have a creamy consistency.
  4. Once the rice is tender, add the prawns and the spinach and heat through for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the parmesan and season well with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
