Prawn & spinach risotto

Delightfully light, but tasty dish. Really quick and simple to make. The spinach is hardly cooked at all, so keeps all its glorious goodness.

25 mins
3 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 mug risotto rice
  • 2 mugs water
  • 2 fish stock cubes
  • 450 g king prawns
  • 200 g spinach, roughly chopped
  • 0.5 mug finely grated parmesan

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a wok, add the
    onion1
    and fry until they begin to soften.

    rice1 mug
    and fry for 1 minute, to allow the
    rice1 mug
    to absorb the juices in the pan.
  3. Add the
    water2 mugs
    and the
    stock2
    , bring to the boil, and then turn down to simmer, with a lid on the pan, for 10-12 minutes, or until the
    rice1 mug
    is tender. Stir the risotto occasionally and add more
    water2 mugs
    is necessary. Risotto should not be dry, but have a creamy consistency.
  4. Once the
    rice1 mug
    is tender, add the
    prawns450 g
    and the
    spinach200 g
    and heat through for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the
    parmesan0.5 mug
    and season well with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
