Coconut cod with lemon sauce and broccoli mash

Servings: 4

Total: 30 mins

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 cm chunks
  • 1 small head broccoli, stalk chopped and head cut into florets
  • 2 mugs defrosted frozen peas
  • 25 g butter
  • 25 g butter
  • 4 cod fillets
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 4 tablespoons dessicated coconut
  • oil to fry
  • juice of a lemon
  • 4 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Put the
    potatoes4 medium
    and
    broccoli1 small head
    stalks into a pan of boiling
    water2 tbsp
    , bring to the boil then turn down to simmer for 5 minutes. Add the
    broccoli1 small head
    florets and
    peas2 mugs
    and simmer for a further 5 minutes. Drain and mash roughly with the
    butter25 g
    .
  2. Dip the
    cod fillets4
    in the
    beaten egg1
    and then into the coconut. Heat the oil in a frying pan and cook the cod, on a medium heat until the fish is browned on the outside and cooked through. Should take about 5 minutes, depending on the thickness of the fish.
  3. Remove the fish from the pan and add the sauce ingredients. Allow to simmer for 2 minutes. The sauce should thicken slightly.
  4. Serve with the fish and the mash.
