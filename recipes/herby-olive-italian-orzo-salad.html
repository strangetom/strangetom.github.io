ItalianHerby olive italian orzo salad

Serves 6     |     Prep 20 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

Beautiful herby Italian orzo salad filled with crunchy veggies, savory olives and delicious feta crumbles. Everything gets tossed in a light homemade Italian dressing for the perfect summer lunch or dinner that's easy to customize. Bring this fresh Italian orzo salad to your next BBQ!

Source: www.ambitiouskitchen.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
For the dressing
  • ¼ cups olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoons oregano, dried
  • ½ teaspoons salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
For the salad
  • 10 oz orzo
  • 1½ cups halved cherry or grape tomatoes
  • 2 persian cucumbers, sliced and quartered (or 1 cucumber, sliced and quartered)
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cups pitted kalamata olives, chopped or sliced
  • ½ cups pitted green olives, chopped or sliced
  • ⅓ cups pepperoncini, sliced
  • ¼ cups red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cups feta crumbles
  • ⅓ cups fresh basil, chopped
  • ¼ cups fresh parsley, chopped
