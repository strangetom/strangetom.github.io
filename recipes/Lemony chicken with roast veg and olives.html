Lemony chicken with roast veg and olives

30 mins Serves 2

This speedy spring chicken is served with roasted veggies and olives

Ingredients

  • 300 g sweet potatoes, cut into 2cm cubes
  • 200 g cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 skinless chicken breast fillets
  • 20 g plain flour
  • 150 ml chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 40 g pitted green olives, halved
  • 20 g butter, diced
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C/Gas 7. Cook the
    sweet potatoes300 g
    in boiling water for 2 minutes. Add the
    cauliflower200 g
    and cook for a further 2 minutes then drain well. Tip the
    sweet potatoes300 g
    and
    cauliflower200 g
    onto a baking tray and toss with 1 tablespoon of the
    olive oil2 tbsp
    , the
    cumin seeds1 tsp
    , salt and some freshly ground black pepper. Roast for 20 minutes until crispy.
  2. Meanwhile, bash the
    chicken2
    breasts between 2 sheets of baking paper or clingfilm until they are an even thickness of about 1 cm. Season the
    flour20 g
    well and coat the
    chicken2
    in the
    flour20 g
    , shaking off the excess.
  3. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil in a nonstick frying pan and brown the
    chicken2
    for 3 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Set aside, loosely covered with foil, while you make the sauce.
  4. Add the
    chicken stock150 ml
    ,
    lemon juice1 tbsp
    and
    olives40 g
    to the same pan, and bubble to reduce by almost half. Gradually whisk in the
    butter20 g
    until the sauce has a coating consistency. Return the
    chicken2
    to the pan for 3-4 minutes to warm through, spooning over the sauce.
  5. Serve the
    chicken2
    and sauce with the roasted veg and garnish with the
    chopped coriander1 tbsp
    .
