Roast tomato & pepper gnocchi

Serves 4     |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Tangy goat's cheese goes wonderfully with the smoky peppers. Try it with spaghetti or pasta shapes too.

Source: BBC Good Food: Low-fat Feasts

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 450 g ripe tomatoes, halved
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cut into strips
  • 2 cloves garlic, unpeeled
  • 1½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1x 500 g pack fesh gnocchi
  • 100 g goat's cheese
  • basil leaves, to garnish
  • green salad, to serve
