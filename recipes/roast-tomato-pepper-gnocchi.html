Roast tomato & pepper gnocchi

25 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

Tangy goat's cheese goes wonderfully with the smoky peppers. Try it with spaghetti or pasta shapes too.

Ingredients

  • 450 g ripe tomatoes, halved
  • 2 red peppers, deseeded and cut into strips
  • 2 cloves garlic, unpeeled
  • 1Â½ tbsp olive oil
  • 1x 500 g pack fesh gnocchi
  • 100 g goat's cheese
  • basil leaves, to garnish
  • green salad, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 220°C/Gas 7. Put the tomatoes, peppers, garlic and oil in a roasting tin. Sprinkly with salt and stir to coat. Just before the tomatoes and peppers are done, cook the gnocchi in salted boiling water for 2-3 minutes or according to the pack instructions.
  2. Remove the tomatoes, peppers and garlic from the oven. Squeeze the garlic from is skin and put in a food processor with the tomatoes, peppers and pan juices. Season. Whizz for a few seconds for a rough sauce.
  3. Drain the gnocchi and transfer to a bowl. Pour the sauce over the gnocchi and mix gently. Divide among plates, crumble over the cheese and tear over the basil leaves. Serve with a green salad.
