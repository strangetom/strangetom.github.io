No churn ice cream

10 mins Serves 6

No churn ice cream (whether it's cookies and cream, or chocolate fudge) is easy and great to make. Unless you are in love with churning. If that's the case, you probably have muscular forearms and fantastic priorities.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml whipping cream
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk (400 g)
  • 2-3 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Whip that cream until you have anger management issues.
  2. Whip in the condensed milk, then the vanilla extract, then a pinch of salt.
  3. Optional: add any additional flavourings (e.g. chocolate, oreos, ginger)
  4. Transfer to a smaller container and freeze.
