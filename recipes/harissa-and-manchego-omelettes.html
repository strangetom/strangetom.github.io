Harissa and manchego omelettes

Serves 4    |     Prep 5 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

Source: SIMPLE by Ottolenghi

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /

  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 12 large eggs, lightly whisked
  • 100 ml whole milk
  • 4½ tbsp rose harissa (80 g)
  • 2 tsp nigella seeds
  • 15 g coriander, roughly chopped
  • 110 g Manchego, coarsely grated
  • 2 limes, halved, to serve
