Penne boscaiola

  • Pork

  • 15 mins

  • 4 servings

  • Gluten Free

'Boscaiola' is Italian for 'woodcutter' or 'lumberjack'. He would typically gather the mushrooms from the woods. This is a classic Italian dish which contains mushrooms, bacon and a creamy pasta. Gluten-free: use gluten-free pasta.

Ingredients

  • 3 mugs penne pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200 g streaky bacon, cut into 2 cm strips
  • 6 spring onions, chopped
  • 250 g chestnut mushrooms, sliced
  • 300 ml single cream
  • 1/2 mug grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp freshly chopped basil

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in a pan of boiling, salted water. Drain and return to the pan until needed.
  2. Heart the oil in a large saucepan or wok. Fry the bacon until it begins to brown.
  3. Add the onions and the mushrooms and fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the cream and parmesan and season to taste. Bring to the boil.
  5. Add the pasta and the chopped basil and serve immediately.
