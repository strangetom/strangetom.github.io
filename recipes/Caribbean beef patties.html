Caribbean beef patties

Spice up beef mince with turmeric, thyme and hot pepper sauce before folding in shortcrust pastry for a tasty dinner or lunch.

60 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 780
  • fat 48 g
  • saturates 26 g
  • carbs 63 g
  • sugar 3 g
  • fibre 4 g
  • protein 23 g
  • salt 2 g

Ingredients

  • drizzle of oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 250 g beef mince
  • 1 potato, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • few sprigs thyme
  • 2 tbsp hot pepper sauce
  • 500 g block shortcrust pastry, pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • green salad, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oildrizzle
    in a pan, add the
    onion1 small
    and cook for 5 mins. Add the
    garlic2 cloves
    and
    beef250 g
    , turn up the heat and cook until the meat is browned. Add the
    potato1
    , half the
    turmeric2 tsp
    , the
    purée2 tbsp
    and
    thymefew sprigs
    , plus 200ml water. Cover and simmer for 15 mins, then remove the lid and cook for 5 mins more. Add the
    hot pepper sauce2 tbsp
    and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oven to 200°C fan. Roll out the pastry to the thickness of a £1 coin. Cut out 6 x 15 cm circles, using a small plate as a guide. Divide the mince between the circles, piling it up on one side. Mix the
    egg1
    with the remaining
    turmeric2 tsp
    , brush around the edge of each circle, then fold over and use a fork to seal the edges. Arrange on a baking tray lined with baking parchment, brush with a little more
    egg1
    and bake for 20-25 mins. Serve with a green salad.
