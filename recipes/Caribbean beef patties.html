Caribbean beef patties

  • Beef

  • 60 mins

  • 4 servings

Spice up beef mince with turmeric, thyme and hot pepper sauce before folding in shortcrust pastry for a tasty dinner or lunch.

Ingredients

  • drizzle of oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 250 g beef mince
  • 1 potato, cut into 1cm cubes
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • few thyme sprigs
  • 2 tbsp hot pepper sauce
  • 500 g block shortcrust pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • green salad, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, add the onion and cook for 5 mins. Add the garlic and beef, turn up the heat and cook until the meat is browned. Add the potato, half the turmeric, the purée and thyme, plus 200ml water. Cover and simmer for 15 mins, then remove the lid and cook for 5 mins more. Add the hot pepper sauce and leave to cool.
  2. Heat oven to 200°C fan. Roll out the pastry to the thickness of a £1 coin. Cut out 6 x 15 cm circles, using a small plate as a guide. Divide the mince between the circles, piling it up on one side. Mix the egg with the remaining turmeric, brush around the edge of each circle, then fold over and use a fork to seal the edges. Arrange on a baking tray lined with baking parchment, brush with a little more egg and bake for 20-25 mins. Serve with a green salad.
