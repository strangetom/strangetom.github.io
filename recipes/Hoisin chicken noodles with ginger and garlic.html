Hoisin chicken noodles with ginger and garlic

  • Chicken

  • 25 mins

  • 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 chicken breast, cut into thin strips
  • 3 mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 1/2 mugs boiling water
  • 1 tsp concentrated chicken stock
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1/2 340g can sweetcorn
  • 1/2 400g pack of straight to wok noodles
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a wok. Add the onions, ginger and garlic. Fry for about 30 seconds.
  2. Add the chicken to the wok and stir fry for about 1 minute.
  3. Add the mushroom and stir fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add the water, stock, soy sauce, hoisin, sweetcorn and noodles. Bring to the boil then turn down to simmer for 1 minute.
  5. Season well and serve.
