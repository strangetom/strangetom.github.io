Ciabatta pizza with tahini balsamic drizzle

Serves 2    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

This recipe is like a hybrid between pizza and bruschetta, and it's delicious. It's also incredibly simple to make. Add whichever veggies you'd like, and enjoy!

Source: www.pickuplimes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 large ciabatta loaf
  • 1 cup passata sauce
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp Italian spice mix
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 12 kalamata olives, cut into halves
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 4 button mushroom, thinly sliced
  • ½ red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup arugula
Tahini balsamic sauce
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar
