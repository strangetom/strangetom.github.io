Ciabatta pizza with tahini balsamic drizzle

30 mins Serves 2 Vegetarian Vegan

This recipe is like a hybrid between pizza and bruschetta, and it's delicious. It's also incredibly simple to make. Add whichever veggies you'd like, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 large ciabatta loaf Substitute with: large focaccia loaf / medium ready-made pizza crust
  • 1 cup passata sauce
  • ¼ cup tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast flakes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp Italian spice mix Substitute with: dried basil
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 12 kalamata olives, cut into halves Substitute with: capers / green olives
  • 10 cherry tomatoes, halved Substitute with: roma tomatoes
  • 4 button mushroom, thinly sliced Substitute with: courgette
  • ½ red bell pepper, thinly sliced Substitute with: green bell pepper / orange bell pepper
  • ¼ small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup arugula
Tahini balsamic sauce
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Slice the ciabatta open and place on a baking tray.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the passata sauce, tomato paste, garlic, nutritional yeast, and spices. Spread this sauce onto the bread.
  3. Arrange all the veggies on top. Bake on the middle rack of the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked to your liking.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare the drizzle by mixing the tahini, water, and balsamic vinegar in a small bowl.
  5. Drizzle the tahini sauce over the cooked pizza, top with arugula, slice, and enjoy!
