Easy teriyaki chicken

Try this easy, sticky Asian-style teriyaki chicken for a speedy weeknight supper - it takes just 20 minutes to make! Serve it with sticky rice and steamed greens

Servings: 4

Total: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 6 skinless and boneless chicken thighs, sliced
  • 2 large cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 thumb-sized piece ginger, grated
  • 50 g runny honey
  • 30 ml light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, to serve
  • 4 spring onions, shredded, to serve
  • sticky rice, to serve
  • steamed bok choi or spring greens, to serve

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a non-stick pan over a medium heat. Add the
    chicken6
    and fry for 7 mins, or until golden. Add the
    garlic2 large cloves
    and
    ginger1 thumb-sized piece
    and fry for 2 mins. Stir in the
    honey50 g
    ,
    soy sauce30 ml
    ,
    vinegar1 tbsp
    and 100 ml water. Bring to the boil and cook for 2 - 5 mins over a medium heat until the
    chicken6
    is sticky and coated in a thick sauce.
  2. Scatter over the
    spring onions4
    and
    sesame seeds1 tbsp
    , then serve the
    chicken6
    with the rice and steamed veg.
