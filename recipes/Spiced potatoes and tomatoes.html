Spiced potatoes and tomatoes

Substantial enough to serve solo, this dish consists of diced potatoes cooked gently in a fresh tomato sauce which is flavoured with curry leaves and green chillis.

22 mins
4 servings

  • kcal 163
  • fat 7.7 g
  • saturates 1.7 g
  • carbs 17.4 g
  • sugar 8.2 g
  • fibre 3.1 g
  • protein 7.5 g
  • salt 0.07 g

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 curry leaves
  • 0.25 tsp onion seeds
  • 1 green chilli, deseeded and chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 tsp grated fresh root ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • salt
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
  • 4 hard boiled eggs

  1. Heat the
    oil1 tbsp
    in a non-stick wok or frying pan and stir-fry the
    onions2
    ,
    curry leaves4
    ,
    onion seeds0.25 tsp
    and
    chilli1
    for about 1 minute.
  2. Add the
    tomatoes4
    and cook for about 2 minutes over a low heat, shaking the pan to prevent them from sticking
  3. Add the
    ginger1 tsp
    ,
    garlic1 clove
    ,
    chilli powder1 tsp
    ,
    ground coriander1 tsp
    and salt to taste. Continue to stir-fry for 1-2 minutes, then add the
    potatoes2
    and cover the pan. Cook over a low heat for 5-7 minutes until the
    potatoes2
    are tender.
  4. Add the
    lemon juice1 tsp
    and fresh
    coriander1 tbsp
    , and stir to mix together.
  5. Shell the
    hard boiled eggs4
    , cut into quarters and add as a garnish to the finished dish.
