Spanish prawn risotto

Gluten-free: make sure the chorizo and stock cube are gluten-free.

30 mins
4 servings

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 red pepper, cut into small chunks
  • 200 g chorizo, cut into small chunks
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 6 spring onions, sliced
  • 1 mug risotto rice
  • 2 mugs water + 1 tbsp concentrated liquid vegetable stock or 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 400 g raw prawns

Instructions

  1. Heat the
    oil2 tbsp
    in a frying pan. Add the
    peppers1
    and
    chorizo200 g
    and fry until the
    chorizo200 g
    begins to brown.
  2. Add the
    garlic2 cloves
    ,
    tomatoes4
    and
    onions6
    and fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the
    rice1 mug
    and fry until it absorbs the juices from the pan.
  4. Add the
    water2 mugs
    and stock and bring to the boil. Turn down to simmer for 15 minutes or until the
    rice1 mug
    is tender.
  5. Add the
    prawns400 g
    and cook until they turn pink. Take off the heat and season with salt and pepper.
