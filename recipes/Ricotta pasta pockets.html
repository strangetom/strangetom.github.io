Ricotta pasta pockets

30 mins Serves 4 Vegetarian

This surprisingly light pasta bake is quick and straightforward, and makes an impressive veggie supper.

Ingredients

  • 250 g pack fresh lasagne sheets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 250 g carton ricotta cheese
  • 50 g bag rocket
  • 2 x 350 g jars tomato and chargrilled vegetable sauce or similar
  • 50 g mature vegetarian cheddar cheese, grated

Instructions

  1. Put the
    lasagne sheets250 g pack
    in a large bowl, and pour over boiling water to cover. Leave to soak for 5 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 180°C/Gas 6 and lightly
    oil1 tbsp
    a shallow baking tray.
  2. Drain the lasagne in a colander. Take on sheet and put a large spoonful of
    ricotta250 g carton
    in the centre. Scatter over a few
    rocket50 g bag
    leaves, then fold the sheet in half and press the edges together to form a pocket. Continue to fill all pasta sheets, then lay them on the baking tray so they overlap slightly.
  3. Cover the pasta pockets with the
    sauce2x 350 g jars
    , sprinkle over the
    cheese50 g
    and bake for 10-12 minutes until hot and bubbly.
