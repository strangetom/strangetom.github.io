SpanishPotato salad recipe with tuna

Serves 8    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 14 mins

Bright, flavor-packed Potato Salad with tuna, green beans and tomatoes. A couple of spices add depth, and a simple dressing pulls it together. This has hints of Nicoise salad and the popular Spanish Potato Salad known as Ensaladilla Rusa.

Source: www.themediterraneandish.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 12 oz fingerling potatoes or small red potatoes
  • 10 oz green beans, trimmed
  • 6 oz small tomatoes, cut into wedges (such as campari tomatoes or small Roma tomatoes)
  • ⅓ cups pearl red onions or small shallots, quartered
  • 15 oz can quality tuna
  • 2-3 large clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (more to your liking)
  • ¾ tsps cumin (more to your liking)
  • ½ tsps crushed red pepper flakes
  • ⅓ cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsps white wine vinegar
  • 6 oz spring greens or butter lettuce
