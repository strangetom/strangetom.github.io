SpanishPotato salad recipe with tuna

Serves 8    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 14 mins

Bright, flavor-packed Potato Salad with tuna, green beans and tomatoes. A couple of spices add depth, and a simple dressing pulls it together. This has hints of Nicoise salad and the popular Spanish Potato Salad known as Ensaladilla Rusa.
