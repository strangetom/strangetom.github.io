Pasta with cherry tomato sauce

Serves 4 Vegetarian Share

A great vegetarian family recipe that you can whip up in no time at all.

Ingredients

  • 450 g cherry tomatoes
  • pinch of golden caster sugar
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tsp dried or 1 tbsp chopped fresh oregano, plus extra to serve
  • 5 tbsp vegetable stock
  • 350 g short pasta shapes such as penne
