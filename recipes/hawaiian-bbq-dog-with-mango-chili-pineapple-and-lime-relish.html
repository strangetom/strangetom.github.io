Hawaiian bbq dog with mango, chili, pineapple and lime relish.

Serves 6     |     Prep 15 mins     |     Cook 10 mins

Hot dogs are just classic summertime cookout food, and let's be honest they taste pretty good going down.

Source: www.halfbakedharvest.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
Hawaiian BBQ Sauce
  • ½ cup of your favourite BBQ sauce
  • 2 tbsp pineapple juice
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • pinch cayenne
  • pinch salt & pepper
Mango, Chili, Pineapple and Lime Relish
  • 1 mango, peeled & diced
  • 1 cup fresh pineapple, diced
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped
  • juice of a lime
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 in chipotle chili in adobo, chopped
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded & chopped
  • salt & pepper (to taste)
The Dogs
  • 4 spicy brats (or hot dogs if you prefer)
  • 4-6 Hawaiian roll hot dog buns (toasted)
  • pickled jalapeños (for serving)
  • swiss cheese (optional)
