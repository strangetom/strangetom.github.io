Vegetarian sausage cassoulet

Cassoulet is a rich and hearty dish that is traditionally slow-cooked and made with meat. In this recipe, however, we've halved the time and opted for Linda McCartney sausages for a delicious vegetarian treat that's packed with wholesome flavour.

Servings: 2

Total: 30 mins

Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 100 g chestnut mushrooms
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 6 Linda McCartney sausages
  • 1 can cannellini beans
  • 1 pot wild mushroom sauce
  • tomato puree
  • dried thyme
  • fresh flat leaf parsley
  • 25 g panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Halve, peel and chop the
    onion1
    into small pieces. Trim the
    carrot1
    , then quarter lengthways (no need to peel). Chop widthways into small pieces. Thinly slice the
    mushrooms100 g
    . Peel and grate the
    garlic2 cloves
    (or use a
    garlic2 cloves
    press).
  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large, high sided pan or casserole pot over high heat. Once hot, add the
    sausages6
    and cook until browned all over, 5-6 mins. (You may need to do this in batches). Add a little more oil if the
    sausages6
    start to stick. Transfer to a plate. Whilst the
    sausages6
    are cooking, drain and rinse the cannellIni beans.
  3. Add a drizzle of oil to the now empty pan and add the chopped
    onion1
    and
    carrot1
    . Cook, stirring, until softened and turning golden, 5-6 mins. Add the
    mushrooms100 g
    and cook for a further 3-4 mins. Add the mushroom paste, tomato puree, thyme, water (see ingredients for amounts) and half the
    garlic2 cloves
    . Season with salt and pepper. Simmer with a lid on for 10 mins.
  4. Remove the lid from the pan, return the
    sausages6
    to the cassoulet and simmer until the mixture has thickened, a further 10 mins.
  5. Meanwhile, roughly chop the parsley (stalks and all). Heat a drizzle of oil in a frying pan over medium to high heat and add the
    breadcrumbs25 g
    with the remaining
    garlic2 cloves
    and cook, stirring, until golden brown and crispy, 3-4 mins. Tip onto a plate and stir through half the parsley.
  6. Stir the remaining parsley through the cassoulet and divide it between bowls. Scatter over the crispy
    breadcrumbs25 g
    . Enjoy!
