MalaysianMalaysian red curry noodles

Serves 4     |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 20 mins

This Malaysian red curry noodle recipe is a perfect quick vegetarian dinner recipe.

Source: www.triedandtruerecipe.com

Ingredients

    /    
    /    
    /    
    /    
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
  • 1 shallot, peeled and sliced
  • ¾ cups carrots, grated
  • 1-2 tablespoons spicy Malaysian red curry paste
  • ½ cups water, or a 7-ounce can coconut cream
  • 12 ounces dry noodles such as rice, udon, soba, or ramen
  • 4 eggs
  • Microgreens, sliced, or minced cilantro, for serving
  • Black sesame seeds, for serving
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
