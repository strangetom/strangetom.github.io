Balsamic chicken with creamy mash

Serves 4    |     Prep 10 mins     |     Cook 30 mins

This balsamic sauce is rich and so yummy. Gluten-free: make sure the stock cubes are gluten-free.

Source: Nosh: Quick & Easy

Ingredients

    /

    /

Mash
  • 5 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 2 cm cubes
  • 25 g butter
  • 2 tbsp double cream
Balsamic sauce
  • 25 g butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 red onions, sliced
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary leaves, chopped
  • 6 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp runny honey
  • ½ mug water
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • sprouting broccoli
