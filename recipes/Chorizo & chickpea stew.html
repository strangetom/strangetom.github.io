Chorizo & chickpea stew

A casual storecupboard Spanish-inspired casserole that can be served in a tapas spread or simply with some toasted bread

Servings: 2

Total: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 cooking chorizo sausages, cut into thick slices
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 400 g can cherry tomatoes
  • juice 1 lemon
  • 400 g can chickpeas, drained
  • 1 handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • toasted sourdough bread, to serve

Instructions

  1. In a frying pan, fry the
    chorizo4
    ,
    onion1
    and
    garlic2 cloves
    for 5 mins or until the
    chorizo4
    is browning on the outside. Drain the excess oil, then stir in the spices.
  2. Pour in the tomatoes, lemon juice,
    chickpeas400 g can
    and seasoning. Bubble for 5 mins more to thicken slightly, then sprinkle over the
    parsley1 handful
    . Serve with toasted sourdough.
