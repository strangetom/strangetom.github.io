Pasta with chilli tomatoes & spinach

10 mins Serves 2 Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ½ tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 200 g wholemeal penne pasta
  • 400 g can chopped tomatoes
  • 100 ml red wine
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • 125 g bag young spinach leaves
  • 25 g parmesan or vegetarian alternative, grated

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and gently fry the
    onion1
    ,
    garlic2 cloves
    and
    chilli flakes½ tsp
    , stirring regularly, for 5 mins (add a little water if they begin to stick).
  2. Cook
    pasta200 g
    following pack instructions. Add the
    tomatoes400 g can
    ,
    wine100 ml
    and
    oregano½ tsp
    to the frying pan and stir to combine. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook, stirring occasionally, for 10 mins.
  3. Shake the
    spinach125 g bag
    into the pan and cook for 1-2 mins until wilted. Drain the
    pasta200 g
    and tip into the pan with the sauce.
  4. Toss to combine, sprinkly with
    cheese25 g
    and serve.
