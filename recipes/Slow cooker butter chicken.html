Slow cooker butter chicken

Not every version of butter chicken uses butter. Coconut milk gives this slow-cooker chicken its creamy richness.

Servings: 4-6

Total: 5 hr 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 170 g tomato paste
  • ¾ tsp kosher salt
  • 3 lbs skinless chicken thighs, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 tsp lime zest
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 cup coconut milk, whisked to combine liquid and solids
  • ½ cup chicken stock
  • ¼ cup cilantro leaves, for garnish (optional)
  • Cooked basmati or jasmine rice, for serving
  • Naan, for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In medium skillet, heat
    oil3 tbsp
    over medium-high heat. Add
    onions2 medium
    to skillet, and cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium, add
    garlic3 cloves
    and
    ginger3 tbsp
    , and cook another 2 minutes. Add
    garam masala1 tbsp
    ,
    tomato paste170 g
    and
    salt¾ tsp
    ; cook and stir 2 minutes.
  2. Place
    chicken pieces3 lbs
    in a slow cooker, then add
    tomato paste170 g
    mixture,
    lime zest1 tsp
    and
    juice1 tbsp
    ,
    coconut milk1 cup
    and
    chicken stock½ cup
    . Stir everything together, cover and cook on low heat setting for 4½ to 5 hours, until the chicken is cooked through. (You may let it cook up to 7 hours if necessary, but the chicken may be very soft and shred.) Garnish with
    cilantro¼ cup
    and serve with basmati or jasmine rice, and naan if you have some.
