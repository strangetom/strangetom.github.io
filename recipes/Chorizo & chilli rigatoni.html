Pork

20 mins

3 servings

Chorizo & chilli rigatoni


Ingredients

  • 200 g rigatoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200 g chorizo, chopped into chunks
  • 1 fat red chilli, deseeded and finely chopped
  • 2 sprigs of rosemary, chopped
  • 6 tomatoes, chopped into chunks
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 4 tbsp creme fraiche
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Put the pasta on to cook in a large saucepan. Once cooked, drain and return to the pan with a lid on.
  2. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok, and fry the chorizo for 2 minutes. Drain the excess oil from the pan.
  3. Add the chilli and rosemary to the frying pan. Fry for 1 minute, being careful not to let things burn. Add the rest of the ingredients, season and stir together.
  4. Add the pasta to the pan.
